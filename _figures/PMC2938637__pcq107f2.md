caption: A current model of early symbiotic signaling pathways. (A) The gene cascades
  in early symbiotic signaling (modified from ). In response to Nod factors, the signal
  generated by NFR1/NFR5 splits into two pathways; one flows into the common symbiosis
  pathway (CSP, blue line) and the other (pink line) is prerequisite for successful
  infection of rhizobia. The genes of CSP components are indicated by green letters.
  (B) The proposed roles of Ca2+signaling and CCaMK activation in infection thread
  formation and growth. The exact localization and composition of the NF receptor
  complex(es) have not yet been determined.
