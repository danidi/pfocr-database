caption: Metabolic shift from mitochondrial TCA/OXPHSO to glycolysis in As3+-induced
  CSCs. Along with the overall upregulation of glycolysis, three subsidiary glycolytic
  pathways, the pentose phosphate pathway (PPP), hexosamine biosynthesis pathway (UDP-GlcNAc)
  and serine-glycine one-carbon metabolism pathway for SAM generation are also upregulated
  in CSCs. Increase in SAM generation enhances methylation of DNA and histones. Decreasing
  mitochondrial TCA cycle in CSCs reduces metabolite α-KG, a co-factor for DNA and
  histone demethylases.
