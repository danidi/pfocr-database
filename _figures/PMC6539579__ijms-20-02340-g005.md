  increases organoid formation and proliferation. The mechanisms might be regulated
  by PCNA-associated factor (PAF)/Wnt/βcatenin signaling, Hedgehog signaling, cell
  migration-inducing and hyaluronan-binding protein (CEMIP)-dependent pathway through
  ERK1/2 or silencing the PR domain containing (PRDM)1 gene. The upper head arrows
  indicate increase while the lower ones indicate the consequences.
