caption: The Cell Wall Integrity (CWI) pathway. In response to cell wall stress, cell
  surface sensors initiate signaling though the SAPK cascade. B. Mechanism of Mpk1
  activation by genotoxic stress. A DNA damage signal drives ubiquitination and proteolysis
  of Msg5, the dual-specificity protein phosphatase that maintains Mpk1 in a low-activity
  state. This shifts the equilibrium to phosphorylated (active) Mpk1 and relies on
  basal phosphorylation of Mpk1 by the redundant MEKs, Mkk1 and Mkk2.
