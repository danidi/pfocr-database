caption: EGFR transactivation is induced by GPCRs, which work though intracellular
  kinases such as Src, PKC to phosphorylate ADAMs, which cleave EGFR ligands into
  soluble active moieties that activate EGFR. This leads to subsequent activation
  of downstream signaling pathways, including the MAPK/ERK pathway, PI3K/Akt pathway
  and JAK/STAT pathway. This ultimately leads to translocation of the signal from
  cytosol into the nucleus, triggering gene transcription and biological effects,
  such as cell proliferation, dedifferentiation and migration.
