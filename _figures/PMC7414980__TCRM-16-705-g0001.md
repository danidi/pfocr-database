caption: IL-6 signaling pathways and blockade of IL-6 signaling by monoclonal antibodies.
  (A) Classical signaling occurs via IL-6 interaction with membrane bound IL-6 and
  gp130. (B) Trans signaling requires IL-6 to associate with soluble IL-6R, thereby
  inducing homodimerization of gp130, leading to activation of the Jak/Stat pathway.
  (C) The monoclonal antibodies siltuximab and tocilizumab disrupt IL-6 signaling
  by blocking IL-6 or IL-6R, respectively.
