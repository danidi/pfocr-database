  with several distinct cellular processes, such as GIRK, adenylate cyclase (AC),
  cAMP, PKA, ERK, p38 MAPK, and Akt pathways, as well as the pathway for the de novo
  synthesis of ceramide. Stimulatory effects are represented by (→) symbols and inhibitory
  effects by (⊥) symbols.
