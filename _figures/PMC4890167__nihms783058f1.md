  activation of IRS-1 in adipocytes and myocytes. The resultant signaling cascade
  leads to GLUT4 sequestration vesicle trafficking to the plasma membrane, allowing

  interfering with this pathway. Specifically, JNK and IKKβ inactivate IRS-1 via serine
  phosphorylation, while SOCS proteins prevent IRS-1 tyrosine phosphorylation and
  promote IRS-1 ubiquitination and subsequent degradation.
