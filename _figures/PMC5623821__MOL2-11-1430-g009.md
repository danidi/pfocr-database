caption: Model of self‐sustaining cancer cells in 3D. Self‐sustaining cancer cells
  depend on autocrine loops of ligands leading to TAM‐dependent RSK activation and
  SRC‐dependent AKT pathway activation. In the case of a dominantly activated SRC‐AKT
  pathway, mTOR signaling might be overdriven. This is partially prevented by inactivation
  of the TAM ligand system. A double role is ascribed to AXL being involved in both
  pathways. Availability of TAM‐dependent RSK pathway activation is crucial for survival
  of cancer cells in case of compound treatment or malnutrition.
