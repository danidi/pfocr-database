caption: Proposed pathway of ECM regulation by BAMBI, TLR4, and TGFβ2 signaling pathways
  in the glaucomatous TM. Increased TGFβ2 leads to an increase in DAMP and ECM production
  such as fibronectin-EDA (FN-EDA), which can further activate TLR4. TLR4 activation
  by FN-EDA downregulates the expression of BAMBI. Downregulation of BAMBI produces
  uninhibited TGFβ2 and BMP signaling. Uninhibited TGFβ2 and BMP alters ECM synthesis.
  The feed-forward loop produced by TGFβ2 -TLR4 signaling leads to progressive fibrosis
  in the TM.
