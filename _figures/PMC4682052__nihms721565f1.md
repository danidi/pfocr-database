caption: Sir2 catalyzes the transfer of acetyl group from the protein to ADP-ribose
  moiety of NAD+ to form O-acetyl-ADP-ribose (OAADPr) and nicotinamide (NAM). Nicotinamide
  phosphoribosyltransferase (Nampt), the rate limiting enzyme in NAD biosynthetic
  pathway converts NAM to nicotinamide mononucleotide (NMN). NMN is then converted
  to NAD by nicotinamide mononucleotide adenylyltransferase (Nmnat). Another NAD salvage
  pathway component, Pnc1, converts NAM to nicotinic acid.
