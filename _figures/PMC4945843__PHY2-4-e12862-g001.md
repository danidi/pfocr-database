caption: Schematic of major cholesterol metabolism pathways in the liver. The first
  pathway is LDL uptake from the blood via Ldlr. The second pathway is HDL export
  to the blood via Abcg1 and Abca1. The third pathway is VLDL export to the blood
  via Fasn, Acc, and Mtp. The fourth pathway is cholesterol catabolism to bile acids
  via Lxrα and Cyp7a1. The fifth pathway is de novo synthesis of cholesterol via Srebp
  2 and Hmgcr. LDL, low‐density lipoprotein; HDL, high‐density lipoprotein; VLDL,
  very low‐density lipoprotein.
