caption: Molecular mechanism of DSS-induced colitis in murine model. NOD2-triggered
  iNOS/NO regulates miR-146a expression through transcription factors, NF-κB, PU.1,
  HSF2, and Oct-1. miR-146a thus produced targets Numb mRNA, leading to reduced expression
  of NUMB and activation of SHH signaling. Overall, NOD2/NO pathway establishes cross-talk
  with SHH pathway via miR-146a-mediated degradation of NUMB to control inflammatory
  responses.
