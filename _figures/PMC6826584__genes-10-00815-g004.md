caption: The TGF-β signaling pathway and its regulation by ubiqutination TGF-β released
  from the LTBP1 complex binds to TGF-β receptor II (TGF-βRII) and initiates transphosphorylation
  and activation of the associated TGF-β receptor I (TGF-βRI). The fully activated
  receptor transduces the signal to downstream factors belonging to the family of
  r-Smad (regulatory-Small mothers against decapentaplegic) transcription factors—Smad2
  and Smad3. Smad2/3 form a trimer complex with Smad4 (co-Smad), translocate to the
  nucleus, and initiate transcription of TGF-β target genes. For details see the text.
