caption: Signaling pathways underlying podocyte injury in DN. Hyperglycemia induced
  podocyte injury through the Rho/ROCK1 and AMPK signal pathways. Part elements of
  the signal pathway initiated autophagy that protects from podocyte injury through
  ROS or mTORC1 participation in the active process. Additionally, GPR-78 activated
  ERS to induce podocyte apoptosis based on the caspase-12 signal pathway, and the
  Wnt/β-catenin signal pathway may have similar changes in podocyte injury, which
  could induce podocyte depletion via upregulating expression of UCH-L1 in high glucose.
