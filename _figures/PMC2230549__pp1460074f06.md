caption: Changes in metabolite profiles in PA2-deficient lines (JI 1345 mutant and
  BC lines where the mutation was backcrossed to ‘Birte’) compared with those of ‘Birte’

  to mature seeds [MS]). Changes in metabolite levels were calculated as the ratio
  between the level in the PA2-deficient lines and the control (‘Birte’) and are listed
  in Supplemental Figure S1. To visualize the changes, significant increases or decreases
  are indicated in red and blue, respectively, within a metabolic scheme (P < 0.05
  using the Student's t test of Excel).
