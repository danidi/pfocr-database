caption: Schematic representation of anisotropic regulation of Ankrd2 in the diaphragm
  muscle. Potential mechanism of activation of two distinct signaling pathways and
  their involvement in the transcription of Ankrd2 gene. Longitudinal stretch of the
  diaphragm up-regulates Ankrd2 gene expression via the activation of Akt-dependent
  NF-κB signaling pathway. Despite activation of Raf-1-dependent AP-1 signaling pathway
  via the activation of Erk1/2 by either form of stretch, only the transverse stretch-activated
  Raf-1-dependent AP-1 pathway regulates Ankrd2 gene expression by GTP-Ras, which
  is activated only by transverse stretch.
