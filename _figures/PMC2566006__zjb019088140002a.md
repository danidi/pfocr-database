  (ΔmenD) grown in TSB medium at the exponential growth phase and at early stationary
  phase. (A) Carbohydrate metabolism. (B) Amino acid metabolism. (C) Fermentation.

  proteins with no changes between the menD mutant and the wild type or the hemB mutant.
