  1 (NFAT1)-MDM2 pathway in breast cancer. In the cytoplasm, calcineurin dephosphorylates
  NFAT1 and induces its nuclear translocation. NFAT1 can also be rephosphorylated
  by various kinases. In the nucleus, the dephosphorylated NFAT1 interacts with multiple
  transcriptional partners to activate the transcription of MDM2. MDM2 targets itself
  for ubiquitination and degradation.
