  < 0.01) and olive oil group (FDR < 0.01). LDLR-KO mice were fed a Western diet supplemented

  genes in the pathway. For each node, left panel: gene expression log2(Fold Change)

  vs. olive oil group (n = 4). Red and green colors of panels in each node indicated
  up- or down-regulated genes, respectively, by LCMUFA consumption. †: Upregulated
  genes of interest verified by qPCR.'
