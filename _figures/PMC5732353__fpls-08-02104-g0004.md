  dehydrogenase; ALT, alanine transaminase; ADC, arginine decarboxylase; TS, theanine

  4-coumarate coenzyme (Co)A ligase; ANR, anthocyanidin reductase; CHI, chalcone isomerase;

  flavonoid 3'',5''-hydroxylase; FLS, flavonol synthase; LAR, leucocyanidin reductase;
  ANS, leucoanthocyanidin oxidase; UFGT, UDP-glucose, flavonoid 3-O-glucosyl transferase.
  This pathway was drawn based on Shi et al. () and Li et al. (). The accumulation
  level of DAPs during three ‘Anji Baicha’ developmental stages is shown using a heatmap
  (from left to right: Stage 1, Stage 2, Stage 3).'
