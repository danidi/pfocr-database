caption: Activation of caspase-2 by FASN inhibitors is mediated by the mTOR pathway.
  Upon FASN inhibition, the mTOR inhibitory protein REDD1 is induced and stimulates
  caspase-2 activation and apoptosis in FASN-sensitive ovarian cancer cells. On the
  other hand, in FASN-resistant ovarian cancer cells, REDD1 induction is impaired,
  but mTOR inhibitor treatment can substitute function of REDD1 and restore caspase-2-mediated
  sensitization of cells to apoptosis.
