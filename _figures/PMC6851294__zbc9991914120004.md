  map of metabolites measured in total heart homogenates of WT and GlycoHi fed short-term

  genotype conditions. A, effect of diet on the metabolite profile of WT and GlycoHi
  mice. B, genotypic effect on the metabolite profile of WT and GlycoHi mice. Data
  are -fold change of means in log10 scale. Dashed arrows, indirect reactions.
