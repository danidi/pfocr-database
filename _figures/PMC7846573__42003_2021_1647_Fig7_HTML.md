caption: a FANCD2 coordinates nuclear and mitochondrial activities to prevent mitochondrial
  dysfunction and maintain the mitochondrial stress response (MSR) in check. b Upon
  mitochondrial stress, FANCD2 relocalizes to CFS and dampens the UBL5-dependent mtUPR,
  limiting transcription–replication conflicts. c In the absence of FANCD2, mitochondrial
  dysfunction activates a mitochondrial stress response involving the ATF4 pathway
  and unrestrained UBL5-dependent CFS gene transcription, leading to transcription-replication
  collisions and CFS instability.
