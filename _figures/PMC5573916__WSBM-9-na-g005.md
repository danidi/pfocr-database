caption: Overview of the major mTOR signaling network. Shown are core components of
  PI3K/AKT/mTOR pathways and the pathways that influence the mTOR signaling pathway.
  Depicted are also critical inputs regulating mTORC1 and mTORC2 including growth
  factors such as insulin, epidermal growth factor (EGF), tumor necrosis factor (TNF),
  wingless type integration site family (WNT) ligands and amino acids. Once active,
  mTORC1 regulates protein synthesis, energy metabolism, lipogenesis, and inhibits
  autophagy and lysosome biogenesis. mTORC2 promotes cytoskeletal organization, cell
  survival, and longevity. Green edges denote feedback loops in the core mTOR signaling
  pathway. For more information, see the text.
