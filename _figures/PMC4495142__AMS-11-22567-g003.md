caption: Apoptotic mechanism of aspirin action. According to the results, aspirin
  leads to inhibition of NFκB and next reduces Bcl2 protein expression. In turn, Bcl2
  protein reduction causes TRAIL and TNF-α-induced apoptosis by activation of various
  caspases, conformational change and translocation of Bax and cytochrome c release.
  Another relative molecular mechanism of aspirin is abrogation of IL-6-IL6R-STAT3
  signalling pathway that may also result in reduction of Bcl2 protein expression
