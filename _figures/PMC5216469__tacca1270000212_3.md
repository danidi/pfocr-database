caption: Regulatory feedback mechanisms govern Etv2 expression. This schematic highlights
  the Vegf/Flk1 signaling pathway that induces a p38MAPK-Creb1-Etv2 network and a
  Cn-Nfat-Etv2 network. This calcineurin (Cn)-Nuclear factor of activated T cells
  (Nfat) is further modulated by Regulator of Calcineurin 1 (Rcan1). Etv2 interacts
  with Gata2 to co-activate gene expression including Flt1. Flt1 competes with Flk1
  for Vegf and functions to repress the Vegf/Flk1 pathway and extinguish Etv2 expression.
