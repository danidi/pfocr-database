  β3 couple the AC signaling pathway with an inhibitory effect (-) that induces cAMP
  decrease (↓ cAMP). Adrenergic receptors are contributing to the invasive potential
  of ovarian cancer cells and to increased ovarian tumor recurrence in patients.
