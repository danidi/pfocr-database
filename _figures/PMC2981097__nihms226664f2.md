caption: 'Double strand DNA repair: Homologous recombination and Non-homologous end
  joining. BRCA1 and BRCA2 regulate repair of damaged DNA through HR (Figure 2) ().
  RAD51 molecules bind to the DNA and form filamentous structures. ATM (ataxia telangiectasia
  mutated) and BRCA2 accumulate with BRCA1 and RAD51 at the sites of double-strand
  DNA damage, and DNA polymerase proceeds using the sister chromatid as the template
  for repair (). In cells lacking functional BRCA1 or BRCA2, HR is deficient and DNA
  repair proceeds through NHEJ (). Repair triggered in the NHEJ pathway typically
  removes the region of damaged DNA and joins the truncated DNA fragment with intact
  DNA (). (Adapted with permission from Sehl, et al. ()).'
