caption: Schematic model of Akt and ERK activation and hypertrophy through miR-200b/c
  and FOG2 pathway in response to TGF-β in the pathogenesis of diabetic nephropathy.
  TGF-β induced by diabetic conditions up-regulates miR-200b/c which targets FOG2,
  ensuing a down-regulation of FOG2. The decrease in FOG2 increases PI3K and Akt activation,
  leading to MMC hypertrophy. In addition, increased PI3K by FOG2 down-regulation
  also activates ERK, which can play a role in fibrosis and other mechanisms related
  to diabetic nephropathy.
