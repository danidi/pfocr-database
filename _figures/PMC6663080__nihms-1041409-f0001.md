caption: The schematic diagram illustrates the murine double minute 2 (MDM2)–p53 feedback
  loop in breast cancer. MDM2 oncoprotein forms a tight complex with the p53 tumor
  suppressor and shuttles p53 out of the nucleus, inhibiting p53-mediated transcription
  and promoting p53 ubiquitination and proteasomal degradation. On the other hand,
  p53 binds to the MDM2 P2 promoter and activates MDM2 transcription. In addition,
  MDM2 also targets itself for ubiquitination and degradation.
