caption: ROS produced by gamma radiation deactivated the protein tyrosine phosphatase
  enzyme thereby activating RTK in an indirect, reversible manner. ROS also changed
  the intracellular redox potential and activated redox sensitive protein/kinase.
  At the end of this signaling pathway the redox sensitive transcription factor NF-κB
  translocated to nucleus and activated its dependent inflammatory genes. FA scavenged
  the ROS, thereby inhibiting the activation of RTK and translocation of NF-κB to
  nucleus and subsequent inflammatory gene expression.
