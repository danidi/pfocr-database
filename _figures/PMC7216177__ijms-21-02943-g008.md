caption: PACAP’s modulatory effect on the autophagy process in SOD1 G93A cells exposed
  to hypoxia. Hypoxia increased the misfolded mSOD1 proteins causing autophagy dysregulation
  and cell death. By binding to the PAC1 receptor, PACAP stimulates cAMP production
  triggering different downstream signaling cascades, including the MAPK/ERK pathway.
  PACAP-induced ERK phosphorylation regulates the autophagy hyperactivation in the
  mSOD1 G93A cells leading to their survival.
