  HepG2 cells. Calycopterin triggers ROS and NO production, which induces mitochondrial

  release of cytochrome c followed by caspase-3 activation and apoptosis. The PI3K/Akt
  pathway indicated by thick arrows promotes ROS and NO generation and is involved
  in mitochondria-mediated apoptosis via increasing Bax and decreasing Bcl-2 protein
  expression
