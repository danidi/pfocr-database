caption: The role of adiponectin in lean and obese women to modulate fetal growth.
  (1A) Adiponectin influence on placental insulin response in lean mothers. Placental
  insulin signaling pathway in lean women where high levels of adiponectin leads to
  PPARα activation and ceramide synthesis to decrease placental insulin responsiveness
  through IRS-1 inhibition limiting amino acid transport to modulate fetal growth.
  (1B) Low maternal adiponectin in maternal obesity leads to fetal overgrowth. Low
  adiponectin in obese mothers allows for improved placental insulin sensitivity and
  activation of placental amino acid transport. This supports fetal overgrowth which
  is associated with maternal obesity
