caption: Signaling network that regulates ISC activity in response to injury. a–b
  Diagrams of the signaling network regulating ISC activity in the Drosophila adult
  midgut. EGFR and Jak-Stat pathways are the major mitogenic pathways that drive ISC
  proliferation during midgut regeneration. Many signaling pathways including Hh,
  Wnt, BMP, JNK, and Hpo pathways regulate ISC proliferation by controlling the production
  of EGFR and Jak-Stat pathway ligands during midgut homeostasis and in response to
  injury. BMP and insulin pathways regulate both ISC self-renewal and proliferation.
  BL, bleomycin; PE, Pseudomonas entomophila; Ecc15, Erwinia carotovora; DSS, dextran
  sulfate sodium
