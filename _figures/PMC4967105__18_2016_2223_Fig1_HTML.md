caption: Schematic representation of the Nrf2-Keap1 pathway. Under normal conditions,
  Nrf2 is sequestered in cytoplasm by Keap1. In stressful conditions the modification
  of –SH groups in Keap1 or phosphorylation of Nrf2 facilitate the dissociation of
  Nrf2 from Keap1 as well as translocation of Nrf2 into the nucleus. After binding
  Maf proteins, Nrf2 activates antioxidant response element (ARE) and increases transcription
  of Nrf2-regulated genes (e.g., HO-1, GST, NQO-1)
