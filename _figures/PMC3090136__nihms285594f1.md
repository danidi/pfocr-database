caption: Prostanoid biosynthesis and response pathway. Arachidonic acid is metabolized
  by cyclooxygenases to the unstable endoperoxide PGH2, the common precursor for the
  prostaglandins and tromboxane. Prostaglandins F2α, D2, I2, E2 and tromboxane A2,
  generated by individual prostaglandin synthase enzymes, elicit their biological
  effects by activating G protein-coupled receptors. EP receptors mediate both neurodegenerative
  and neuroprotective effects in models of brain injury.
