caption: Model for the role of TAK1 signaling in bone metastasis. (A) TGF-β and pro-inflammatory
  cytokines can activate TAK1 in tumor cells. TAK1 is likely to interact with the
  TAB2/TAB3-TRAF complex leading to activation of NFkB and up-regulation of MMP-9,
  COX2, IL8 and PTHrP. Expression of dnTAK1 (TAK1-K63W) hinders this signaling pathway
  in tumor cells. (B) In bone environment, the TAK1 signaling pathway in tumor cells
  promotes the “vicious cycle” leading to the disproportionate activity of osteoclasts
  and bone destruction.
