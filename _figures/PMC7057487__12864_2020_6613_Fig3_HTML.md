  conceptualized figure is cited from the book of Stephen M. Stahl (2013). The 7 pathways

  (NE), dopamine (DA), endocannabinoid (EC), γ-aminobutyric acid (GABA), serotonin

  genes (PSGs) within the signaling pathway are labeled with different colors for
