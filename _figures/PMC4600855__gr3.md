caption: S-nitrosylation in TNF-JNK-AP-1 signaling. Upon TNFα binding, TNFR1 activates
  JNK-AP-1 signaling through the association of TRADD, RIPK1 and TRAFs (especially
  TRAF2) to the cytoplasmic death domain of TNFR1. TRAF2 recruits ASK1 (when released
  from Trx) an upstream MAP3K that can activate both JNKs (via the activation of MKK4
  and MKK7) and p38 MAPKs (via the activation of MKK3 and MKK6). The activation of
  JNKs leads to the activation of the transcription factor AP-1. S-nitrosylation can
  stimulate (disruption of ASK1-Trx interaction) or prevent (inhibition of ASK1 and
  JNKs kinase activities as well as AP-1 DNA-binding ability) JNK-AP-1 signaling.
