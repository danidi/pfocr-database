caption: Major signalling pathways of DNA sensors. TLR-9-MyD88, STING, and AIM2-ASC
  are the major pathways initiated by DNA sensors. TLR9 preferentially binds bacterial
  and viral DNAs to trigger NF-κB and IRF7 signaling cascades via MyD88 adaptor and
  lead to a proinflammatory cytokine response. STING is a common pathway of many DNA
  sensors to induce IFN and other proinflammatory cytokines. AIM2-ASC induces inflammasome
  assembly and activation to process IL-1beta and IL-18.
