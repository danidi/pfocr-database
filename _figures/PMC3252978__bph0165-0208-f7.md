  receptors, activates cAMP signalling in HUVEC monolayers which on the one hand inactivates
  RhoA/Rock pathway leading to inactivation of contractile machinery. On the other
  hand, cAMP signalling activates Rac1, thus mediating adherens junction stabilization.
  The combined effect of this phenomenon is barrier stabilization. AJ, adherens junctions;
  MLC: myosin light chain; MLCK, MLC kinase; MLCP, MLC phosphatase.'
