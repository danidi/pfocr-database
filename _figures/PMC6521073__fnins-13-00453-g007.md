  in the retina. DA, by interacting with the D1R/cAMP/EPAC2 () induces glutamate release

  receptors that, in its turn, activates the MAP Kinases and the PI3K/AKT signaling
  pathways (, ; ). These signaling pathways, somehow, induces the SVCT2 reversion
  necessary to induce ascorbate release in the retina (; ).
