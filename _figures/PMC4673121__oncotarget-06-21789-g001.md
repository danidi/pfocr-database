  TPL2 binds to and phosphorylates TAK1, triggering TAK1 catalytic activity and the
  activation of JNK and p38. TAK1 also activates IKK, which serves as a positive feedback
  mechanism that is required for optimal activation of the TPL2-TAK1 signaling axis.
  IL-17-stimulated ERK activation is independent of TPL2.
