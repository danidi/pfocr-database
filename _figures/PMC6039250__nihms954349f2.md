caption: 'NRF2 is negatively regulated by three E3 ubiquitin ligase complexes: the
  KEAP1-CUL3-RBX1 complex, the β-TrCP-SKP1-CUL1-RBX1 complex, and HRD1. When NRF2
  protein levels increase following exposure to reactive oxygen species (ROS), electrophiles,
  or autophagy dysregulation, NRF2 translocates to the nucleus, dimerizes with sMAF
  proteins, and together they bind the antioxidant response element (ARE) to activate
  the transcription of its target genes. Examples of the general processes regulated
  by NRF2 target genes are indicated.'
