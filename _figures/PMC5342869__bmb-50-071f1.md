  stress inhibits Lats1/2 kinase activity via Rho GTPase and the actin cytoskeleton.
  GPCR signaling can either activate or inhibit YAP/TAZ activity through the coupled
  Gα protein. Cellular junction and cell polarity modulate the Hippo pathway. Nutrient
  signaling modulates the core Hippo kinase and YAP activity through AMPK. YAP/TAZ
  activity is involved in amino-acid induced mTORC1 activation.
