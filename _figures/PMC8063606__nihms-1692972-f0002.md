  to transcribe pro-growth proteins. In the absence of STrIPAK, hippo signaling proceeds,
  allowing phosphorylated MST1/2 to activate LATS1/2–MOB1, which phosphorylates YAP1.
  14-3-3 binds to phosphorylated YAP1, leading to its degradation (image generated
