caption: 'MSC PGE2 secretion reprograms M1 via two independent pathways to M2 macrophages.
  A: MSCs secrete PGE2, which binds to the EP4 receptors on M1 macrophages and facilitates
  their transition to M2 macrophages. B: PGE2 binding to EP4 receptors activates two
  independent pathways (1) the PI3k pathway which inhibits NF-kβ simulation of pro-inflammatory
  cytokines such as TNF-α and IL12 and (2) the cAMP pathway which leads to the inhibition
  of GSK and subsequent activation of CREB signaling, which leads to the transcription
  of genes associated with the M2 macrophage phenotype.'
