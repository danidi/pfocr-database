caption: Schematic diagram of PI3K/AKT pathway. The → indicates activation or induction.
  In blood vessels, Mas promotes a variety of beneficial cardiovascular outcomes via
  the activation of the PI3K/AKT signaling pathway. Activation of the PI3K/AKT signaling
  pathway promotes the phosphorylation of GSK3β and inhibits apoptosis through increasing
  the expression of Mcl. Besides, the PI3K/AKT pathway plays an antioxidant role by
  increasing Nrf2 protein levels in the nucleus. Moreover, eNOS phosphorylation is
  regulated by PI3K/AKT to initiate NO synthesis.
