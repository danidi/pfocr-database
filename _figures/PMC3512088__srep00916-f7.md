caption: Binding of histamine to H1R activates PKCδ and translocates PKCδ from the
  cytosol to the Golgi apparatus, where PKCδ phosphorylates MEK and ERK. Phosphorylated
  ERK then activates PARP-1. Activated PARP-1 PARylates Ku86 and induces dissociation
  of Ku86 from region B1 of the promoter, which activates H1R gene transcription.
  Activated PARP-1 also PARylates histone and induces decondensation of the chromatin
  structure, enhances the binding of Ets-1 and AP-1 transcription factors to region
  A of the promoter, which activates H1R gene transcription.
