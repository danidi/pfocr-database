caption: (A) Ionizing radiation of young (early passage) human breast stromal fibroblasts
  leads to premature senescence. In senescent cells, an autocrine TGF-β loop is formed
  leading to SDC1 overexpression via the Smad pathway. (B) The highly invasive breast
  cancer cell line MDA-MB-231 enhances SDC1 expression in young breast fibroblasts
  in a paracrine manner via the action of TGF-β. Interestingly, it further potentiates
  SDC1 expression in senescent cells. This indicates a synergistic effect of ionizing
  radiation with invasive cancer cells leading to the overexpression of SDC1, the
  latter known to be a poor prognostic factor in breast cancer development.
