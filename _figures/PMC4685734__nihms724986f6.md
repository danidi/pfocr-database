caption: A. BAI1 engulfment pathway promotes ABCA1 transcription for cholesterol efflux
  at the cell membrane. B. Integrin mediated engulfment similarly relies on Dock180
  and ELMO to elicit Rac1 activation. It is unclear whether this pathway also elicits
  ABCA1 transcription. C. Stabilin-2 can cooperate with integrins to promote RBC engulfment.
  Alternatively, stabilin-2 and its homolog MEGF10 can utilize the adapter GULP to
  elicit Rac1 activation. ABCA1 is important for MEGF10 mediated engulfment but it
  is unknown how ABCA1 promotes GULP activity and binding to MEGF10.
