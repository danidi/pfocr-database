caption: A model of biochemical pathways for LTM formation in associative olfactory
  conditioning. The model is proposed on the basis of the present findings in crickets
  and some documented findings in fruitflies (see text). Multiple-trial conditioning
  activates the NO-cGMP pathway, and this in turn activates the adenylyl cyclase (AC)-cAMP-PKA-CREB
  (cAMP-responsive element-binding protein) signaling pathway via the CNG channel
  and calcium-calmodulin, resulting in protein synthesis-dependent LTM formation.
  (CAM) Calmodulin; (Arg) arginine; (Gs) receptor; (R)-coupled G-protein.
