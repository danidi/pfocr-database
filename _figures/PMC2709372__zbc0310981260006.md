caption: Regulation of cell invasion via miR-21 in HER2/neu overexpression cancer
  cells. In the normal cells without overexpression of HER2/neu or other oncogenes
  associated with Ras pathway, the level of endogenous PDCD4 restricts the cell invasiveness.
  However, in the cancer cells with HER2/neu overexpressing, HER2/neu activates Ets-1
  via the Ras/ERK1/2 pathway and consequently initiates the miR-21 transcription.
  miR-21 inhibits the translation of metastasis suppressor protein PDCD4 and/or other
  unidentified targets to promote the invasiveness of the HER2/neu-overexpressing
  cancer cells.
