caption: 'Schematic representation of plant miRNA biogenesis pathway. miRNA genes
  are transcribed to the pri-miRNA in the presence of RNA polymerase II (Pol II).
  Pol II activity is also subjected to phospho-regulation at its C-terminal domain
  (CTD). Complex of DCL1 with SE and HYL1 performs primary processing and secondary
  processing resulting in pre-miRNA and the miRNA/miRNA* duplex, respectively. HEN1
  mediates methylation and Hasty (HST) mediate nuclear export of miRNA. Argonaute
  complex (AGO1, HSP90, and SQN) loading occurs in the cytoplasm. Passenger strand
  (miRNA*) is degraded in P-bodies on the basis of complementarity; full complementarity
  (miRNA cleavage) or near complementarity (translation repression). Proteins/TFs
  involved in (1) Phospho-regulation of Pol II; CDKF;1, CYCLIN-DEPENDENT KINASE F;1;
  CDKDs, CYCLIN-DEPENDENT KINASE D; (2) MIR transcriptional regulation: NOT2, NEGATIVE
  ON TATA LESS 2; CDC5, CELL DIVISION CYCLE 5; (3) Phospho-regulation of HYL1: CPL,
  C-TERMINAL DOMAIN PHOSPHATASE-LIKE; RCF3, REGULATOR OF CBF GENE EXPRESSION 3; PP4,
  Protein Phosphatase 4 complex; MPK3, MITOGEN-ACTIVATED PROTEIN KINASE 3; SnRK2s,
  SNF1-related protein kinase subfamily 2; (4) Splicing: CBC, Cap Binding Complex;
  AtGRP7, GLYCINE-RICH RNA-BINDING PROTEIN 7; STA1, STABILIZED 1; PRL1, PROTEIN PLEIOTROPIC
  REGULATORY LOCUS 1; MAC, MOS4-associated Complex; MOS2, MODIFIER OF SNC1, 2; THO/TREX,
  suppressor of the Transcription defects of Hpr1 mutants by Overexpression/TRanscription-EXport
  complex; (5) Unknown molecular functions: DDL, DAWDLE; HST, HASTY; HSP90, HEAT SHOCK
  PROTEIN 90; EMA1, ENHANCED MIRNA ACTIVITY 1; TRN1, TRANSPORTIN 1; (6) Potential
  splicing related: PINP1, PSR1-INTERACTING PROTEIN 1; DBR1, LARIAT DEBRANCHING ENZYME
  1'
