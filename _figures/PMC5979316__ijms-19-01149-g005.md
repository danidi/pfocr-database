  the mature form (IL-37) which subsequently translocates to the nucleus upon binding
  to SMAD3. The SMAD3/IL-37 complex inhibits pro-inflammatory cytokine gene transcription
  in the nucleus and in consort with the tripartite molecule (IL-37/IL-18Rα/IL-1R8)
  inhibits downstream signal transduction pathways including NF-κB and mitogen activated
  protein kinase (MAPK) pathway. Adapted with modifications from [].
