caption: Binding of endothelins to their receptors initiates a series of intracellular
  signal transduction events via heterotrimetic G-proteins. Activation of Ednr leads
  to the activation of phospholipase Cβ, inhibition of adenyl cyclase, activation
  of plasma membrane Ca2+ channels, and activation of non-receptor tyrosine kinases.
  In melanocytes, binding of Edn1 or Edn3 to Ednrb causes the activation of the pathways
  that include protein kinase C (PKC), mitogen-activated protein kinase (MAPK) and
  Raf-1.
