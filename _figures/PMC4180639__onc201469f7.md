caption: Putative model of Ahnak-dependent activation of TGFβ signaling. Ahnak protein
  interacts with R-Smad proteins for activation of R-Smad-dependent transcriptional
  regulation and with I-Smad for inhibiting I-Smad-mediated pathway, resulting in
  the downregulation of c-Myc and cyclin D protein. Downregulation of cyclin D initiates
  a series of events leading to the inactivation of CDK4 and dephosphorylation of
  Rb protein, resulting in cell cycle arrest at the G0/G1 stage. Thus, Ahnak serves
  as novel tumor suppressor through potentiation of TGFβ signaling.
