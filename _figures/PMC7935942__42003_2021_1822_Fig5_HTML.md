  were administered at different concentrations, here ranked from higher to lower
  (concentrations in Supplementary Data ). Readouts graph show the differential abundance
  vs baseline for the B and T cell system (BT), arterial smooth muscle cells (CASM3C)

  readouts. Interactions are color-coded for positive (green), negative (red) and
  unknown (gray) modulation.
