caption: 'Mesenchymal stem cells (MSCs) home to lung in response to injury via chemokines
  and receptors (CXCL8, SDF-1, and CXCR4). On one hand, MSCs differentiate into type
  II AECs and ameliorate pulmonary fibrosis through canonical Wnt pathway (GSK-3β
  and β-catenin) and noncanonical Wnt pathway (JNK and PKC). On the other hand, MSCs
  differentiate into fibroblasts and promote pulmonary fibrosis by activating Wnt/β-catenin
  signaling. Abbreviations: AECs: alveolar epithelial cells; CXCR4: C-X-C chemokine
  receptor type 4; CXCL8: interleukin-8; SDF-1: stromal cell derived factor-1; JNK:
  c-Jun N-terminal kinase; PKC: protein kinase C; GSK: glycogen synthase kinase.'
