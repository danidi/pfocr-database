caption: The figure shows a schematic representation of major regulators RB1-mediated
  cell cycle regulation. RB1 is responsible of inactivation of E2F transcription factors
  involved the expression of proteins required for G1 to S transition. Phosphorylation
  (P) of RB1 results in release from E2F and activation of transcription. RB1 phosphorylation
  is positively regulated through induction of Cyclins and CKDs, such as RET-mediated
  increase of Cyclin D, and negatively regulated through activation of CDK inhibitory
  proteins, the CDKN2 and CDKN1 families.
