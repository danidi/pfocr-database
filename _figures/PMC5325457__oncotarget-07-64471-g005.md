caption: In the canonical NF-κB pathway, receptor activation (left of dashed line),
  the TNFα stimulates TNFR in a Ubc13 dependent manner that either leads to NF-κB
  activation or apoptosis. IL-1β stimulation of IL-1R and stimulation of TLRs lead
  to NF-κB activation in a Ubc13-dependent way. In the noncanonical NF-κB pathway
  receptor activation (right of dashed line) CD40L stimulation of CD40 involves Ubc13-dependent
  Lys63-linked ubiquitin chains and leads to p52/RelB (NF-κB) activation. TGFβ stimulation
  of TβRI/II activates JNK, p38 and TAK1 through Ubc13-dependent activity. The E3
  ligases that participate with Ubc13 are colored orange.
