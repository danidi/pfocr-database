  (eg, insulin) and energy substrate availability (eg, glucose) regulate TSC1/TSC2
  activity through Akt and AMPK, respectively to modulate mTOR function in promoting

  Akt and AMPK further regulate trafficking of hexose transporters such as Glut4 via
  the AS160/Rab pathway.
