caption: Lipid metabolic disorder impairs the basal autophagy, leading to activation
  of ER stress-JNK-mitochondrial pathway. In the presence of GCM, the impaired autophagy
  is reactivated and subsequently the activated ER stress and its downstream events
  are inhibited. In addition, GSK-3 is activated by lipid metabolic disorder, which
  also contributes to activation of JNK-mitochondrial pathway. These events are suppressed
  by GCM. Together, GCM protects against hepatocyte lipoapoptosis via activation of
  autophagy and inhibition of ER stress/GSK-3-JNK-mitochondial cascade.
