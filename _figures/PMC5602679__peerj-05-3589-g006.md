caption: During severe dengue infection (SDI), levels of soluble VEGFR2, TGF-β, IL-7,
  and RANTES are suppressed, whereas levels of IL-8, IL-18, and IL-10 increase significantly.
  VEGF-VEGFR2 signaling, IL-8, and TNF-α increase endothelial permeability by disrupting
  tight junctions and adherens junctions between endothelial cells. Changes in levels
  of IL-18, IL-7, TGF-β, RANTES, and IL-10 are related to prolonged inflammation during
  SDI.
