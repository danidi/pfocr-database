caption: The cRGD-induced apoptosis hypothetical pathway. The tripeptide RGD is a
  common domain in ECM ligands (such as fibronectin, fibrinogen, and vitronectin)
  for integrin binding. cRGD is a synthetic tripeptide with a similar structure with
  RGD in the integrin binding ligands. This is a proposed signaling pathway by which
  interaction of cRGD with integrin α5β3 in the plasma membrane leads to cancer cell
  apoptosis via caspase-9 activation.
