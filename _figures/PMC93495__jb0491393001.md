  is the branching point intermediate. The conversion of α-aminoadipate into α-aminoadipic
  semialdehyde is catalyzed by α-aminoadipate reductase encoded by lys2. Note that
  the disruption of the lys2 gene (indicated by the bold X on one pathway) directs
  the α-aminoadipate pool toward penicillin biosynthesis (thick arrows). Acetyl-CoA,
  acetyl coenzyme A.
