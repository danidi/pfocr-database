caption: Effect of Aβ on CypD-involved mPTP formation. Aβ-cyclophilin D interaction
  mediates impairments in axonal mitochondrial transport due to an increase in the
  opening of CypD-mediated mitochondrial permeability transition pore (mPTP). This
  leads to the disruption of Ca2+ balance and increases the production/accumulation
  of reactive oxygen species (ROS). Elevation of Ca2+ and oxidative stress activates
  the downstream p38 MAP kinase signaling pathway, thus contributing to mitochondrial
  dysfunction.
