caption: Gαi coupled receptors increase or decrease NMDAR function depending on the
  signalling pathways recruited. The activation of dopamine D4 receptor depresses
  NMDAR mediated currents by transactivating PDGF receptors. The resulting increased
  PLC activity hydrolyzes PIP2 leading to the generation of IP3 and DAG. The release
  of Ca2+ from internal stores following IP3 receptor stimulation activates CaM, which
  then promotes calcium dependent inactivation of NMDARs. In contrast, activation
  of group II mGluRs enhance NMDAR mediated currents via PKA/Csk/Src dependent pathway.
