caption: PI3K/AKT signaling pathway. Binding of the ligand to membrane receptor tyrosine
  kinases activates PI3K, which phosphorylates PIP2 to produce PIP3. PIP3 recruits
  PDK1 to the plasma membrane. PDK1 phosphorylates and activates AKT, which regulates
  various cellular processes. The lipid phosphate activity of cytoplasmic PTEN dephosphorylates
  PIP3, thereby decreasing PIP3 levels and increasing levels of PIP2, resulting in
  a concomitant decrease in AKT activity. PI3K, phosphoinositide 3-kinase; AKT, protein
  kinase B; PIP2 [PI(,)P], phosphatidylinositol 4,5-bisphosphate; PIP3 [PI(,,)P],
  phosphatidylinositol (,,)-trisphosphate; PDK1, phosphoinositide-dependent kinase
  1; PTEN, phosphatase and tensin homolog.
