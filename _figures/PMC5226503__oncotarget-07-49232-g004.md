  the Carbon Metabolism and the Cancer Cellular Pathway genes are greatly altered
  in HCC. (A, B) whole co-regulation views of network show the interaction of genes

  in non-tumor (A) and HCC tissues (B). (C) detailed mapping shows the carbon metabolism
  genes with enhanced co-regulation of the cancer cellular pathway genes. (D, E) indicate
  gene RPE (green node) co-regulation network with cancer cellular pathway genes (red
  nodes) in non-tumor tissue (D) and HCC (E).
