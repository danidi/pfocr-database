  pathway previously characterized (, ), which shows stimulation by 1E3 at all concentrations.
  Right: PI3K/Akt/NF-κB signaling pathway (, ), which shows a biphasic response to
  1E3. NF-κB has been closely associated with atrophy (, ) while Akt signaling is
  associated with resisting muscle atrophy (). The p21 small G-protein CDC42 has been
  shown to be important to myofiber diameter and increases upon atrophy () but was
  not included in the studies here.'
