caption: Panx3 signaling pathway in keratinocyte differentiation. Schematic representation
  of Panx3 signaling pathway in keratinocyte differentiation. 1) ATP releasing Panx3
  channel activates Akt signaling in autocrine or paracrine manner. 2) Panx3 ER Ca2+
  channel opened by Akt signaling promotes calmodulin (CaM)-NFAT signaling by raising
  up intracellular Ca2+ levels. 3) Dephosphorylated NFATc1 (active form NFATc1) enters
  the nucleus and binds to the promoter regions of Epfn. Epfn then promotes Nocth1
  expression.
