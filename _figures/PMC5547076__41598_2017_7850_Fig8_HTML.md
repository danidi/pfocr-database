caption: Schemas depicting the proposed MUC1-C-induced regulation of EZH2 expression
  and function in epigenetic repression. (A) MUC1-C drives EZH2 expression by inducing
  (i) the pRB→E2F pathway and in turn E2F-mediated activation of the EZH2 promoter,
  and (ii) NF-κB p65 occupancy of the EZH2 intron 1 and enhancing EZH2 transcription.

  enhances EZH2-mediated H3K27 trimethylation with repression of gene expression.
