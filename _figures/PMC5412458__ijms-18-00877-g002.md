caption: Ubiquitylating enzymes and deubiquitylating enzymes (DUBs) govern the outcome
  of TGF-β signaling. (A) USP15 deubiquitylates both TGF-β receptor and SMURF2 (SMAD
  ubiquitination regulatory factor 2) to enhance the signaling; (B) TRAF4 can either
  ubiquitylate itself or SMURF2 forming different polyubiquitin chains, and it has
  been shown to recruit USP15 (Ubiquitin-specific protease 15) to the receptor; (C)
  SMAD6 recruits A20 to the receptor which deubiquitylates TRAF6 and attenuates the
  signaling. Arrows indicate addition and dotted lines indicate removal.
