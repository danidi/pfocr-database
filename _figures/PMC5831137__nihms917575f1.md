caption: A. Following Wnt ligand binds to co-receptors LRP5/6 and Frizzled, the Wnt/β-catenin
  pathway is activated and β-catenin is freed from the APC/Axin destruction complex
  and translocates into the nucleus where it activates Wnt-target gene transcription.
  B. When Wnt ligand is absent or blocked by a Wnt/β-catenin pathway inhibitor like
  DKK1, β-catenin is phosphorylated by the APC/Axin destruction complex, ubiquitinated,
  and degraded. This leads to a repression in Wnt target gene transcription.
