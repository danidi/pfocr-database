caption: Interaction of HBV and TLR2/4. TLR2/4 activation in hepatocytes inhibits
  HBV replication in an IFN-independent manner but requires the participation of intracellular
  signaling pathways like MAPK pathway. TLR4 stimulation in KCs leads to the production
  of IFN-β and an unknown antiviral effector which inhibits HBV replication in hepatocytes.
  HBV downregulates TLR2/4 expression during chronic HBV infection. HBsAg and HBeAg
  are able to block TLR2 signaling at different steps, preventing the production of
  pro-inflammatory cytokines
