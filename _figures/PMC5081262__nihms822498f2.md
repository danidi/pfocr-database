caption: 'Major metabolic pathways in fatty acid metabolism: A) de novo lipogenesis
  and B) Polyunsaturated fatty acid metabolism. De novo lipogenesis converts excess
  acetyl CoA, from carbohydrate metabolism, into fatty acids that are then esterified
  to storage triglycerides. This metabolic pathway involves synthase, elongase and
  desaturase. Polyunsaturated fatty acid (PUFA) metabolism converts C18PUFA (AA and
  ALA) into long chain PUFA by desaturation and elongation. n-3 long chain PUFA undergo
  multiple steps including beta-oxidation to generate DHA.AA: arachidonic acid; ALA:
  α-linolenic acid; DGLA: Dihomo-γ-linolenic acid; DHA: docosahexaenoic acid; EPA:
  eicosapentaenoic acid; GLA: γ-linolenic acid; LA: linoleic acid; n-3 PUFA: n-3 polyunsaturated
  fatty acid; n-6 PUFA: n-6 polyunsaturated fatty acid.'
