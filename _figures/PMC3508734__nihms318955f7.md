caption: S1P binds to S1PR1 and/or SIPR3 and activates Gαi. Gαi couples to PLC, thereby
  increasing intracellular IP3 and subsequently cytosolic Ca2+. Intracellular Ca2+
  accumulation activates CamKII, which in turn mediates the phosphorylation of ERK1/2.
  Acute activation of the S1P signaling pathway culminates in an induction of StAR
  gene expression and an increase in cortisol secretion. S1P also stimulates PKA-mediated
  phosphorylation of HSL at Ser563, which increases cholesterol ester hydrolysis and
  substrate availability for cortisol production.
