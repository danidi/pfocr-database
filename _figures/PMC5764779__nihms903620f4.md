  A) Ingenuity pathway analyses integrate at least one high impact and/or moderate
  impact Cancer Census and COSMIC mutation from each of the liver metastasis into

  are mutated in breast cancer liver metastasis, whereas CEP89 is mutated in liver
  metastasis of spindle cell carcinoma, melanoma, and colon cancer. B) Four out of
  five liver metastases showed deregulated MIF-Glucocorticoid pathway. The commonly
  mutated cPLA2 gene is indicated in pink.
