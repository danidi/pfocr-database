caption: nAChR-mediated mitogen-activated protein kinase (MAPK) activation in squamous
  cell carcinoma and adenocarcinoma is mediated by the scaffolding protein β-arrestin,
  which binds to and recruits Src to the receptors and stimulates the Raf/MAPK pathway.
  MAPK activation functions with the PI3K/Akt and the EGFR/Raf/MAPK signaling pathways,
  both activated by Ca2+ influx through nAChRs, to induce phosphorylation and activation
  of transcription factors, further regulate downstream gene transcription and protein
  expression, provoke cell proliferation, anti-apoptosis, tumor invasion, and therapy
  resistance.
