caption: OAS-RNase L pathway. (Left) Interferon induction and signaling. Viral dsRNA
  is produced during virus replication (1) and sensed by PRRs, such as MDA5 (2), initiating

  (3). Autocrine and paracrine IFN signaling through the interferon receptor (IFNAR1)
  (4) stimulates the expression of ISGs (5 and 6). (Right) RNase L activation. (7)
  OASs sense viral dsRNA and synthesize 2-5A. (8) 2-5A binds to RNase L, inducing
  its dimerization and subsequent activation. (9) RNase L degrades RNA.
