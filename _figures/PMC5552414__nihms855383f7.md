caption: BRAFV600E contributes to Abl and Arg activation by recruiting Abl/Arg to
  the signaling complex where ERK (and also potentially BRAF) phosphorylate Abl/Arg.
  This prevents nuclear targeting of Abl and promotes phosphorylation of Abl and Arg
  by SFKs. Once activated, Abl/Arg are required for BRAFV600E induction of the EMT
  transcription factor switch, invasion, and proliferation, and potentiate BRAFV600Esignaling,
  potentially by increasing BRAF expression. Finally, Abl/Arg cooperate with a parallel,
  compensatory signaling pathway (PTEN loss/Akt activation) to promote melanoma growth
  and survival.
