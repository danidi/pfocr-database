caption: A schematic diagram of the JNK signaling pathway promoting cell death in
  ovarian cancer. a Endoplasmic reticulum stress (ER), reactive oxygen species (ROS),
  and the Akt signaling pathway can regulate IER1 alpha, UPR and A SK1, thereby activating
  MKKK4/7 to regulate JNK signaling pathway activity, leading to autophagic cell death,
  mitochondrial pathway-mediated cell death and AP-1-induced apoptosis. Death-related
  antibodies mediate cell death. In addition, the JNK signaling pathway can regulate
  the expression of P27/P21 and cause cell cycle arrest. b SiRNA and the JNK related
  inhibitor WBZ_4, inhibit JNK1 expression and cell proliferation. c The JNK inhibitor
  SP600458 affects the JNK signaling pathway and destroys MMP, thereby enhancing the
  expression of PARP1 and Caspase-3 and, therefore, promoting cell death
