caption: The Fanconi anemia pathway of DNA repair. a Repair of ICL in replicative
  phase starts with convergence of two replication forks surrounding the ICL site.
  b FANCM/FAAP24 complex recognizes the X shaped DNA structure and recruits other
  members of the FA core complex and FAAPs. Monoubiquitination of the ID2 complex
  represents the step of activation of the FA pathway. c ID2 recruits FAN1 and structure
  specific nucleases for incision surrounding the ICL sites to unhook the damage.
  d Translesion synthesis polymerases REV1/pol ζ bypass the unhooked ICL damage. The
  unhooked ICL remnant will be subsequently repaired by NER. e Double strand break
  intermediates can be repaired via four sub-pathways of DNA DSB repair depending
  on the result of end resection
