caption: Models for Golgi fragmentation induced by truncated APC. Truncated APC proteins
  interact with Asef and activate Asef downstream signaling, resulting in activation
  of RhoA-ROCK-Myosin light chain II pathway and resultant Golgi fragmentation. In
  WT APC cells, the armadillo repeats interact with the APC-2,3 region, competing
  off its interaction with Asef and inactivating downstream signaling pathway, thus
  maintaining an intact Golgi structure.
