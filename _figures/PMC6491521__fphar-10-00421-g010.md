  endothelial cells under HG conditions. HG decreases expression of SIRT1 in endothelial
  cells and induces endothelial dysfunction, which impairs angiogenic function of
  endothelial cells. Under HG conditions co-treatment with RES improves endothelial
  cells survival and function, which relies on RES as agonist of SIRT1, stimulates
  c-Myc expression through promoting FOXO1 degradation.
