caption: The complex roles of Wnts in cartilage homeostasis and disease. a | Both
  overexpression and loss of β-catenin in the articular cartilage lead to joint damage.
  Overexpression β-catenin results in chondrocytes hypertrophy and loss of matrix
  quality, whereas loss of β-catenin function results in tissue damage through chondrocyte
  death. b | Canonical and noncanonical Wnt signalling pathways keep each other in
  check through reciprocal inhibition. The canonical pathway appears to stimulate
  proliferation. The noncanonical pathway stimulates dedifferentiation.
