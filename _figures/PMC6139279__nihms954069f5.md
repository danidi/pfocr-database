caption: Schematic showing the role of Nox4 in relaying a local ER stress signaling
  pathway downstream of RhoA. Nox4-derived H2O2 oxidizes sulfhydryls of the sarcoendoplasmic
  reticulum calcium ATPase (SERCA), leading to an increase in cytosolic calcium, activation
  of the calcium sensitive ER guanyl exchange factor RasGRF, and GTP loading of Ras
  on-site on the ER surface. ER-localized Ras leads to homeostatic correction through
  the UPR, ERK activation, and autophagy.
