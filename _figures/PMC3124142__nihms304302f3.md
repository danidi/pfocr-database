caption: A schematic diagram illustrates the signaling pathway for IKKβ-mediated Htt
  proteolysis. DNA damage or cytokines activate IKKβ, which can phosphorylate Bcl-xL
  and reduce its level. Reduction of Bcl-xL levels triggers the activation of caspases,
  which cleave Htt. Inhibition of IKKβ or elevation IKKα and Bcl-xL prevent Htt proteolysis.
  N-terminal fragments of mutant HDx1 could further stimulate IKKβ by direct binding
  to the IKK complex (). These interactions could form a persistent cycle of IKK activation
  and Htt cleavage.
