caption: BMP2/4 increase neurogenesis from adult hippocampal NSPCs and synergize with
  WNT3A. Schematic of our proposed model summarizing the main results. BMP2/4 induce
  neurogenesis through the activation of the P-SMAD canonical pathway downstream of
  the BMPR1A type 1 receptor. The pro-neurogenic effect of BMP signalling is partly
  dependent on endogenous WNT signalling, and the mechanism relies on the up-regulation
  of the Lef1 gene, a direct SMAD target. WNT-independent effects of the BMPs are
  not excluded (dashed line, red). BMP2/4 also decrease the number of oligodendrocytes.
