caption: 'VANTED integrated pathway map of quantitative regulation of metabolites
  and enzymes of primary metabolism under nitrogen limitation in Chlamydomonas reinhardtii.
  Reduced total nitrogen availability at half the normal starting nitrogen conditions
  (50% N; 200 mg/L NH4Cl) are compared respectively to normal nitrogen conditions
  (100% N; 400 mg/L NH4Cl), given for 10 h (left panel) and 18 h (right panel) after
  inoculation. VANTED visualizations for all other time points and nitrogen conditions
  are given as supplemental material. Red: Up-regulated; Blue: Down-regulated metabolites
  (circles) or proteins (squares) in comparison to 100% standard nitrogen (400 mg/L
  NH4Cl) at p < 0.05. White: Metabolites or proteins were not found differentially
  regulated.'
