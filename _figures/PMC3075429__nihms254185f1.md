  activation of the PKA → CREB pathway to increase transcription of cAMP-regulated
  genes, while A1R and A3R activation decrease cAMP levels. In addition to mediating
  gene expression via cAMP, adenosine receptor activation can also influence gene
  expression via the PI3K → Akt/PBK or PLC → MAPK pathways, among others not shown
  here. CREB, cAMP response element binding protein; MAPK, mitogen-activated protein
  kinase; PI3K, phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase; PLC, phospholipase C; PK, protein kinase.
