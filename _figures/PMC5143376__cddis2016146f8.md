  the JAK–STAT signaling pathway and regulates the levels of p-Tyk2, p-JAK1, p-STAT1,

  signaling pathway and upregulate TMEM2 expression, leading to enhanced inhibitory
  effects of IFN on HBV production. Furthermore, TMEM2 increases the translocation
  of IRF9 into nuclei, thus enhancing the expression of a luciferase reporter gene
  under the control of ISRE motifs
