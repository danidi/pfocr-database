caption: 'There are 4 estrogen and estrogen receptor signaling pathways. Pathway 1:
  The nuclear-initiated estrogen signaling mediated through classical ERs leads to
  the transcriptional changes in estrogen-responsive genes with or without EREs. Pathway
  2: The membrane initiated estrogen signaling leads to diverse cytoplasmic effects,
  including the regulation of membrane-based ion channels, regulation of second messenger
  systems, and modification of transcription factors or other membrane receptors.
  Pathway 3: Estrogen can also exert antioxidant effects in an ER-independent manner.
  Pathway 4: Ligand-independent genomic actions. Growth factors (GF) activate protein-kinase
  cascades, leading to phosphorylation (P) and activation of nuclear ERs at EREs.'
