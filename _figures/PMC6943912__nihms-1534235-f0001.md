caption: The pathways highlighted (red and green) in the Kegg diagrams demonstrate
  significant differential expression, compared to control, for a variety of relevant
  genes following mNPH, radiation or both. The red boxes highlight the genes discussed
  within the paper, while the green boxes highlight the positive (immune stimulatory,
  anti-tumor) outcomes of these genetic changes. The diagrams illustrate how mNPH
  and mNPH + 8 Gy affect each pathway and the possible outcomes or effects of these
  changes with respect to apoptosis and immune response. The pathways illustrated
  are (a) the cytokine–cytokine receptor pathway and (b) the toll-like receptor signaling
  pathway. The comparison also visually demonstrates how the addition of radiation
  enhances or alters gene expression, compared to low dose hyperthermia alone.
