caption: CBD blocks the IL-1β/IL-1RI/β-catenin pathway and induces an epithelial phenotype.
  A hypothetical model of CBD blockage of the IL-1β-activated signaling pathway that
  leads into a malignant phenotype is represented by the black pathway. CBD binding
  to its receptor CB1 induces AKT activation and blocks the translocation of β-catenin
  into the nucleus. CBD also decreases the expression of malignant markers such as
  ∆NP63α isoform, BIRC3, and ID1 (blue pathway). CBD increased epithelial marker E-cadherin
  and its associated β-catenin in the adherens junction resulting in cells with an
  epithelial phenotype.
