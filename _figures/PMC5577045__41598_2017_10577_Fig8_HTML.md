caption: Signaling pathways altered by XWL-1-48. XWL-1-48 is a novel orally topoisomerase
  II inhibitor and exerts potent in vitro and in vivo antitumor activity against HCC.
  Treatment of XWL-1-48 significantly inhibited topoisomerase II activity, activated
  ATM/Chk2/Cdc25A and ATM/p53/p21 pathways, triggered DNA damage response, arrested
  cell cycle at S phase, and induced mitochondria- and death receptor-mediated apoptosis.
  Meanwhile, XWL-1-48 strongly blocked PI3K/Akt/Mdm2 pathway, enhanced degradation
  of Mdm2, and suppressed HCC cell survival.
