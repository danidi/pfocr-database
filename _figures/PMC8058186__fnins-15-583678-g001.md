  excitatory projection neurons. Blue arrows correspond to GABAergic inhibitory projection
  neurons. D1, D2: striatal receptors. SNc, subtantia nigra pars compacta; SNr, subtantia
  nigra pars reticulata; STN, subthalamic nucleus; GPi, globus pallidus interna; GPe,
  globus pallidus externa; BG, basal ganglia. The direct (STR/GPi/TH), indirect (STR/GPe/STN/GPi/TH),
  and hyperdirect (cortex/STN) pathways are represented. Thick lines represent increased
  excitation/inhibition. Thin lines represent decreased excitation/inhibition. Dotted
  lines represent DA neuronal loss in the SNc.'
