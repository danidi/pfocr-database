caption: Regulation of apoptosis by several pathways. When pro-apoptotic members of
  the Bcl-2 family, such as Bax and Bak, are activated by some pathway, mitochondria
  release cytochrome c. Cytochrome c in turn associates with Apaf-1 and then caspase
  to trigger apoptossis. Anti-apoptotic members, such as Bcl-2 and Bcl-xL, will keep
  cytochrome c in the mitochondria inhibiting the apoptotic pathway. The Ubc9/ITGB3
  pathway activates apoptossis in a mitochondria-independent manner.
