caption: Role of the FGF/FGFR signaling pathway in the development and progression
  of HCC. FGF signaling pathway governs multiple cellular processes including cell
  proliferation, differentiation, apoptosis, drug resistance, migration and invasion.
  FGF/FGFR exerts its physiological function through governing its downstream targets.
  The chemical inhibitors of FGF/FGFR, antibodies, miRNAs, and natural agents could
  inactivate FGF signaling pathway. Thus, FGF/FGFR could be explored as potential
  drug targets for the treatment of HCC patients. FGF, fibroblast growth factor; HCC,
  hepatocellular carcinoma.
