caption: Canonical Wnt signaling activates DPAGT1 expression and protein N-glycosylation,
  leading to extensive N-glycosylation of E-cadherin. In HNSCC, the positive feedback
  loop between Wnt signaling and DPAGT1 is amplified and partially inhibited by wnt
  pathway inhibitor DDK1. Furthermore, extensive N-glycosylation of E-cadherin prevents
  it from depleting nuclear β/γ-catenins allowing the positive feedback between Wnt
  and DPAGT1 to operate without controls. CTHRC1 is upregulated by DPAGT1 and canonical
  Wnt signaling, affecting the noncanonical Wnt pathway.
