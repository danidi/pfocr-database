  1 (GFPT1)–mediated MTOR signaling pathway affected by glucose and fructose in porcine
  trophectoderm cells. Available evidence from our study indicates that fructose and
  glucose are metabolized via GFPT1 in the hexosamine biosysthesis pathway and activate
  mTOR-RPS6K and mTOR-EIF4EBP1 signal transduction cascades for porcine trophoblast
  cell proliferation and mRNA translation, as well as synthesis of glycosaminoglycans
