caption: Paracrine FGF signaling pathways. A ternary FGF-FGFR-HS complex results from
  the binding of canonical FGF to FGFR with HS. This complex then activates the FGFR
  intracellular tyrosine kinase domain by phosphorylation of specific tyrosine residues.
  The FRS2α, a major FGFR kinase substrate, is phosphorylated by the activated FGFR
  kinase. Phosphorylated FRS2α then recruits the RAS/MAPK pathway. MAPK activates
  members of the ETS transcription factor family and negative regulators of the FGF
  signaling pathways. Phosphorylated FRS2α also recruits the enzyme PI3K, which then
  phosphorylates AKT. AKT has multiple activities including the activation of forkhead
  box protein O1 (FOXO1) transcription factor. Activated FGFR kinase recruits and
  activates the enzyme PLCγ as well, which produces inositol triphosphate (IP3) and
  phosphatidylinositol bisphosphate (PIP2). Also, FGFR kinase activates STAT1, 3,
  and 5, which mostly regulate gene expression in the nucleus. Copyright held by,
  and used with permission of, The University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center.
