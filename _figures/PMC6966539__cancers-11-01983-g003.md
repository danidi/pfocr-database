caption: Dual effect of p53 on autophagy. Multiple mechanisms have implicated p53
  in the regulation of autophagy with both repressing and promoting effects. Regulation
  of DRAM, Ulk1, Atg7, and Isg20L1 results in promotion of autophagy, whereas repression
  of PINK1 results in promotion of mitophagy. Effect on AMPK/mTOR pathway negatively
  influences autophagy and similar effect has been ascribed to cytoplasmic functions
  of p53.
