caption: Enzymatic biosynthesis of 2-AG. Receptor-regulated endocannabinoid release
  (RER) and calcium-assisted RER (CaRER) employ a phospholipase C β (PLCβ)-dependent
  pathway to convert phospholipid precursors to diacylglycerol (DAG). The source of
  DAG for calcium-dependent endocannabinoid release (CaER) is currently unknown. Both
  pathways intersect with the formation of DAG, which is then converted to 2-AG precursors
  by diacylglycerol lipases (DAGL-α and -β).
