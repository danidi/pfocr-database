caption: The main apoptotic pathways. Three main pathways are involved in apoptosis.
  Some molecules that regulate apoptosis may act as apoptosis activators (red) or
  repressors (green font). Abnormal proteins (AP) activate ER stress induced apoptotic
  pathway (blue) whereas death ligands attach to death receptors, which activates
  receptor pathway (grey). These two pathways may activate mitochondrial pathway (yellow)
  by the action of so-called molecular link-tBid protein. Nevertheless, mitochondrial
  pathway may be also activated independently.
