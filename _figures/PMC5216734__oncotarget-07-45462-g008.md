caption: miR-222 transcription is increased via the PKC and PI3K pathways and partially
  via the MAPK pathway. The pri(mary)-miR-222 transcript is cleaved by the RNAse II
  enzyme Drosha into a hairpin structure (pre-miR-222) which is transported from the
  nucleus to the cytoplasm via exportin-5. Pre-miR-222 is cleaved by a second RNase
  II enzyme, Dicer, into mature miR-222 which associates with RISC to target imperfect
  complementary mRNA sequences. Mature miR-222 inhibits the translation of p27 which
  increases cell migration and epithelial mesenchymal transition in AGSGR cells.
