caption: CD55 regulates self-renewal and cisplatin resistance in endometrioid tumors.
  CD55 is glycophosphatidylinositol (GPI)–anchored to lipid rafts and via LIME binding
  signals intracellularly to ROR2 and LCK. ROR2 via JNK signaling regulates pluripotency
  gene expression, namely NANOG, SOX2, and OCT4 to maintain stemness in CSCs. In parallel,
  CD55 via the LCK pathway promotes the expression of DNA repair genes (including
  BRCA1 and MLH1) to drive cisplatin resistance.
