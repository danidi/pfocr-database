caption: The model of signaling pathways in RTK-expressing cells. (1) In RTK-activated

  Bak. (3) ABT-263 binds to Bcl-xL with high affinity, dissociating it from Bak. (4)
  When freed from all its inhibitory associations, Bak is activated, forming a pore
  on the mitochondria. (5) Cytochrome c is released into cytosol. (6) The release
  of cytochrome c triggers the assembly of the death inducing apoptosome in the cytosol
