  underlying the DNA damage response, including the G2M cell cycle checkpoint, BRCA1-dependent

  and ATM signaling. (Dark gray) miR-182-5p targets identified by biotin pull-down.

  random gene lists of similar size using IPA (P-value < 0.02).
