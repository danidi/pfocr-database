caption: A potential role for TXNIP in HG-mediated RPE dysfunction. Diabetes and hyperglycemia
  upregulates TXNIP and causes cellular ROS/RNS stress, mitochondrial dysfunction,
  mitophagic flux, lysosome enlargement/destabilization, inflammation and RPE dysfunction.
  Released lysosomal enzymes may degrade mitochondrial membrane proteins and a vicious
  cycle of mitochondria–lysosome pathway dysfunction may be established. TXNIP knockdown
  or mitochondrial targeted antioxidants may prevent several deleterious effects of
  high glucose and TXNIP on RPE in DR.
