caption: Predicted targets of miR-100 and miR-101 in the 3′-UTR of members of the
  mTOR pathway. (A) Schematic of the mTOR signaling pathway. mTOR functions by partnering
  with either raptor or rictor and controls mRNA translation or cell growth and survival,
  respectively. Rapamycin inhibits the function of the mTOR/raptor complex, while
  the function of Akt, the only known target of the mTOR/rictor complex, can be inhibited
  by caffeine. PI3K, phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase. (B) Predicted targets. For each
  duplex, the upper sequence is from the predicted target and the lower sequence is
  the mature form of the indicated miRNA. For mTOR at least, the predicted target
  sequences are highly conserved among diverse mammalian species.
