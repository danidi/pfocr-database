  that are involved in the regulation of diverse cellular functions, including apoptotic,
  metabolism and cell cycle progression. As illustrated by these targets, a high degree
  of functional versatility and overlap exists amongst AKT substrates. Red and black
  line indicates inhibition and activation respectively. AKT, protein kinase B; AS160,
  Akt Substrate of 160 kDa; FoxO1, Forkhead box O1; GSK3, glycogen synthase kinase
  3; GPCR, G-protein-coupled receptors; IRS-1/2,insulin receptor substrate-1/2; IKK,

  protein kinase 1; PI3K, phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase; PIP2, phosphatidylinositol
  4,5-biphosphate; PIP3, phosphatidylinositol 3,4,5-triphosphate; PTEN, Phosphatase

  proteins; TSC1/2, tuberous sclerosis complex 1/2.
