caption: Annexin A1 binds to membrane FRP2, a G-protein coupled receptor, and activates
  G protein. Activated β subunit down streams phospholipase Cγ2 (PLCγ2), phosphatidylinositol
  3,4-bisphosphate (PIP2), inositol triphosphate (IP3), and diacylglycerol (DAG).
  IP3 mobilizes intracellular pool of calcium, which activates calmodulin/calcineurin
  (CaM/Calcineurin). DAG also activates protein kinase C (PKC). PKC and CaM/Calcineurin
  activates nuclear factor activated of T cells 2 (NFAT2) to induce differentiation.
  α subunit downstream RAS, MEK1/2, ERK1/2. ERK1/2 activates ELK-1 and cell differentiation
