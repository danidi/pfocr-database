  beta-catenin (green background), and PCP (blue background). Up/down arrows indicate
  overexpression and downregulation, respectively, in Group 1 samples. Genes whose
  expression change is consistent with canonical pathway inhibition and PCP pathway
  activation are colored red. Those genes promoting canonical signaling and inhibiting
  the PCP branch are colored green. Genes with no significant change in expression,
  or whose expression change has no selectivity between the canonical and non-canonical
  branches are colored white. The down-regulation of CAMK2D and PRKCA (dark blue)
  acts to inhibit Wnt Ca2+ signaling.
