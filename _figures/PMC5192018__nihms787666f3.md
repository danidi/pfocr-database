  metabolic hormonesThe peripheral hormones that transmit metabolic signals to GnRH

  IGF-1=insulin-like growth factor 1. KISS1=kisspeptin. KISS1R=kisspeptin receptor.
  LH=luteinising hormone. +=stimulatory effect. –=inhibitory effect.'
