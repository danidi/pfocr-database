caption: Hierarchical signaling pathway expression regulates regeneration. Tissue
  injury results in the activation of the inflammatory response at the site of damage.
  Following inflammation, lower vertebrate regeneration requires cellular dedifferentiation
  and cellular proliferation. FGF signaling is initiated at the early stages of regeneration,
  which further activates HH signaling. Both FGF as well as HH signaling pathways
  directly and indirectly activate WNT signaling. These factors activate the genes
  required for cell cycle and growth during regeneration.
