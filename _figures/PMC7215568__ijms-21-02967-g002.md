  of the PI3K/AKT/mTOR pathway in response to growth factors, among others, stimulates
  the binding of MNK to mTORC1, regulating the formation of the mTORC1/TELO2/DDB1
  complex. MNKs phosphorylate eIF4E and other substrates controlling the expression
  of specific proteins involved in cell growth, apoptosis and metastasis.
