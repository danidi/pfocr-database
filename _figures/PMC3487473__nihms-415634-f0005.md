caption: 'SCOP localization within the neuron. SCOP is expressed in the cytosol, on
  membranes, and in membrane rafts. SCOP colocalizes with and binds to K-Ras in membrane
  rafts. SCOP is also reported to have phosphatase activity in non-neuronal cells,
  which may have other functions in neurons. Inset: In the hippocampus, SCOP attenuates
  ERK1/2 activity by binding to the nucleotide-free form of K-Ras, thereby inhibiting
  the ERK signaling pathway. Calpain-mediated degradation of SCOP may contribute to
  activation of ERK1/2.'
