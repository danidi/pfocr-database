caption: The schematic summarization for the signaling mechanisms of the antifibrotic
  effects of H2S. (a) H2S inhibits LPS-induced inflammation through the Toll-like
  receptor-NF κB signal transduction. (b) H2S ablates the activation of TGF-β1 and
  MAPK kinases pathway. (c) H2S regulates redox production by inhibiting the expression/activity
  of NOX4 in addition to direct reaction with some reactive oxygen species (ROS).
  Lastly, H2S is able to modulate cellular metabolism and homeostasis by activating
  AMPK and sirtuin 1.
