caption: Model of Izb regulation of PTHR signaling cascade in bone. Both PTH and Izb
  are able to induce β-catenin (β-Cat)/TCF signaling. Izb stabilizes β-Cat by inhibiting
  its proteasome degradation. β-Cat–mediated PTHR signaling switch is blocked by Izb
  due to its promoting of β-Cat translocation, thereby dissociating β-Cat from the
  PTHR. Izb facilitates PTH stimulation of CREB phosphorylation, β-Cat (Ser-675) phosphorylation,
  GSK3β (Ser-9) phosphorylation, and TCF/Lef activity through a cAMP/PKA signaling
  pathway. Collectively these findings suggest that the combination of PTH and Izb
  favors the anabolic PTH action in bone.
