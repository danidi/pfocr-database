  cells. The left panel shows a neuroendocrine cell in benign prostate and prostatic
  adenocarcinoma. Autocrine stimulation of CXCR2 by IL-8 activates P53 and inhibits
  cell proliferation. On the right is a NE tumor cell in prostatic small cell carcinoma.
  A p53 mutation inactivates the IL-8/CXCR2/p53 pathway and removes a major growth
  inhibitory signal, leading to rapid proliferation of the NE cell.
