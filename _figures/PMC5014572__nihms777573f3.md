caption: The ligands for MRGPRX2 activate pertussis toxin-dependent Gαi and pertussis
  toxin-independent Gαq pathways for MC responses. MCs express MRGPRX2 on the cell
  surface as well as intracellular sites. The extracellular receptor probably uses
  the Orai-1 Ca2+ channel and the pertussis toxin-independent Gαq pathway for MC degranulation
  whereas the intracellular receptor may use the Orai-2 Ca2+ channel to mediate MC
  responses. Activation of MRGPRX2 also results in Gαi-dependent production of the
  pruritogenic cytokine IL-31 via the MAP kinase and Akt-dependent pathways.
