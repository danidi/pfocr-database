caption: Working model for SPRR1A activation in the myocardium. In response to myocardial
  injuries, IL-6 cytokines are released and act on the surrounding cells (left panel).
  The binding of IL-6 cytokines (CT-1, LIF) to gp130/LIFR receptor complex activates
  STAT3, PI-3K, and MAPK signaling pathways (right panel). A MAPK signaling pathway
  activates the transcription factors C/EBPβ and AP-1. In turn, they bind to SPRR1A
  promoter and induce SPRR1A gene transcription. SPRR1A confers a protective effect
  to the cardiomyocytes, possibly by stabilizing the actin cytoskeleton.
