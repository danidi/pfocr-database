caption: Lipid A biosynthesis pathway. In Pseudomonas aeruginosa LpxA catalyzes the
  first step in the lipid A biosynthetic pathway (Raetz pathway) by mediating the
  reversible transfer of R-3-hydroxydecanoate from the acyl carrier protein (ACP)
  onto the 3-OH position of UDP-GlcNAc forming an ester bond. LpxD catalyzes the third
  step in the Raetz pathway, the reversible transfer of a R-3-hydroxydodecanoate from
  ACP onto the 2-NH2 of the UDP-3-O-(3-hydroxydecanoyl) glucosamine through the formation
  of an amide bond.
