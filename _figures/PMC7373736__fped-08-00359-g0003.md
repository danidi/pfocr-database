caption: Liver metabolic pathway of alcohol. ADH, alcohol dehydrogenase; ALDH, aldehyde
  dehydrogenase; ROS, reactive oxygen species; SOD, Superoxide Dismutase; GPX, Glutathione
  peroxidase; GR, Glutathione reductase. The ADH, ALDH enzymes and the P450 2E1 cytochrome
  participate in the oxidative metabolism of alcohol. The activity of the P450 2E1
  cytochrome in alcohol metabolism and the re-oxidation of NADH via the electron transport
  chain in the mitochondria results in the formation of ROS. Catalase in peroxisomes,
  SOD in mitochondria and GPX-GR in cytosol are activated by an increase of oxidative
  stress to reduce the levels of ROS. High amounts of ROS lead to a down-regulation
  (negative feedback) of the antioxidant response.
