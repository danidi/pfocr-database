caption: Proposed model of the HCMV-induced apoptotic signaling pathways. HCMV infection
  causes genotoxic stress, which activates ATM kinase. The activated ATM can activate
  p53 either directly or through activation of Chk2. The activation of p53 leads to
  the activation of mitochondrial pathway, ie, Bax and Bak, hence the activation of
  caspase-9 and caspase-3 resulting in cell apoptosis.
