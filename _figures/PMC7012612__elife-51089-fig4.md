caption: (A) IGF1 signaling pathway genes are significantly correlated with change
  in the 3L phenotype. (B) The IGF1 signaling pathway is significantly enriched for
  increased evolutionary constraint in large, long-lived species. The barcode indicates
  ranks of genes in the pathway within the list of all pathway-annotated genes. The
  worm indicates enrichment as calculated by a tricube moving average, a type of moving
  average in which values near the end of the sliding window are down weighted to
  reduce the effect of extreme values in any given window. The dashed horizontal line
  marks the null value indicating no enrichment. (C) The IGF1 signaling pathway contains
  many genes whose evolutionary rates are negatively correlated with the 3L phenotype.
  Shading indicates the Rho-signed negative log p-value for the correlation. Genes
  in white are not included in the IGF1 pathway annotation used to calculate pathway
  enrichment statistics, but they are included in the diagram for sake of completeness.
  The GNB2L1 gene (gray) is in the IGF1 pathway annotation, but correlation statistics
  were not calculated for that gene because too few branches in the gene tree met
  the minimum branch length cut-off.
