caption: 'Proposed mechanism of ER stress-mediated induction of CAMP in human KC.
  A VDR-dependent pathway has been shown previously to regulate CAMP expression in
  human cells including KC (). In addition to the VDR-dependent pathway, ER stress
  regulates CAMP via a novel NF-κB-C/EBPα signal in human KC. Both pathways are likely
  to alternatively regulate CAMP expression depending upon cellular environments:
  e.g. non-stress versus low levels of stress. VDRE, VDR element; RXR, retinoid X
  receptor.'
