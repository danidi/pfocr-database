  pathways. (1) Conventional chemotherapeutic agents or radiation target DNA. (2)
  Different strategies to disrupt microRNAs can be offered. (3) EBV-specific cytotoxic

  the lytic cycle of EBV. Agents targeting the (5) NF-kB pathway, (6), PI3K/Akt pathway,
  (7) PKC pathway, (8) MAPK pathway, (9) Lyn or (10) Sky can be tried. The monoclonal
  antibody SGNE-35 targets (11) CD30.
