  (XOR) catalyzes the transformations of hypoxanthine to xanthine and xanthine to
  uric acid. XOR-deficient patients secrete xanthine, which is formed from guanine.
  Accumulated hypoxanthine is mostly converted to inosine monophosphate (IMP) via
  the salvage pathway using 5-phospho-α-D-ribosyl 1-pyrophosphate (PRPP) as a co-substrate.
