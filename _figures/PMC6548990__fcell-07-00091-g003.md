  complexes which allow activation of the classical NFκB pathway. TNFR2 recruitment
  of TRAF2-cIAP1/2 and TRAF1-TRAF2-cIAP1/2 complexes concomitantly depletes the cytosolic
  pool of these proteins and hinder them so to trigger degradation of the alternative
  NFκB pathway stimulating kinase NIK. Thus, TNFR2 also activates the alternative
  NFκB pathway.
