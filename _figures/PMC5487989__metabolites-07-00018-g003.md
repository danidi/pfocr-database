  dinucleotide; NADH: reduced nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide; NADP+: oxidized nicotinamide
  adenine dinucleotide phosphate; NADPH: reduced nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide
  phosphate; Pi: inorganic phosphate; ROS: reactive oxygen species.'
