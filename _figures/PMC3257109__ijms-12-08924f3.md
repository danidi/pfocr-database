caption: BMP signaling in motor neurons. (A) In Drosophila, the evidence suggests
  that a retrograde signal involving homologues of the BMP pathway regulates synaptic
  growth, assembly and maintenance by pathways related to either Smad or cytoskeleton
  regulators; (B) In vertebrates, spinal cord injury induces an increase of BMP ligands
  around the injury site. BMP receptors and Smad1 phosphorylation is also increased
  in spinal neurons, revealing the ability of BMP to transduce Smad-dependent signaling.
  However, the target genes induced by Smad pathways or the mechanisms involving local
  cytoskeletal rearrangement are unknown.
