caption: 'A: In macrophages, TRIF signaling activates TBK1 and IKKe that activate
  expression of Pellino-1 via the IRF3-dependent mechanism and also phosphorylate
  Pellino-1. Pellino-1 promotes K63-linked polyubiquitylation of RIP1 (dashed arrow),
  leading to recruitment of TAK1 and TAK1-mediated phosphorylation (p) and activation
  of IKK kinases that mediate activation of NF-κB. B: In microglia cells, TLR2 or
  TLR4 signal activation of TRAF6 via the MyD88-dependent pathway to activate MAPK
  and the transcription factor AP-1 that co-operate with NF-κB to initiate transcription
  of pro-inflammatory cytokine genes. TRAF3 negatively regulates MAPK activation,
  which is blocked via Pellino-1-mediated K63-linked polyubiquitination of IAP2, leading
  to IAP2-mediated K48-linked polyubiquitination and degradation of TRAF3.'
