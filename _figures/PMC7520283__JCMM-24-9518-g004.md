caption: 'Wnt/β‐catenin signalling pathway in OA‐FLS inflammation. FoxC1, a target
  gene of miR‐200a‐3p, promoted human OA‐FLS proliferation, pro‐inflammatory cytokine
  and catabolic enzymes expression by elevated β‐catenin production. IL‐1β inhibited
  miR‐200a‐3p enhanced the effects of FoxC1 in human OA‐FLS. XAV‐939, a small‐molecule
  inhibitor of Wnt pathway, reduced proliferation and I collagen levels of OA‐FLS.
  Lorecivivint, a small‐molecule Wnt pathway inhibitor, declined pro‐inflammatory
  cytokines by inhibiting NF‐κB and STAT3 phosphorylation through reduced CLK2 and
  DYRK1A production in human OA‐FLS. LRP5/6: Lipoprotein receptor‐associated protein;
  GSK3β: Glycogen synthase kinase 3β; CK1: Casein kinase 1; APC: Adenomatous polyposis
  coli; LEF/TCF: Lymphoid enhancer factor/T‐cell; CLK2: CDC‐like kinase 2; DYRK1A:
  Dual‐specificity tyrosine phosphorylation‐regulated kinase 1A'
