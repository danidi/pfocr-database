caption: Blue shaded boxes indicate probable negatively correlated pathway while yellow
  boxes indicate positively correlated pathway due to presence of mutant FAD2 genes.
  Action of mutant FAD2A and FAD2B in the S-population while FAD2A QTL in the T-population
  stops desaturation of oleic acid (C18:1) to linoleic acid (C18:2). The capital letters
  in parenthesis (A-D) indicate the pathway through which fatty acids are produced
  by acyl carrier protein (ACP) synthesis within the plastid. ACCase, Acetyl-CoA carboxylase;
  FAS, fatty acid synthase; KAS, ketoacyl ACP synthetase; Δ9DES, Δ9 desaturase; ACS,
  acyl-CoA synthetase; FAT B, palmitoyl-ACP thioesterase; FATA, stearoyl-ACP thioesterase;
  FAE, fatty acid elongase, FAD2, fatty acid desaturase.
