caption: 'Different gene expression profiles in metabolic pathways of E. limosum ATCC
  8486. (a) Embden-Meyerhof-Parnas glycolytic pathway (blue circle), incomplete tricarboxylic
  acid cycle (brown circle), Wood-Ljungdahl pathway (green circle), and pentose phosphate
  pathway (purple circle) are shown. (b) Ethanol producing pathway (red circle), hydrogenase
  complex (grey circle), Rnf complex (dark circle), ETF complex (blue circle), and
  ATP synthase complex (orange circle) are shown. Metabolites are shown as circles
  and reactions are shown as arrows. All gene numbers and enzyme reactions are provided
  next to the arrows. Transcriptional dynamics of E. limosum ATCC 8486 are described
  in the heatmap with three columns; the left and middle boxes indicate normalized
  RNA reads from heterotrophic and autotrophic conditions, respectively. The right
  box indicates RNA fold change, as autotrophic expression over heterotrophic expression.
  In addition, gene numbers responsible for the expression are located under the bottom
  box of the heatmap. A full list and expression data can be found in Additional file
  : Table S1'
