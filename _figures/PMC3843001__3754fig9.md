caption: A proposed model of mTOR myogenic signaling. Amino acids activate two pathways
  of opposing functions in myogenesis. The Rag GTPases activate mTORC1 and subsequently
  suppress the PI3K-Akt pathway through IRS1 phosphorylation. The Vps34-PLD1-PA pathway
  activates a kinase-independent function of mTOR that regulates myogenic expression
  of IGF-II. A third mTOR pathway, mediated by MyoD and follistatin, is rapamycin
  sensitive and kinase dependent but is independent of amino acids.
