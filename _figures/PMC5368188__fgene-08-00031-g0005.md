caption: Model for LIN28A/ZCCHC11 regulation of let-7 microRNA biogenesis, promoting
  cellular proliferation and modifying bioenergetics. LIN28A recruits the TUTase ZCCHC11
  to pre-let-7 where ZCCHC11 adds a short polyU tail to pre-let-7. Pre-let-7 is no
  longer a DICER substrate and is targeted for degradation by DIS3L2, thereby blocking
  let-7 maturation into its functional tumor suppressor form. Loss of mature let-7
  microRNAs causes overexpression of numerous oncogenes and bioenergetic genes.
