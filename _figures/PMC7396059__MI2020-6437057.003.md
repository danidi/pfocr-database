caption: NKG2D signaling pathways. After DAP10 associates with NKG2D, it can bind
  to PI3K and the Grb2-Vav-1-SOS1 complex. The Grb2-Vav-1-SOS1 complex activates SLP76,
  PLCγ2, and Rac1. Rac1 can activate the MAPK signaling pathway and promote actin
  polymerization. SLP76/PCLγ2 causes Ca2+ influx. Furthermore, it leads to cellular
  degranulation and secretion of cytokines. PI3K can recruit PCL-γ, Rac1, and Akt
  signaling molecules to activate NK cell function. In addition, PI3K can recruit
  the SOS1-Grb2-Vav1 complex via SOS1 and recruit the small Ras-family GTPase Rap1
  with the help of CrkL to achieve cytotoxicity.
