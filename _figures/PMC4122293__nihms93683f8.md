caption: 'On the left, fibronectin promotes ET-1 production by activating Src, which,
  in turn, activates a Shc/Grb2/Sos complex by direct phosphorylation of Shc; phosphorylated
  Shc then activates the ERK signaling pathway, which leads to increased preproendothelin-1
  expression. Increased endogenous ET-1 activity contributes to enhanced contraction
  and smooth muscle α actin expression in activated stellate cells. The proposed pathway
  to cellular contraction is based on previous literature in smooth muscle cells .
  Abbreviations: PKC = protein kinase C, MLC = myosin light chain, MLCK = myosin light
  chain kinase.'
