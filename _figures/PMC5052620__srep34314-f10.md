caption: Areca nut acts on muscarinic acid receptors to release calcium and activate
  CAMKII. It also induces intracellular reactive oxygen species (ROS). CAMKII and
  ROS together activate JNK which subsequently phosphorylates ATF2 and c-Jun transcription
  factors. The two transcription factors induce TGF-β2 promoter. The translated TGF-β
  protein can now activate the canonical SMAD signaling pathway and auto-induce TGF-β
  and other targets *22 in epithelial cells.
