caption: TWEAK/Fn14 Signaling. TWEAK/Fn14 binding induces the recruitment of cIAP
  and TRAF-1, 2, 3, and 5 and leads to activation of different kinases such as mitogen-activated
  protein kinase kinases (MKK), transforming growth factor β-activated kinase 1 (TAK1),
  and NF-κB inducing kinase (NIK). TAK1 activates IKKβ and NIK activates IKKα, leading
  to the activation of canonical or non-canonical NF-κB pathway, respectively. MMK
  are responsible for the activation of c-Jun N-terminal kinase 1 (JNK1) and p38 MAPK,
  which activate transcription factor activator protein-1 (AP-1). Increased activation
  of NF-κB and AP-1 leads to the expression of specific target genes responsible for
  TWEAK-mediated responses.
