caption: Ovulatory cascade. LH/hCG induces PGE2 synthesis and secretion in cumulus
  cells. Additionally, granulosa cells under FSH stimulation release PGE2, which is
  coupled with PGT and transported to the cumulus cell membrane to bind the PGE2 receptors
  EP2 and EP4. Via these receptors, PGE2 induces MAPK phosphorylation, increases intracellular
  cAMP levels and activates the PKB/MAPK3/1 pathway to stimulate ovulatory gene expression.
  Furthermore, PGE2 stimulates Angiotensin II to inhibit the inhibitory factors against
  meiotic maturation, released by theca cells
