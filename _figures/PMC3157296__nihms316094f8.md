  differentiation and survival. Collaboratively, PTEN loss activates the PI3K/AKT
  signaling pathway and reduces the AR-regulated FKBP5-PHLPP negative feedback loop
  to AKT activation, further enhances AKT activation, leading to androgen/AR-independent
  prostate epithelial proliferation.
