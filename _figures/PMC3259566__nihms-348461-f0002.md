caption: In normoxia, hydroxylation at 2 proline residues promotes HIF-α association
  with pVHL and HIF-α destruction via the ubiquitin/proteasome pathway, while hydroxylation
  of an asparagine residue blocks association with coactivators. In hypoxia, these
  processes are suppressed, allowing HIF-α subunits (both HIF-1α and HIF-2α) to escape
  proteolysis, dimerize with HIF-1β, recruit coactivators, and activate transcription
  via HREs. N, asparagine; P, proline; OH, hydroxyl group; Ub, ubiquitin [modified,
