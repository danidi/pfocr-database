caption: Schematic representation of Fas apoptotic signaling pathway and the hypothetic
  steps inhibited by LFG. After Fas stimulation, caspase-8 cleaves Bap31 and tBid.
  p20 fragment induces Ca2+ efflux from the ER to the mitochondria, which in conjunction
  with tBid induces mitochondrial permeabilization and cytochrome c release. Although
  Bcl-XL is able to inhibit ER calcium release and mitochondria permeabilization directly,
  LFG is only able to block the ER step.
