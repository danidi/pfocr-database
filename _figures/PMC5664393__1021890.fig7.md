  (CLL) cells. BAFF-R engagement stabilizes NIK within the NIK/TRAF2/TRAF3/cIAP1/2
  complex, promoting the non-canonical NFκB pathway activity. SYK recruitment to TRAF2/TRAF3
  assists BAFF-mediated activation of BCR signaling, which contributes to activation
  of the canonical NFκB. Concurrently, SYK induces STAT3 transcription factor, thereby
  up-regulating Mcl-1, a pro-survival Bcl-2 family member.
