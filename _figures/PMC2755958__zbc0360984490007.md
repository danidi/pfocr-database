  physiological conditions, Stat3 activity is under the positive and negative regulation
  of Janus kinases and Shp2, respectively (A). Shp2 gain-of-function mutations lead
  to the hyperactivation of the RAS/ERK signaling pathway and the excessive inactivation
  of Stat3, which synergistically promotes the pathogenesis of Noonan syndrome and/or
  JMML (B).
