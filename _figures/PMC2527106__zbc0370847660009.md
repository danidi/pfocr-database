caption: Proposed model for Rap1-mediated response on stimulated amylase  release.
  Activation of Rap1 occurs after the stimulation by secretagogues  CCK, carbachol,
  and VIP in pancreatic acini. Our findings show that different  second messengers,
  including DAG, Ca2+, and cAMP generated from  activation of different receptors,
  CCK and muscarinic as well as VPAC  receptors, are able to mediate the activation
  of Rap1 via either CalDAG-GEFIII  or Epac1 and thereby regulate amylase secretion
  in pancreatic acinar cells.  Note that the broken line indicates a less well defined
  pathway.
