  cell populations. It is a growth factor for B lymphocytes, and promotes survival
  for a number of different cell types. When IL3 binds to its heterodimeric receptor,
  this leads to activation of downstream pathways including JAK/STAT, PI-3K/AKT and
  MAPK. We hypothesized that comprehensive mutagenesis of Ba/F3 cells, which normally
  require IL3 for growth, would reveal novel genes that promote signaling through
  the IL3 pathway.
