  C4Flaveria species In MC, PEP is used as a substrate for PEPC catalyzed carboxylation

  which is expected to exist in both MC and BSC (purple lines); moreover, phosphoenolpyruvate

  and glycolysis in cytosol (black lines). PEP is also involved in nitrogen recycling
  from xylem (orange lines). (Abbreviations: MC, mesophyll cell; BSC, bundle sheath
  cell; SP, shikimate pathway; PCR, photosynthetic carbon reduction; GC, glycolysis
  cycle.)'
