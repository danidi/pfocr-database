caption: Illustration contemplating experimental (in vitro, 3D organoids and in vivo)
  and clinical observations presented in the study. AZD5363 can be used as an efficient
  compound to treat mPIK3CA tumors by blocking the pathway at the level of AKT. However
  its efficiency may be compromised in patients harboring co-occurring MAP3K1 LoF
  mutation. Patients with reduced MAP3K1 expression have deregulated JNK pathway activity
  and consequently decreased phospho-serine IRS1 (S312) levels. This increases IRS1
  stability and subsequently brings about the re-activation of PI3Kα. pS, phospho-serine,
  GF, growth factor, GF-R, growth factor receptor.
