caption: Model of RhoG function in invadopodia. Phorbol esters, including PDBu and
  PMA, stimulate PKC, which induces the formation of invadopodia in a Src-dependent
  fashion. Assembly of invadopodia requires Rac1 activity. In contrast, RhoG activity
  needs to be downregulated for invadopodia to form. During disassembly, SGEF is recruited
  to invadopodia, where it activates RhoG, which promotes the phosphorylation of paxillin.
