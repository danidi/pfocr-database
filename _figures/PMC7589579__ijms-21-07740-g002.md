caption: (A) Schematic representation of the endogenous and microbiota-associated
  pathways engaged in tryptophan breakdown in the gut environment. Highlighted in
  violet is a putative light-independent synthesis pathway of 6-formylindolo [3,2-b]carbazole
  (FICZ), an endogenous aryl hydrocarbon receptor (AHR) ligand. (B) The Trp metabolites
  act as AHR and pregnane X receptor (PXR) ligands. Upon activation, AHR and PXR translocate
  into the nucleus and activate the transcription of target genes. (C) AHR stimuli
  play an important role in the development of intraepithelial lymphocytes (IEL) subsets
  and in control of the Treg–Th17 axis. Graphic created with BioRender.
