caption: The molecular mechanisms for acquired resistance. The mechanisms include
  target gene modification, alternative parallel pathway activation, downstream pathway
  activation, and histological/phenotypic transformation. Both the amplification and
  mutations of receptor tyrosine kinases (RTKs) can induce downstream survival signaling
  pathways. Moreover, the direct overexpression and/or mutations of components of
  downstream pathways can also contribute to acquired resistance by assisting tumor
  cell survival. These mechanisms provide potential targets for combination strategies
  for treatment in cases of acquired resistance.
