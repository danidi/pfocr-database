caption: Proposed model of HDAC10 regulation in G2/M transition. Under basal conditions,
  HDAC10 binds to the let-7f-2/miR-98 promoter region and deacetylates histone H3.
  let-7 repression results in upregulation of HMGA2. HMGA2 interferes with E4F binding
  to the cyclin A2 promoter and activates cyclin A2 transcription. In HDAC10 knockdown
  cells, expression levels of let-7f and miR-98, which mediate HMGA2 mRNA degradation,
  increase. Loss of HMGA2 promotes E4F binding to the cyclin A2 promoter, inhibiting
  its transcription.
