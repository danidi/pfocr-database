  to improve ʟ-ornithine accumulation. The red × represented this pathways were inactivated.

  and arrows indicated that pathways were overexpressed. The genes encoding enzymes
  involved in catalytically relevant reactions. pgi, encodes glucose-6-phosphate isomerase;
  zwf, encodes glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase; pgl, encodes 6-phosphogluconolactonase;
  tkt, encodes transketolase; tal, encodes transaldolase; opcA, encodes the putative

  branched amino acid transporter protein; odhA, encodes a subunit of 2-ketoglutarate
  dehydrogenase; gdh, encodes glutamate dehydrogenase; ncgl1221, encodes glutamate
