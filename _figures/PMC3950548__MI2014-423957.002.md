caption: Representation of IL-33 pathway in T-helper cells. IL-33 interacts with ST2L
  and the receptor accessory protein IL-RAcP in the membrane. Both possess a domain
  TIR that allows interacting with MyD88, IRAK1/4, TRAF6, and TAK1 in the cytosol.
  These intracellular signaling molecules determine IκK inactivation by phosphorylation
  and degradation in proteasome complex. The consequent NFκB activation results in
  the production of Th2 cytokines.
