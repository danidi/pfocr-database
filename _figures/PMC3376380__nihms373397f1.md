caption: mTOR signaling and its regulation by DGKs. mTOR signaling is mediated by
  two signaling complexes, raptor-containing mTORC1 and rictor containing mTORC2.
  DAG, generated by PLCγ1 following TCR engagement, activates the RasGRP1-Ras-Erk1/2
  pathway. The pathway plays important roles for mTORC1 and mTORC2 activation in T
  cells. DGKs negatively control both mTORC1 and mTORC2 signaling by terminating DAG
  in T cells.
