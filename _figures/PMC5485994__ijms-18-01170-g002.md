  activation of tyrosine phosphatases via Crh-r1 receptors leads to reduced phosphorylation

  StAR and consequently, diminished androgen production [,]. Green arrows/+ symbolize
  stimulatory effects. Red arrows/- symbolize inhibitory effects.
