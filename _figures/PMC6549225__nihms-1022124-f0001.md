caption: The potential role of a melanocyte antigen within the complex psoriatic inflammatory
  pathway. T cells encounter the autoantigen ADAMTSL5 (yellow circle) in the skin,
  via melanocyte (MC) presentation on MHC I. TCR stimulation in the context of the
  psoriatic pro-inflammatory milieu may lead to enhanced IL-17 production by these
  cells; however, the functional significance of ADAMTSL5 specific T cells remains
  unknown. Higher IL-17 levels contribute to dysregulated KC proliferation and release
  of LL37, cytokines and chemokines (red arrows) that perpetuate the inflammatory
  response.
