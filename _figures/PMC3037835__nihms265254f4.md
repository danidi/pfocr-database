  Ino− phenotypes. a Protein kinase C–Cell Wall integrity (PKC–CWI) pathway, signaling

  genes shaded in gray confer an Ino− phenotype. Genes enclosed in a rectangle are
  essential. Genes enclosed in a cylinder encode pore-forming proteins. Genes enclosed

  circle with a white letter “P” represents a phosphorylated target. A black oval
  with white letters “GTP” indicates a GTP-binding protein. Genes encoding mitogen
  activated protein kinase (MAPK) cascade components (kinases and scaffold proteins)
  are indicated with star symbols and elongated octagons according to the key on the
  left side of the figure. CW cell wall, PM plasma membrane, N nucleus
