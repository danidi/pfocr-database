caption: Scheme of the effect of pioglitazone on CKD-mediated ER stress in CKD-MSCs.
  CKD pathophysiological condition induces ER stress, ROS generation, and mitochondrial
  dysfunction, leading to reduction of cell proliferation in CKD-MSCs. However, treatment
  of CKD-MSCs with pioglitazone increases the activation of the PGC-1α-PrPC signal
  pathway, resulting in protection of mitochondrial function and cell proliferation
  against CKD-induced ER stress. Black arrow means cell signal pathway. Red arrow
  means down-regulation of protein activation or expression. Blue arrow means up-regulation
