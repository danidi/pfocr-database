caption: PI3 kinase signaling pathway in glioma. Class I PI3 kinases are activated
  by upstream signals from receptor tyrosine kinases (RTKs) including EGFR and other
  RTKs. PI3 kinase catalyzes production of the second messenger PIP3, which actives
  both Akt and PKC. Akt and PKC phosphorylate multiple downstream substrates. We found
  Akt was dispensable for mitogenic signaling between EGFR and mTOR in glioma cells,
  whereas PKC was critical (33). PIP3 is negatively regulated by the tumor suppressor
  PTEN, a phosphatase driving dephosphorylation of PIP3
