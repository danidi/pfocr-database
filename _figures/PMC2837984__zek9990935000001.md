caption: Mammalian SREBP pathway. Under sterol replete conditions, the SREBP-Scap
  complex is retained in the ER bound to INSIG. When sterol levels decrease, SREBP-Scap
  is transported to the Golgi apparatus, where SREBP is cleaved by the site 1 and
  site 2 proteases. The soluble N terminus of SREBP enters the nucleus and activates
  genes required for the synthesis and uptake of sterols and other lipids.
