caption: Pathway schematic. APE1/Ref-1 redox signaling contributes to the transactivation
  of HIF-1 and certain other transcription factors. HIF1α is stabilized under hypoxic
  conditions, leading to the formation of HIF-1 and subsequent expression of CA9.
  CA9 coordinates with the bicarbonate transporter and intracellular CAs to stabilize
  intracellular pH. APE1/Ref-1 redox signaling inhibition (with APX3330, APX2009,
  or APX2014) attenuates HIF-1-mediated CA9 expression, sensitizing tumor cells to
  CA9 inhibition (with SLC-0111 or FC12-531A).
