caption: 'Crosstalk between the PI3K/AKT pathway and p53 activation: Potential stratagies
  for combined radiosensitization. Gain of function mutations in PI3K or loss of function
  mutations in PTEN result in recruitment of Akt and PDK1/2 to the membrane where
  Akt is phosphorylated. Active Akt can phosphorylate MDM2 increasing cytoplasmic
  to nuclear shuttling and effectively dampening the p53 pathway. The effectiveness
  of the p53 pathway can be augmented by increasing p53 stability (nutlin-3), or targeting
  PI3K (LY294002), PDK1 (UNC-01) or Akt simultaneously.'
