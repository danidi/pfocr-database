caption: The GPR54/GnRH signaling pathway in the HPG axis. The Fy formula inhibits
  the gene expression of Kiss1, decreases the secretion of kisspeptin, and weakens
  the stimulating effect on the kisspeptin receptor GPR54. Gq/11 inhibits PLC, which
  reduces the hydrolysis of PIP2. This process leads to a decrease in the production
  of the second messenger DAG, which inhibits the downstream Erk1/2 signaling pathway,
  resulting in a decrease in GnRH secretion, weakening the stimulatory effect on the
  pituitary gland, reducing the secretion of LH and FSH, and inhibiting gonadal development.
