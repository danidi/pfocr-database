caption: . Model for non-cell-autonomous neurotoxicity of neuron-released α-synuclein.
  Neuron-released α-synuclein interacts and activates TLR2 on the surface of microglia.
  Activation of the TLR2 signaling pathway induces proinflammatory responses in microglia,
  which releases neurotoxic agents, such as pro-inflammatory cytokines and chemokines.
  This model suggests that the interaction between TLR2 and α-synuclein is a potential
  therapeutic target for PD.
