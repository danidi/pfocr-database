  action are schematically depicted. Two examples of E1/E2-derived genotoxic mutations
  are provided. Each pathway represents a simplified summary of the current understanding
  of the area from its vast literature, some of which are referenced in the introduction.
  Ade: adenine, Gua: guanine.'
