caption: A crosstalk model of ATF3 and c-myc in serum induction. Serum induces c-Myc
  and ATF2 through MEK1–ERK pathway, and also c-Jun by RALGDS/Src–JNK pathway (; ).
  These factors form complex at or around the ATF/CRE motif of ATF3 gene promoter,
  and thereby activate ATF3 gene expression. Expressed ATF3 protein functions as a
  positive regulator of cell cycle and mediates cell proliferative activity of c-Myc.
