caption: Cellular ceramide upregulates apoptotic Bcl-x or caspase-9 expression via
  activation of PP1 and RNA splicing. Ceramide generated in the de novo synthesis
  pathway responding to gemcitabine activates nuclear PP1 and increases the amounts
  of non-phosphorylated SRp30a that binds to the splicing sites of pre-mRNA of Bcl-x
  or caspase-9. These will increase the expression of Bcl-x (L) or the caspase-9b
  isoform that is the pro-apoptosis. PP1, protein phosphatase 1.
