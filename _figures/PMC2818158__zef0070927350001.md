  by TNF, IL1RI by IL1β, TLR3 by double-stranded RNA (dsRNA), TLR4 by bacterial LPS,
  and activation of other TLRs can signal via specific intermediary factors such as
  TRADD, TRAF2, RIP1, MEKK3, TAK1, TAB2/3, and NIK (for the TNFR), MyD88, IRAKs, TRAF6
  and TAK1 (for IL1RI), Trif, RIP1, TRAF6 and TAK1 (for TLR3) and MyD88, Mal, Trif,
  Tram, RIP1, IRAKs, TRAF6 and TAK1 (for TLR4) to the MAPK pathway and to the activation
  and regulation of NF-κB and AP-1. Additionally, triggering TLR3 or TLR4 signaling
  cascades can instigate IKKε and TBK1 activation and subsequent IRF3-regulated gene
  transcription (,,,). TAB, TAK1-binding protein.'
