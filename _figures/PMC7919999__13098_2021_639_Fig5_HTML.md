  induces macrophage infiltration, developing an inflammatory state. In the insulin
  signaling pathway (IRS-1/2:PI3K:AKT:mTORC2) the MCP1 gene is suppressed by mTORC2
  in adipose. In situations of insulin resistance, the downregulation of insulin signaling
  with reduced activity of mTORC2 will derepress MCP1 and will attract monocytes to
  adipose tissue, which will be converted into M1 macrophage
