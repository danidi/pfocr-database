  HER2 activates 2 major signaling pathways (ERK and AKT) that utilize transcriptional
  and posttranscriptional mechanisms to inhibit the mitochondrial pathway of apoptosis.

  the addition of the drug (B). These interactions are supported by the literature,
  but the true complexity of this interface is likely much greater.
