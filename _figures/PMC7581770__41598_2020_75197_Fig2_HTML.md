  RANKL induced osteoclastogenesis transcriptomics. (A–C) Metabolism of water soluble
  vitamins and cofactors pathway sub networks showing differentially regulated genes

  conjugation of compounds sub network showing methylation pathway. (E,F) Metabolism

  blue—downregulated genes). (G) Summary of findings showing defective vitamin metabolism
  could lead to Homocysteine accumulation. (H) RANKL/RANK signaling in AVNFH transcriptomics.
  (I) Iron uptake and transport pathway in RANKL induced osteoclastogenesis transcriptomics.
