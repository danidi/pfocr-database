  in the regulation of: (A) MYC at p21, rRNA, TERT, and other promoters; (B) E2F1

  KAI1 promoter in normal and metastatic cells; (G) LEF/TCF-mediated activation/repression
  of the Wnt signaling pathway through various co-factors; and (H) other transcription
  factors.'
