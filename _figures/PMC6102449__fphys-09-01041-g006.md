caption: The working pathway for Er-Miao-Fang inhibiting inflammation and blocking
  hepatic gluconeogenesis. HFD feeding induced inflammation and increased hepatic
  glucagon response, which leading to endogenous glucose production. Er-Miao-Fang
  inhibited inflammation and improved adipose dysfunction by preserving PDE3B and
  decreasing the accumulation of cAMP to prevent lipolysis. By blocking the traffic
  of fatty acids and inflammatory mediators from adipose tissue to the liver, Er-Miao-Fang
  inhibited the effect on hepatic gluconeogenesis through preserving PDE4B and reducing
  cAMP/PKA signaling.
