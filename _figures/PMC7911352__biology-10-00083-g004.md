  indicated in green and red, respectively. (Abbreviations: α-KG, α-ketoglutarate;

  90; MESH1, metazoan SpoT homologue 1; NADH, nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide; NADK,
  NAD kinase; NADPH, nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide phosphate NEDD4, neuronal precursor
  cell-expressed developmentally downregulated 4; SLC1A5, solute carrier family 1
  member 5; SLC38A5, solute carrier family 38 member 5; SQSTM1, sequestosome 1; VDAC2/3,
  voltage-dependent anion channels 2 and 3).'
