  molecules become stably associated with areas of the plasma membrane enriched in
  PI5P and, possibly, other mono-PIs. This favors both the optimal signaling output
  and the diversification activities of active Vav1 proteins, as the mono-PI-mediated
  regulation impacts more significantly in the levels of stimulation of the NFAT pathway.
