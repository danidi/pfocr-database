caption: Activation of PI3K-AKT pathway by external signals via G-protein coupled
  receptors (GPCR) or receptor tyrosine kinases (RTK) leads to dissociation of the
  p85 regulatory subunit from the active p110 PI3K subunit. The active subunit catalyses
  the conversion of PIP2 to PIP3 at the plasma membrane, leading to the recruitment
  and activation of the AKT kinase. Via multiple downstream effector pathways, cellular
  states of growth, proliferation, heightened metabolic activity and survival are
  promoted. For more details, see text.
