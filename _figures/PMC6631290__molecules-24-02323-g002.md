caption: Classic biosynthetic pathway of pinocembrin []. L-Phenylalanine as a precursor
  compound produces cinnamic acid under the action of phenylalanine ammonia lyase,
  which generates cinnamoyl-CoA under the action of CoA ligase, and adds malonyl-CoA
  to the reaction system to form pinocembrin chalcone under the action of chalcone
  synthase. Finally, pinocembrin is generated under the action of chalcone isomerase.
