  immune balance. In the cases of tumor, overexpressed PD-Ls activate PD-1 signaling,
  leading to exhaustion of effector T cell after interaction of PD-1 and PD-Ls. AKT:
  v-akt murine thymoma viral oncogene homolog; APC: antigen presenting cell; CD: cluster

  B; PD-1: programmed death-1; PD-L: programmed death-ligand; PI3K: phosphoinositide-3-kinase;

  TCR: T-cell receptor; ZAP70: zeta chain of T cell receptor associated protein kinase
  70.'
