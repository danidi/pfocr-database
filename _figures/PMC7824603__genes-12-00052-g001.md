caption: An integrated model of the molecular pathway underlying light-mediated seed
  germination. Updated model of the phyB-PIF1 signaling pathway regulating the seed
  germination process. PIF1 directly activates GAI and RVE1 expression, and in turn
  RVE1 directly induces PIF1. RVE1 interacts with the DELLA protein RGL2 to repress
  GA3ox2, while GAI interacts with DAG1 to repress GA3ox1. The transcription factors
  VOZ1 and VOZ2 also repress GA3ox1, downstream PIF1. Dotted arrow, indirect control;
  full arrow, direct control; Pr, phyB inactive form. Pfr, phyB active form.
