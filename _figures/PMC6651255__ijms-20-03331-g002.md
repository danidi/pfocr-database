  silencing complex (RISC) activation. Chondroitin sulfate (CS)-E is a matrix glycosaminoglycan

  D-glucuronic acid (GlcA) and N-acetyl-D-galactosamine (GalNAc), whose 4- and 6-positions
  are sulfated. Carbohydrate sulfotransferase 15 (CHST15) is a type-2 transmembrane
  Golgi protein that transfers sulfate to position 6 of GalNAc (4SO4) residues of
  CS-A to yield CS-E.
