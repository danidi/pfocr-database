caption: 'Melanocyte differentiation pathway dependent on MSHα and ACTH. MSHα and
  ACTH can both bind to MC2R. MC2R is coupled to G-protein α-s, which stimulates adenylate
  cyclase type I, increasing production of cAMP, hence activating PKA. PKA phosphorylates
  CREB1, which in turn activates the expression of MITF. MITF regulates transcription
  of genes coding mitochondrial ribosomal proteins through interactions with TYRP1
  and MLANA.Abbreviations: ACTH, adrenocorticotropic hormone; AMP, adenosine monophosphate;
  CREB1, cAMP response element-binding protein 1; MC2R, melanocortin 2 receptor; MITF,
  microphthalmia-associated transcription factor; MLANA, melan-A; MSHα, melanocyte-stimulating
  hormone α; PKA, cAMP-dependent protein kinase; TYRP1, tyrosinase-related protein
  1.'
