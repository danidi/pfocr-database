  potential mechanism of apoptosis induced by CTS in HCCC-9810 and RBE cells. Sustained

  contribute to apoptosis progression in CCA cells. *According to Yu and Jove and
  Rane and Reddy, JAKs play an essential role in mediating the activation of both

  PARP, poly (ADP-ribose) polymerase.'
