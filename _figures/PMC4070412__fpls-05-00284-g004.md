  shikimate/quinate hydroxycinnamoyl transferase; C3H, 4-coumarate 3 –hydroxylase;
  COMT, caffeic acid O-methyltransferase; CCoAOMT, Caffeoyl CoA O-methyltransferase;
  F5H, ferulate 5-hydroxylase; CAD, cinnamyl alcohol dehydrogenase; POX, peroxidase;
  LACC, laccase). Steps in phenylpropanoid and ligin pathways that give rise to other
  secondary metabolism products are indicated.
