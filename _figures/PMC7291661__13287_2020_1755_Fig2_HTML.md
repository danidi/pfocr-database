caption: Schematic diagram of proinflammatory cytokines regulating keratinocytes or
  stem cells. Keratinocytes, neutrophils, and macrophages produce IL-1, which regulates
  stem cells through the caspase 8 signaling pathway. TNF-α binds to TNFR1 to induce
  AKT phosphorylation in iSCs or to TNFR2 to activate the NF-κB signaling pathway.
  Neutrophils and macrophages produce TWEAK, which binds to Fn14, and they have a
  potential effect on iSCs. IL-6 and IL-17 activate the STAT-JAK and Act1-TRAF4-MEKK3-ERK5
  signaling pathways, respectively
