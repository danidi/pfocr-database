caption: Diagram of the role of GSK3β in skin tumorigenesis. EGF and TPA activate
  PI3K/Akt and PKC signaling pathways which induce phosphorylation of GSK3β(Ser9)
  and inactivate GSK3β. Inactivation of GSK3β stimulates oncogenic transcription factor
  AP-1. Hyperactivity of AP-1 promotes skin tumorigenesis. EGF and TPA can also activate
  AP-1 through the MEK1/ERK pathway which is independent of GSK3β.
