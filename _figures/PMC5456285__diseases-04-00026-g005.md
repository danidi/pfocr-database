  as TLRs, RIG-I and MDA5 are activated to trigger a series of signalling pathway,

  the JAK-STAT signalling pathway to induce ISGs. The yellow-orange boxes show the
  viral proteins that have been reported to modulate host innate immunity at multiple
  stages.
