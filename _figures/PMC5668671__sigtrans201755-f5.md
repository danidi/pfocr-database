caption: HPVs infect host epithelial cells through TLRs. The HPVs DNA viral replication
  is synchronous with host cellular DNA replication. The cells express E6/E7/E5 oncoproteins
  to damage the infected cells by inhibiting the expression of tumor suppressors p53
  and Rb and decreasing apoptosis. E6/E7/E5 oncoproteins can also activate the PI3K/Akt/mTOR
  signaling pathway. All these processes enhance cell proliferation and promote tumor
  growth.
