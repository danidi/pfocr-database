caption: 'Core analysis of genes differentially regulated by SP-D nitrosylation. Network
  of genes differentially regulated in RAW cells exposed to SNO-SP-D OE-BAL (A) or
  SNO-SP-D OE-BAL + LPS (B) and involved in inflammation of organ pathway. Solid lines
  indicate direct regulation while dashed lines indicate indirect regulation. Green
  indicates downregulation, red indicates upregulation (fold change > 2). C: expanded
  list of upstream regulators and diseases or functions significantly enriched among
  differentially expressed genes in data set. Selected upstream regulators were among
  the most significantly enriched in data set. Diseases or functions were limited
  to those including at least 11 molecules in data set and relevant to inflammatory
  signaling. BAL, bronchoalveolar lavage fluid; CCL2, chemokine (C-C motif) ligand
  2; CCL20, chemokine (C-C motif) ligand 20; CXCL9 chemokine (C-X-C motif) ligand
  9; FASLG, Fas ligand G; GADD45A, growth arrest and DNA damage-inducible 45α; IRF1,
  interferon regulatory factor 1; MMP7, matrix metalloproteinase 7; MMP10, matrix
  metalloproteinase 10; N/A, not significantly represented within data set; Nos2,
  nitric oxide synthase 2; OE, overexpressing; PTGS2, prostaglandin-endoperoxide synthase
  2; SELP, selectin, platelet; SNO, S-nitrosothiol; SP-D, surfactant protein-D; VCAM1,
  vascular cell adhesion molecule 1.'
