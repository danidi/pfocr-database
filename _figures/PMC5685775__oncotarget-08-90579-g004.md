caption: PPAR γ agonists activate Smad7, an inhibitor of Smads pathway. PPAR γ agonists
  activate PTEN, which inhibits both PI3K/Akt pathway and TGF-β1 signaling. PPAR γ
  agonists activate DKK and Smad7, two inhibitors of WNT/beta-catenin pathway leading
  to the β-catenin degradation in the proteasome. PPAR γ agonists activate GSK-3β
  activity, which forms the destruction complex to degrade β-catenin. In contrast,
  presence of WNT ligands inhibits GSK-3β and lead to cytosolic accumulation of β-catenin.
  Then nuclear β-catenin associates with TCF/LEF in the nucleus to inhibit PPAR γ
  expression.
