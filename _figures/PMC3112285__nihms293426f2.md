caption: 'Pathway Crosstalk. The Ras-MAPK and PI3K-mTORC1 pathways regulate each other
  via cross-inhibition (red) and cross-activation (green). Each pathway has a mechanism
  to negatively feed onto the other: ERK phosphorylation of GAB and AKT phosphorylation
  of Raf. Components of the Ras-ERK pathway (Ras, Raf, ERK, and RSK) also positively
  regulate the PI3K-mTORC1 pathway. TSC2 and mTORC1 are key integration points that
  receive many inputs from both the Ras-ERK and PI3K signaling. Positive regulation
  of the substrate protein is shown as an arrow. Negative regulation of the substrate
  protein is depicted as a blunt-ended line.'
