  Tg-B1 mice leading to accelerated hearing loss. The aberrant activation of AMPK

  knockouts of the AMPK pathway. The conjectured downregulation of AMPK could attenuate
  the apoptotic signaling and ROS accumulation in auditory cells, which accounts for
  rescue of hearing loss in Tg-B1 mice.
