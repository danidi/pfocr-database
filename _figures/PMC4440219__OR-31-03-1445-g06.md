caption: Model of miR-503 mediates constitutive suppression of the IGF-1R pathway
  leading to a less aggressive glioma phenotype. The IGF-1R/AKT pathway is a core
  signaling pathway in glioblastoma oncogenesis. miR-503 influenced AKT pathway by
  targeting IGF-1R, and consequently inhibits cell growth, cell cycle progression,
  cell migration and invasion, and induces cell apoptosis. Among them, AKT may regulate
  cell cycle progression by P21/CCND1, influence cellular invasiveness by MMP-9, affect
  apoptosis by caspase-3 and Bcl-2. In addition to directly target IGF-1R, miR-503
  may also influence GBM pathogenesis by directly regulating Bcl-2, CDC25, CCND1 and
  CCNE1 according to previous research. miR-503, microRNA-503; IGF-1R, insulin-like
  growth factor-1; GBM, glioblastoma multiforme.
