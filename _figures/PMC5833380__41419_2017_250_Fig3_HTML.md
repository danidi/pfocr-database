  expression in ROSHigh cells. (Right panel) NRF2 mediated metabolic genes, involved

  in the oxidative PPP and glutamine pathway in ROSHigh cells (Group B). (Lower left
  panel) Summary of NRF2 regulating distinct metabolic pathways in ROSLow cells (quiescent
  CICs) and ROSHigh cells (proliferating CICs). b ChIP-Seq tracks of NRF2 in SAS sphere
  cells, assessing the binding of glycolysis and PPP. Location of ChIP-Seq peaks that
  were identified by using Strand NGS software. RNA-Seq traces (UCSC genome browser)
  for each gene in ROSLow cells and ROSHigh cells
