caption: Schematic illustration of CCh-induced endogenous TGF-β1 signaling loop in
  COPD lung fibroblasts. Carbachol (CCh) induces ERK5 activation directly or indirectly
  through stimulating endogenous transforming growth factor-β1 (TGF-β1) release. Glycopyrronium
  (GLY) directly blocks CCh binding to its receptor, while indacaterol (IND) interrupts
  CCh signaling pathway or TGF-β1-induced ERK5 activation. Through these mechanisms,
  GLY plus IND blocked CCh- or TGF-β1-augmented collagen gel contraction and α-SMA
  expression
