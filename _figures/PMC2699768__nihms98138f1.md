caption: Activation of the AhR pathway alters cellular adhesion in a variety of model
  systems. (1) AhR pathway alters the expression of ECM proteins, including types
  I and IV collagen and fibronectin in fetal heart and marmoset myocardium and thymocytes
  (–). (2) Expression of several integrin subtypes, including the β7 subtype is altered
  following AhR signaling in cytotoxic T-cells (), thymic epithelial cells (), macrophages
  (), T-lymphocytes () and human amniotic epithelial cells (). (3) Focal adhesion
  kinase (FAK) phosphorylation in HUVECs (). (4) Over-expression of β-catenin results
  in increased AhR expression in prostate cancer cell lines (). (5) Expression of
  T-cadherin is decreased following TCDD exposure in smooth muscle cells in culture
  and murine fetal hearts (,). (6) AhR-activation results in the loss of γ-catenin
  associated with the adherence complex in rat liver epithelial cells ().
