  a canonical estrogen receptor binding element (ERE), with a FOXA1 binding site downstream
  of ERE; (B) When ERα is bound by its ligand it drives the expression of HIF-1α.
  However, ERα antagonists block the expression of HIF-1α; (C) The pathways mediated
  by hypoxia, estrogen, metabolites, and cancer genes converge on HIF-1α, which drives
  a plethora of genes that are involved in multiple biological processes, cancer progression,
  and therapeutic resistance.
