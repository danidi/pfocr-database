caption: A number of transcriptional factors positively or negatively regulate ARF
  transcription. Smads, DMP1α, E2Fs, MYC, and FoxO activate ARF transcription. E2F3b,
  enhancer of zeste homolog 2 (Ezh2)/Twist-1, chromobox protein homolog 7 (CBX7),
  T-box transcription factor 2 (TBX2), B-cell-specific Moloney murine leukemia virus
  integration site 1 (BMI-1), and epidermal growth factor receptor (EGFR) directly
  binds to the ARF promoter and suppress ARF transcription. DMP1β interacts with DMP1α,
  subsequently blocking the binding of DMP1α to the ARF promoter. Furthermore, the
  transforming growth factor beta 1 (TGFβ1) signaling pathway negatively regulates
  ARF transcription by inhibiting E2F1 expression.
