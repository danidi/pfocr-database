caption: Thermodynamic and kinetic framework of P4-P6 folding (5 mM MgCl2, 100 mM
  KCl, 23 °C). The preferred folding pathway is depicted with the green arrows. Values
  for each step are taken from results in , , and . The value in the square bracket
  for F→IMC/MCR is the average of the unfolding rate constants determined for constructs
  that isolate the TL/TLR (). The value in the square bracket for F → MITL/TLR is
  the average of unfolding rate constants for constructs that isolate the MC/MCR (P4-P6,
  MU⇌IMC/MCR) (); (MC/MCRiso, 5 mM Mg2+) (). The value for AltP5cITL/TLR→AltP5cU was
  corrected by a factor of two to account for the uniform twofold slower unfolding
  in 5 mM Ba2+ relative to 5 mM Mg2+ ( and ) because the experiment was carried out
  with Ba2+ to prevent Mg2+ occupancy of the metal core (MC) (). Values in parentheses
  were calculated from completion of the thermodynamic cycle ().
