caption: Notch signaling modules relevant for brain tumors. Notch signaling has been
  shown to be modulated on multiple levels in glioma cells and is linked upstream
  and downstream to other tumorigenic pathways. Its expression is induced by Ras and
  Akt, while Notch itself induces expression of epidermal growth factor (EGFR) via
  p53 (indirectly; hence dashed arrow) and pro-migratory glycoprotein tenascin C (TNC).
  Hypoxia-inducible factor 1α (HIF-1α) and HIF-2α compete for NICD binding. The Notch
  inhibitor HIF-2α is displaced by HIF-1α under hypoxic conditions. Further, several
  proteins modulate the Notch pathway at the level of NICD. Examples are Numb, which
  promotes Notch degradation via ubiquitin ligases such as FBW7 or Itch, or Numb4d7
  and ligand of Notch protein X (LNX), which stimulate Notch signaling.
