caption: An integrated model of the TLR signaling pathway modulated by the multifurcating
  motif of poeModules M14-M18. Shown is the TLR/RLR signaling pathways in response
  to the poly I:C, PAM and LPS pathogenic-like ligands. Transcriptional regulation
  is shown as dashed lines. Each associated trait in poeModules M14-M18 is accompanied
  with the gene name, the relevant stimulus (*poly IC;†PAM), and a rectangle that
  is color coded with its primary (left) and secondary (right) underlying eQTLs. The
  plot suggests the existence of a single general (secondary) eQTL that acts pleiotropically
  on the TLR signaling network while cooperating with several specific (primary) eQTLs
  to control the expression of particular genes within this network.
