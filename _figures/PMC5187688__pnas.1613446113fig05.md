  lines. (B) The TCA pathway, the proposed biologically relevant TCA process. Insertion

  the WT in both (C) continuous light and (D) cycles of alternating light–dark (12–12
  h). Colony area was measured using ImageJ (). Reaction and metabolite abbreviations
  are given in BiGG format (bigg.ucsd.edu) and found in . ***Significance level of
  0.001 (t test).
