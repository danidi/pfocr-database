  LOXs, NOXs and increased labile iron result in lipid peroxide. Cysteine can be generated
  from uptake of cystine via system Xc‐ or transsulphuration pathway. GPx4 catalyses
  reduction reaction at the cost of GSH. CoQ10H2/α‐TOH traps lipid peroxyl radicals
  and FSP1 catalyses the regeneration of CoQ10
