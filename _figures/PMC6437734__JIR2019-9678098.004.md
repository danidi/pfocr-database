  to DC differentiation after being stimulated with IL-4 and GM-CSF, by affecting
  RelB translocation (a noncanonic NF-κB subunit) into the nucleus, which contributes
  to the reduction in the expression of diverse characteristic surface molecules from
  immature DCs, including costimulation (CD80, CD83, and CD86), adhesion (ICAM-1/CD54
  and DC-SIGN), MHC-II (HLA-DR), and CD1 isoform (a, b, and c) molecules. Green arrows
  indicate the processes, molecules, or mediators in the signaling pathway that are
  augmented and/or promoted by VPA. Red arrows indicate processes, molecules, or mediators
  in the signaling pathway that are inhibited by VPA.
