caption: Tandem nadA motif domains are found upstream of genes in the biosynthetic
  pathway for NAD+. (A) The chemical structure of NAD+. (B) Biosynthetic pathway of
  NAD+ and related molecules (; ). Note that all nadA motif representatives are found
  exclusively upstream of nadA genes. (l-Asp) l-aspartate, (IA) iminoaspartate, (QA)
  quinolinic acid, (NaMN) nicotinic acid mononucleotide, (NaAD) nicotinic acid adenine
  dinucleotide, (NMN) nicotinamide mononucleotide, (NADP+) nicotinamide adenosine
  dinucleotide phosphate, (Na) nicotinic acid, (Nam) nicotinamide. Riboside derivatives
  carry a ribose moiety linked by an N-glycosidic linkage to the nicotinic or nicotinamide
  ring nitrogen atom. (C) Consensus sequence and secondary structure model for 101
  unique examples of tandem nadA motif RNAs. The putative ribosome binding site (RBS)
  is followed by a predicted start codon for the adjacent nadA open reading frame
  (ORF).
