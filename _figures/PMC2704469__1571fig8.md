  signaling to inhibit the transition from premyelinating to mature oligodendrocyte.

  acting to promote differentiation. HYCCIN, a Wnt-repressed target () with essential
  roles in human myelination (), is expressed in rodent oligodendrocytes and down-regulated
  in Olig2cre/DA-Cat mice (Supplemental Fig. 8). Temporal expression of Tcf4 and markers
  of oligodendrocyte lineage tested in this study is shown. (Pre OL) Premyelinating
  oligodendrocyte; (OL) oligodendrocyte.
