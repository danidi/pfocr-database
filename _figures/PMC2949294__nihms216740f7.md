caption: Proposed model of IL-1 signaling in prostate. IL-1 family members expressed
  in the epithelium during prostate development or either epithelium or leukocytes
  during inflammation signal to the stroma where IL-1R1 is present. Stimulation of
  IL-1R1 activates Jak-STAT signaling, leading to the expression of genes, including
  (but not limited to) IGF. IGF signals to the epithelium in both developing and hyperplastic
  prostates, resulting in PI3K-Akt pathway activation, epithelial proliferation, and
  tissue expansion.
