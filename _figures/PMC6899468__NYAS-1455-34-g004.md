caption: Schematic diagram showing the putative signaling pathways for the anti‐inflammatory
  actions of CST. Since CST decreases plasma TNF‐α levels, it is expected that less
  TNF‐α will be available for binding to its receptor (TNFR1), resulting in the attenuation
  of the IκB–NF‐κB signaling pathway leading to the translocation of fewer NF‐κB to
  the nucleus. This reduced NF‐κB signaling will affect the formation of NLRP3 inflammasome
  resulting in less cleavage procaspase 1 to active caspase 1 leading to less conversion
  of pro–IL‐1β and pro–IL‐18 to IL‐1β and IL‐18 and secretion of IL‐1β and IL‐18.
