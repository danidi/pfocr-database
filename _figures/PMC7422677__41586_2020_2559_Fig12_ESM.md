caption: 'a–c, Two-dimensional projection coordinates generated using t-SNE on all
  genes significantly associated with a DHS component and shown selectively for subsets
  of gene categories, namely transcription factors (TFs; diamonds: ZNF TF genes) (a),
  lincRNA genes (b) and pseudo-genes (c). Indicated are the number of labelled genes
  in each combination of gene category and DHS component. Examples of labelled genes
  are shown as follows. a, Regulatory landscape of ZNF331; a poorly annotated zinc-finger
  (ZNF) TF gene (lymphoid and placental components). b, Regulatory landscape of BANCR;
  a long intergenic non-coding RNA (lincRNA) gene, recently associated with cardiomyocyte
  migration. c, Regulatory landscape of the pseudo-gene IGHGP (lymphoid component).
  d, DHS component labelling of MSigDB canonical pathways, through the regulatory
  landscapes of constituent genes. Shown are pathways with a significant association
  (5% FDR) and an observed/expected ratio of at least 2. The most strongly associated
  components are indicated for each pathway, with their source databases. e, Examples
  of three component-associated pathways from the KEGG database, with genes coloured
  according to their majority component.'
