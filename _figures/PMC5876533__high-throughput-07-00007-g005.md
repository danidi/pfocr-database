  abundant metabolites are shown in cyan, and less abundant metabolites are shown
  in red; (b) representation of relative contents of marker compounds for starch and
  sucrose metabolism (sucrose) and the pentose phosphate pathway (gluconate). CoA:

  ThPP: Thiamine pyrophosphate; P: Phosphate; UDP: Uridine diphosphate.'
