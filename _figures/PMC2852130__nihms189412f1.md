caption: 'Schematic representation of the major pathways of ganglioside biosynthesis.
  GM3, derived from lactosylceramide, is the common precursor for both “a” and “b”
  pathway gangliosides. Each ganglioside consists of a ceramide backbone (CER) and
  a carbohydrate chain (glc, glucose; gal, galactose; GalNAc, N-acetylgalactosamine)
  containing one or more sialic acid (SA, sialic acid) residues. Parallel steps in
  both pathways are catalyzed by the same glycosyltransferases: (1) glucosylceramide
  synthase; (2) lactosylceramide synthase; (3) GM3 synthase; (4)GD3 synthase; (5)GM2/GD2
  synthase; (6)GD1b/GM1a synthase; (7)GT1b/GD1a synthase; (8) GQ1b/GT1a synthase.'
