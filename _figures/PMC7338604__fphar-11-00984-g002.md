caption: Natural products targeting the β-catenin signaling pathway for cancer therapy.
  Many natural products have been discovered to inhibit the β-catenin signaling pathway
  through (1) down-regulating β-catenin expression, (2) modulating β-catenin phosphorylation,
  (3) promoting β-catenin protein degradation, (4) inhibiting β-catenin nuclear translocation,
  and other mechanisms of action. 20(S)-Rh2, 20(S)-ginsenoside Rh2; 4β-HWE, 4β-Hydroxywithanolide
  E; AEUT, alkaloid-enriched extract of Uncaria tomentosa; APC, adenomatous polyposis

  GS25, 20(S)-25-OCH3-PPD; GSK3β, glycogen synthase kinase 3β; LRP, low-density lipoprotein
  receptor-related protein; PEITC, phenethyl isothiocyanate; PPL, polysaccharides
  from Phellinus linteus; TCF/LEF, T cell factor/lymphocyte enhancer factor; TPPE,
  Teucrium polium plant extract.
