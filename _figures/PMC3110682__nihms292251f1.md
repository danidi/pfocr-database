caption: Hypothesized mechanisms of physical activity in colon cancer. Physical activity
  may block inflammatory pathways, resulting in downregulating nuclear factor kappa
  B (NF-κB)/AKT signaling, decreased development of oxidative species, and less DNA
  damage. Physical activity may also affect the insulin-like growth factor (IGF) pathway
  and AMP kinase signaling, resulting in lower levels of cell proliferation and increased
  apoptosis. The effect of physical activity on cycloxygenase-2 (COX-2) and prostaglandin
  E2 (PGE2) levels is unclear (–, ). IGFBP, IGF binding protein; iNOS, inducible nitric
  oxide synthase; APC, adenomatous polyposis coli.
