  differential expression in the T0 (fasting) vs T2 (7 h after eating) comparisons.

  parentheses represent the source database as follows: R – Reactome, K – KEGG, N
  – NCI PID, P - Panther, and B – BioCarta. Enriched pathways (q-value <0.05) in each
  one of the individual network modules are: 1: Mitotic G1-G1/S phases (R); 2: Nicotinic

  protein targeting to membrane (R); 4: Senescence-associated secretory phenotype

  pathway (N), 7: Oxidative stress induced gene expression via Nrf2 (B); 8: ABC-family

  tubule bicarbonate reclamation (K); 12: Wnt signaling pathway (K); 13: Nucleotide-binding

  ATF-2 transcription factor network (N); 15: ECM-receptor interaction (K); 16: GPCR

  (P); 19: Myogenesis (R); 20: Transcriptional regulation of white adipocyte differentiation
