  NH3, ammonia; CP, carbamoylphosphate; NAG, N‐acetylglutamate; NO, nitric oxide;
  OAT, ornithine aminotransferase; CPS1, carbamoylphosphate synthetase I; NAGS, N‐acetylglutamate

  argininosuccinate lyase; NOS, nitric oxide synthase; ARG1 and ARG2, arginase I and
  II, respectively. Enzymes within the grayed area are mitochondrial and the rest
  cytosolic. Whereas CPS1, NAGS, and OTC are only present in enterocytes (and hepatocytes),
  OAT, ASS1, and ASL are widely expressed in other tissues (mainly in kidney)
