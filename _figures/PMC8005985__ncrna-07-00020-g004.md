caption: Regulatory effect of H19-LncRNA in cardiomyocyte maturation. (A) H19 regulates
  the cardiomyocyte maturation process at different steps, including apoptosis, proliferation,
  hypertrophy and contraction. A. H19 physically interacts with PRC2 to suppress pro-hypertrophic
  NFAT signaling. (B) H19 regulates the cardiac ca2+ flux and cardiac contraction
  function through the H19-miR-675-CamkIIδ axis. (C,D) The regulatory function of
  H19 lncRNA in mitochondrial apoptotic pathway and cardiomyocyte proliferation is
  mediated through either H19-miR-675-Vdac1 (C) or H19-miR-19b-Sox6 (D) axis. More
  details are described within the text.
