caption: Activation of NF-κB and STAT signaling pathway. LPS-binding protein (LBP)
  transfers LPS to the CD14 receptor on the cell membrane. Binding LPS activates NF-κB
  or AP-1. The IκB-NF-κB complex is inactive in the cytosol. After activation of the
  IκB/NF-κB complex, free NF-κB transfers into the nucleus and induces the expression
  of iNOS. IFNγ binds to IFNγR and phosphorylates STAT1. Activated STAT1 translocates
  into the nucleus and increases iNOS induction and NO production [].
