caption: Increased expression of ABCA8 in PC cells fasilitates the efflux of TCA,
  one conjugated bile acid. Extracellular TCA activates the ERK signaling via the
  transmembrane receptor S1PR2. The activation of ERK signaling regulates the expressions
  of the apoptosis-related proteins BAX and BCL2, and then leads to the insensitivity
  of PC cells to the anticancer drug GEM. Either blockade of S1PR2 by JTE-013 or blockade
  of ERK signaling by SCH772984 can inhibit the ABCA8-mediated GEM resistance of PC
  cells.
