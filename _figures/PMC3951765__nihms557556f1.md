caption: CD36 and the TLR4-TLR6 heterodimer recognize oxLDL, which accumulates in
  atherosclerotic lesions. This interaction initiates a signaling pathway that leads
  to transcriptional upregulation of NLRP3 and pro-IL-1β (signal 1). CD36 also mediates
  internalization of oxLDL into the lysosomal compartment, where the ligands are converted
  into crystals that induce lysosomal rupture and NLRP3 inflammasome activation (signal
  2).
