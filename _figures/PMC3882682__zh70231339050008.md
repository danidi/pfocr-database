caption: 'Canonical pathways. A: the PI3K signaling pathway was the most significantly
  affected canonical pathway in terms of H3K4me3 occupancy and RNA transcription.
  PI3K signaling activates cell survival, cell growth, and cell cycle and contributes
  to colon carcinogenesis. Orange highlights indicate the differential expression
  of genes enriched at the H3K4 trimethylation and mRNA levels. Red highlighted genes
  represent overexpression (distal > proximal) and green represents underexpression
  in the distal colon at the RNA level. PI3K-related genes differentially regulated
  solely at the H3K4me3 level are highlighted in pink and yellow, representing up-
  and downregulated genes, respectively. B: Venn diagram showing the overlap between
  canonical pathways linked to changes in H3K4me3 occupancy and RNA transcription.'
