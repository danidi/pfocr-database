  to the deficiency of ferrochelatase, the last enzyme of the heme-biosynthetic pathway
  resulting in the accumulation of protoporphyrin. In X-linked protoporphyria (XLP),
  a gain-of-function mutation in ALAS2 results in more protoporphyrin than is required
  for hemoglobinization and it accumulates despite the normal ferrochelatase activity.
