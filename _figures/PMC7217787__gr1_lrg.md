  Zoom Binding SARS-CoV-2 to TLR Activation of NFkB signaling pathway Activation of

  Macrophages, Monocytes, PMNs recruitment and cytokines release. SASP, Pyroptosis,

  of CYTOKINE STORM CD8+ T lymphocytes senescence under cytokine storm and extensive
  SARS-CoV-2 replication : Cytokine storm increases the numbers of STC expressing
  the senescent marker PD-1 PD-1+ STC become unable to secrete IFN-γ, Perforin, and
  Granzyme, and eventually kill the infected cell ⑦ Kinetics of SARS-CoV-2 infection
  and window of opportunity for rapamycin treatment Abbreviations: DAMPs: Damage Associated
