caption: H. pylori stimulated synthesis and metabolism of polyamines in macrophages.
  H. pylori stimulates expression of iNOS, arginase 2, ODC, and SMO. High output iNOS-derived
  NO kills H. pylori. Polyamines are synthesized through the arginase 2/ODC metabolic
  pathway. Spermine inhibits iNOS translation. The back-conversion of spermine to
  spermidine by SMO releases H2O2 that induces macrophage apoptosis.
