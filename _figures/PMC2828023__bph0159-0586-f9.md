caption: Possible mechanisms for ganglioside-induced autophagic cell death in astrocytes.
  Gangliosides, via lipid rafts, initiate autophagic cell death signalling cascades
  in astrocytes. The ERK pathway and class III PI3K/beclin-1 complex may promote autophagic
  cell death in astrocytes. In contrast, Akt/mTOR pathway inhibits autophagic cell
  death. ROS appear to be important mediators of ganglioside-induced astrocyte cell
  death. The data obtained with various pharmacological inhibitors and siRNAs support
  the signalling pathways shown here. 3-MA, 3-methyladenine; DPI, diphenyleneiodonium;
  LC3, microtubule-associated protein 1 light chain 3; MβCD, methyl β-cyclodextrin;
  mTOR, mammalian target of rapamycin; PI3K, phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase; ROS, reactive
  oxygen species.
