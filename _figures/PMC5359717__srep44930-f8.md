caption: In hASM (left panel), NE activates rapidly the non-canonical NIK/IKK1/RelB
  axis. Nuclear translocated RelB binds to the κB site in the NKRF promoter, transactivates
  and produces NKRF. Although NE stimulates the canonical IKK2/p65 pathway, the inducible
  NKRF bound at the IL-8/CXCL8 promoter abolishes the transcriptional activity of
  p65 NF-κB, leading to the suppression of IL-8/CXCL8. In A549 cells (right panel),
  a representative respiratory epithelial cell line, NE only stimulates the canonical
  NF-κB pathway. In the absence of inducible NKRF, p65 binds to the IL-8/CXCL8 promoter
  and transactivates it, leading to the enhanced production of IL-8/CXCL8.
