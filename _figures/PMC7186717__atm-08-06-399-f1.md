  can be recruited to tumour microenvironment where they differentiate into TAMs with
  various phenotypes ranging from the M1 phenotype to the alternative M2 phenotype.

  TGF-β, IL-4, Arg-1 and IL-10 to regulate the degree of immune response and boost
  tumour formation. TAMs, tumour-associated macrophages.'
