  their products. Colored squares next to each gene represent the log2fold change

  the number of indicators of a significant phylogenetic signal (out of five indicators)
  between the fungal phylogeny and the transcription of a given cassava gene. Genes
  that were shown to play a role, or putatively play a role, in fatty acid biosynthesis
  are represented. For a description of each gene acronym see Data .
