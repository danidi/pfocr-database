  of PERK pathway after ICH. Following ICH caused by autologous whole blood or other
  blood components (especially Fe3+ and Fe2+), the activation of ER stress activates
  the PERK pathway and increases the protein amounts of p-PERK, p-eIF2α and ATF4.
  The activation of PERK pathway blocks the initiation process of translation and
  synthesis of protein, leading to neuronal apoptosis.
