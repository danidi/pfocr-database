  of mTOR signaling pathway. The intake of Bc probably suppresses mTOR (mTOR1 and
  mTOR2) activation that leads to decreased phosphorylation of p70S6K, 4E-BP1, STAT3,

  down-regulation of transcription factor GATA-3. These events, in turn, facilitate

  responses caused by ST.
