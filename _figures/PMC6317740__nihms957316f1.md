caption: In canonical Wnt pathway, DVL promotes clustering of Wnt-LRP5/6-Frizzled
  to form signalosomes, which results in phosphorylation of LRP5/6 and recruitment
  of Axin to the plasma membrane, further stabilizing the β–catenin levels in the
  cytoplasm. DVL has also been found to shuttle between the cytoplasm and the nucleus,
  acting as a transcriptional activator of Wnt target genes. In non-canonical pathway,
  Wnt-Frizzled complex interacts with DVL which relays signal to downstream effectors.
  Multiple pathways downstream of DVL regulate gene transcription, polarity and actin
  cytoskeleton remodeling of a cell.
