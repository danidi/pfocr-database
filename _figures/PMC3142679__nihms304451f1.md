  in reactivation of different viruses are depicted. The Raf/MEK/ERK pathway of signaling

  a variety of transcription factors that are critical for the transcription of specific
  cellular genes and in inducing viral reactivation. Signaling molecules that are
  boxed in blue are critical upstream components in the divergent MAPK and NF-κB signaling
  pathways. These molecules can be activated by one or more of the ligands listed
  above.
