  includes α-secretase cleavage, producing sAPPα and αCTF, which mediate neurite outgrowth,

  γ-secretase, and caspase produces sAPPβ, Aβ, Jcasp, and C31, mediating neurite (Nt)
  retraction, synaptic loss, caspase activation, and potentially cell death (B) (Descamps
  et al JAD 2011;25:51-57).
