caption: FcγR-mediated phagocytosis pathway in the KEGG database is the putative pathway
  which is related to TNF-α and TGF-β1 expression in microglial cells. The green box
  represents the altered genes of the FcγR-mediated phagocytosis pathway in the geniposide-treated
  group. The red boxes represent the altered genes of the FcγR-mediated phagocytosis
  pathway in the ginsenoside-Rg1-treated group. The blue boxes represent the altered
  genes of the FcγR-mediated phagocytosis pathway in the combination-treated group.
  The purple box represents the overlapping altered genes of the FcγR-mediated phagocytosis
  pathway in the geniposide- and ginsenoside-Rg1-treated groups. The orange box represents
  the overlapping altered genes of the FcγR-mediated phagocytosis pathway among the
  3 drug groups.
