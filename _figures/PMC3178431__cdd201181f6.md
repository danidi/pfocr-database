caption: p63 signaling in cancer cells. Opposing effects of ΔNp63 and β-catenin in
  cancer cells. In normal cells (a), p53 facilitates the degradation of ΔNp63 and
  seems to activate GSK3β, causing degradation of β-catenin. In squamous cell lung
  cancer (b), mutant p53 fails to downregulate ΔNp63, which binds B56α, inhibiting
  GSKβ and altering the β-catenin APC-binding complex, with accumulation of non-phosphorylated
  β-catenin. (c) STAT3/p63 regulate Notch signaling downstream of TORC1 to affect
  the molecular switch of differentiation versus proliferation. NF-kB counteracts
  this pathway
