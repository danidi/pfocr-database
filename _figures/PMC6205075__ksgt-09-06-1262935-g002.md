caption: Integration of oncogenic KRAS signaling by MYC in PDECs. GTP-bound oncogenic
  RAS induces several effector pathways. Depicted are the pathways with clear and
  definite impact on the carcinogenesis in the pancreas. Canonical signaling leads
  to activation of the RAF - (mitogen-activated kinase/ERK kinase) MEK - extracellular
  regulated kinase (ERK) protein kinase signaling pathway. Non-canonical signaling
  initiates the phosphoinositide 3-kinase (PI3Ks)-AKT-glycogen synthase kinase 3 (GSK3)
  cascade. ERK can phosphorylate MYC at serine 62 (S62), contributing to its stabilization
  and activity. AKT-mediated inhibitory phosphorylation of GSK3 impairs MYC threonine
  58 (T58) phosphorylation and degradation. The KRASmut induced EGFR-loop increases
  MYC mRNA expression. The function of MYC to license growth and cell cycle entry
  is depicted.
