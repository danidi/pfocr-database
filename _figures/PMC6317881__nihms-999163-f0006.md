  Vitamin D–responsive genes (Gjb2, Mmp13, Mmp17, Efemp1, and Pdlim2) are shown interacting

  CDH2 (N-cadherin), VIM, ACP5 (tartrate-resistant acid phosphatase 5), BCL2 (apoptosis
  regulator), ZEB1, PCNA (proliferating cell nuclear antigen), ACTA2 (alpha-actin-2),
  MMP2, MMP9, (matrix metallopeptidase 9), MMP3, and CASP3 (caspase-3). ITG, integrins.
