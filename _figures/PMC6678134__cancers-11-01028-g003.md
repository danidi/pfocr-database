  ER+ve breast cancers are treated with hormone therapy (HT) (A). (B) The treatment

  (GFR)-PI3K-AKT-mTOR, NOTCH, or other pathways. The GFR-PI3K-AKT-mTOR pathway inhibits
  the ER and NOTCH signaling. (C) These pathways (B) drive the progression of tumors
  with resistance to endocrine therapy (ETR).
