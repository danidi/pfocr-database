caption: 'Molecular components and pathways of BMP signaling. The BMP signaling is
  initiated by the binding of BMP ligands to BMPR1 and BMPR2. In the canonical pathway,
  BMP receptors phosphorylate Smad1/5/8, which can bind to Co-Smad4 and are translocated
  to the nucleus to regulate the expression of target genes. In the non-canonical
  pathways, BMP receptors activate non-Smad pathways. Termination of BMP signaling
  is achieved by noggin, Smad6, and/or Smad7. BMP: bone morphogenic protein; BMPR:
  BMP receptor'
