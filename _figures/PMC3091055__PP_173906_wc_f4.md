  representation of the citrate cycle, with intermediate products indicated as blue
  dots and enzymes in italic type (black = represented, gray = not represented on

  8 DPI (P < 0.05) plotted using the indicated fold change color scale. Expression
  levels similar to the control are indicated in gray. HEN, Hua enhancer (RNA methyltransferase);
  RDR, RNA-dependent RNA polymerase; SDE, silencing defective; SGS, suppressor of
  gene silencing.
