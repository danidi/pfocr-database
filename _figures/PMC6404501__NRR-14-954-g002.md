caption: 'Modulation of autophagy the signaling pathway by electroacupuncture (EA)
  for treating cerebral ischemia/reperfusion (CIR) injury.EA activates class-I phosphatidylinositol
  3-kinase (PI3K) to promote the generation of phosphatidylinositol (3,4,5)-trisphosphate
  (PIP3), which can activate protein kinase B (AKT) through phosphorylating AKT at
  Thr308 and Ser473. AKT subsequently inhibits the tuberous sclerosis 1/2 (TSC1/2)
  complex, leading to the activation of Ras homolog enriched in brain (Rheb) and mammalian
  target of rapamycin (mTOR), and finally inhibiting autophagy. PIP2: Phosphatidylinositol
  (4,5)-bisphosphate.'
