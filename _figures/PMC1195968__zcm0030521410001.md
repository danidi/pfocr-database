caption: RSV binding and triggering of cellular responses. RSV is bound by surface
  glycosaminoglycans, and the F protein binds to TLR4, while G glycoprotein (virus
  associated or secreted) binds fractalkine receptor CX3CR1. The interaction with
  TLR4 leads to upregulation of NF-κB via MyD88. RSV upregulates NF-κB via IκB and
  STAT1 and -3 via reactive oxygen species (ROS), and RSV RNA activates protein kinase
  R. Viral NS proteins inhibit the interferon response factor (IRF3) pathway.
