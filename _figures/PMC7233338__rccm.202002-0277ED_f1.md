caption: NFκB (nuclear factor-κB) drives the transcription of pro-IL1β, and the NLRP3
  inflammasome controls the formation of mature IL1β. IL1β increases the expression
  of Wnt5a in the mesenchyme, leading to increased Wnt signaling in type 2 alveolar
  epithelial cells. Wnt5a expression can also be increased by several other signaling
  pathways such as Hedgehog, TGFβ, and Notch. The mechanisms leading to impaired alveolar
  development secondary to increased Wnt signaling in the pulmonary mesenchyme and
  type 2 alveolar epithelial cells still need to be elucidated.
