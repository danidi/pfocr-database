caption: In TKI‐resistant EGFR mutant cells, the Gefitinib‐untargetable palmitoylated
  EGFR positively impinges on FASN, therefore promoting tumor cell growth via the
  Akt pathway. Palmitoylation is a process where a 16‐carbon fatty acid palmitate,
  synthesized by FASN, is covalently attached to proteins. Pharmacological inhibition
  of FASN by Orlistat inhibits EGFR palmitoylation and restricts tumor cell survival.
