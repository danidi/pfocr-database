  (RTK) by a ligand results in phosphorylation of the receptor and adaptor proteins.

  phosphorylated and activated by both PIP3 and PDK1. Activated Akt modulates several
  transcription factors. (B) In breast cancer, mutations in several PI3K pathway members
  impact downstream functions, including gene expression.'
