  intake stimulates insulin and mTOR signaling, increases adipogenesis, and causes
  obesity and insulin resistance. Evodiamine stimulates EGFR-ERK signaling in preadipocytes

  mTOR-S6K signaling (red pathway) in mature adipocytes, leading to improvements of
  obesity and insulin resistance.
