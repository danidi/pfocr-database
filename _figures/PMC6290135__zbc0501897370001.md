caption: 'Polyamine biosynthetic pathway in single-celled parasitic eukaryotes. A,
  core polyamine biosynthetic pathway. The enzymes found in the various species are
  indicated by italicized species names as follows: Tb, T. brucei; Ld, L. donovani;
  Tc, T. cruzi; Pf, P. falciparum; Tg, T. gondii; Cp, C. parvum; Tv, T. vaginalis;
  Gl, G. lamblia; Eh, E. histolytica. The inset identifies enzymes that are formed
  as either bifunctional enzymes or require oligomerization with a pseudoenzyme for
  activity. Abbreviations are defined in the text with the exception of DAP, diaminopropane.
  B, trypanothione biosynthetic pathway in the trypanosomatids. T(SH)2, reduced trypanothione;
  TS2, oxidized trypanothione. Gene resource sites are as follows: http://tritrypdb.org/tritrypdb/
  (), http://plasmodb.org/plasmo/ (), http://toxodb.org/toxo/ (), http://giardiadb.org/giardiadb/,
