  in senescent preadipocytes. This schematic diagram represents how STAT1 and STAT3
  function antagonistically in regulating cGAS-STING mediated induction of CXCL10
  and interferon signaling related genes and p53/p21-dependent growth arrest. STAT1
  cooperates with cGAS/STING in driving the induction of CXCL10, and interferon signaling
  related genes such as IFNα, IFNβ, ISG15, MX and OASL2 and antagonizes the function
  of STAT3 in inhibiting p53-dependent growth arrest and cGAS-dependent induction
  of interferon signaling related genes.
