caption: A model describing the mechanism of TLRs in cancer therapy. Activation of
  p53, due to DNA stress or antitumor agents, leads to enhance TLR signaling. Activated
  TLRs induce autophagy through recruitment of MAP1S and regulation of Bcl-2/XL and
  p27 in a noncanonical pathway. TLR3 signaling requires RIP1 to activate caspase-3
  and caspase-8 and induces apoptosis. TLR5 ligand flagellin induces pyroptosis through
  introduction into cells by TLR5, and recognition by Naip5/NLRC4 to activate caspase-1.
  TLRs = toll-like receptors.
