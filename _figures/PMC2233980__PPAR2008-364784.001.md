  with the subsequent increase in apolipoprotein E (apoE) and sterol transporters
  ABCA1 and ABCG1 expression. Activation of the PPAR-LXR-ABC metabolic pathway in
  response to hexarelin favors cholesterol efflux by macrophages through high density
