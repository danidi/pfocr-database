caption: Hyaluronic acid biosynthetic pathway in S. zooepidemicus. Glucose is first
  converted by hexokinase to form glucose-6-Phosphate which then enters one of the
  two distinct pathways to form UDP-glucuronic acid (pgm, hasC and hasB) or UDP-N-acetylglucosamine
  (hasE, glmS, glmM and hasD). These precursors are then bound together via the action
  of hyaluronic acid synthase or HAS (encoded by hasA in S. zooepidemicus) to form
  hyaluronic acid
