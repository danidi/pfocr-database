  ring structure and carbon position numbering are shown at the top of the figure.

  and squalene cyclase; 2, lanosterol 14-α-demethylase; 3, 3β-hydroxysterol Δ14-reductase;
  4, C4 demethylation complex (C4-sterol methyloxidase, C4-sterol decarboxylase [NSDHL],
  and 3-ketoreductase); 5, 3β-hydroxysterol Δ8,Δ7-isomerase; 6, lathosterol 5-desaturase;
  7, 3β-hydroxysterol Δ7-reduct-ase; 8, 3β-hydroxysterol Δ24-reductase.'
