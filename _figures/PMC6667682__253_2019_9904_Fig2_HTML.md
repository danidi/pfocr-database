caption: Changes in expression of genes involved in ethanol oxidation and TCA cycle
  in K. xylinus E25. a The general pathway of ethanol metabolism and the TCA cycle
  progression. Genes are colored according to log2 fold change in expression between
  SH and SH+EtOH cultures. Stars denote statistically significant changes (called
  by DESeq2; adjusted p value ≤ 0.05). IM, inner membrane; OM, outer membrane. Mean
  expression levels (FPKM) of b subunits of cytochrome ba(3) ubiquinol oxidase (UOX);
  c subunits of NADH-quinone oxidoreductase, operon 2. Transcripts mean FPKM values
  are shown in either gray or blue, for cells grown in either the basal medium or
  the medium supplemented with EtOH, respectively. Bars represent the means from 3
  replicated cultures. Thin black bars denote standard error
