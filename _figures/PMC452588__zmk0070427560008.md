caption: Spindle assembly and orientation checkpoints in fission yeast. The spindle
  assembly checkpoint inhibits the APC via the Mad1, Mad2, Mph1, Mad3, Bub1, and Bub3
  proteins in response to a lack of bipolar attachment of kinetochores to spindle
  poles. The spindle orientation checkpoint inhibits APC by a pathway that requires
  Bub1, Bub3, and Mad3 but not Mad1 or Mad2. Microtubule depolymerizing agents activate
  both spindle assembly and spindle orientation checkpoints. Actin depolymerizing
  drugs only disrupt astral microtubule interaction with the cell cortex and activate
  only the spindle orientation checkpoint.
