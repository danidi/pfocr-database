caption: miR-340 is associated with various genes modulating cancer cell proliferation.
  Upregulated miR-340 inhibits the expression of JAK1, SKP2, and CDK4, repressing
  cell proliferation, while it inhibits the expression of BMP4 and cyclin G2, inducing
  cell proliferation. miR-340 inhibits the Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway by targeting
  LGR5 or FHL2, as well as the CTNNB1-mediated Notch signaling pathway, resulting
  in repressed cell proliferation.
