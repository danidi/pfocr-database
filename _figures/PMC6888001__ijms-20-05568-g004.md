  5-HT2C, 5-HT4, 5-HT6, and 5-HT7 receptors couple the PLC signaling pathway with
  a stimulatory effect (+) that triggers intracellular calcium release (↑ Ca2+), while
  5-HT1 couples the AC signaling pathway with an inhibitory effect (-) that induces
  cAMP decrease (↓ cAMP). Serotonin receptors have increased mRNA expression in ovarian
  cancer cells and interfere with SSRI effects in ovarian cancer patients.
