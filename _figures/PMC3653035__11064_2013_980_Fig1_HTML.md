caption: β-Catenin plays two main functions in the cell. a β-Catenin interacts with
  Cadherins at the internal site of the membrane and links them to the actin cytoskeleton
  together with α-catenin. b Cytoplasmic β-catenin plays an effector role in the canonical
  Wnt pathway. In the absence of WNT ligands, β-catenin is phosphorylated by GSK3α/β
  and subsequently degraded in the proteasome. Upon Wnt stimulation of a Frizzled
  receptor, Dishevelled (DVL) recruits AXIN to the LRP5/6 co-receptor, β-catenin is
  stabilized, enters the nucleus, and activates LEF1/TCF transcription factors
