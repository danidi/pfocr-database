  in remifentanil-induced postoperative hyperalgesia.Notes: Schematic illustration
  of proposed TRPV1-CaMKII/PKC-NR1 signaling in remifentanil-induced postoperative
  hyperalgesia. The activation of TRPV1 in RIH causes the cellular calcium influx,
  increasing CaMKII/PKC activity, which triggers NR1 membrane trafficking, and promoting
  a large amount of calcium influx, which triggers the release of Glu and transmits
  to the central neurons in the model of RIH.Abbreviations: CaMKII, calcium/calmodulin-dependent
  kinase II; Glu, glutamate; NMDA, N-methyl-D-aspartate; NR1, N-methyl-D-aspartate
