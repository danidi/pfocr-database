caption: Under the action of BMP2 and inflammatory mediators such as tumor necrosis
  factor, the expression of transcription factors Runx2 and osterix can be activated
  via the BMP/Smad pathway. Moreover, BMP can induce Wnt signaling pathway to activate
  the relevant transcription factors. It can increase the activity of NADPH oxidase
  during the oxidative stress and upregulate H2O2, leading to the endoplasmic reticulum
  stress response, increased XBP-1 expression; the latter induces Runx2 activation.
  NADPH oxidase, H2O2 can directly promote Runx2 activation.
