caption: Fyn signaling pathway. Activation of NOX, TLR, CD36, and TNFR may increase
  Fyn with or without ROS-mediated oxidative stress. Activated Fyn may i) suppress
  LKB1-AMPK and thus increases mTORC1-ER stress pathway and ii) activate STAT3 signaling
  which inhibits macroautophagy through suppression of VSP34, activates inflammation
  signaling, and mediates fibrosis. Additionally, Fyn also activates inflammation
  signaling (PKCδ>MAPK>NF-κB). All of these ultimately may contribute to kidney dysfunction.
  TNFR, tumor necrosis factor receptor.
