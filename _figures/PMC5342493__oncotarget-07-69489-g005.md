caption: Endothelial cell-specific loss of NRP-1 down-regulates and deactivates TGFβ
  signaling and its receptors leading to reduced pSMAD2, Slug, CTGF and Collagen 1.
  The reduction in the levels of transcription factor Slug further up-regulates the
  expression of endothelial markers like VE-cadherin and CD31, limiting EndMT. Contrastingly,
  overexpression of NRP-1 exacerbates the TGFβ-induced EndMT and associated tumor
  fibrosis.
