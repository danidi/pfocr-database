caption: The mammalian heat shock response stress responsive signaling pathway matches
  proteostasis network capacity with demand in the cytosol. The heat shock response
  is turned on by Hsp90 being recruited away from the transcription factor HSF1 to
  deal with aggregation and/or misfolding. This allows the HSF1 transcription factor
  to trimerize and be phosphorylated, which initiates transcription of genes harboring
  the heat shock response element (HSE). Phosphatases and acetyl transferase enzymes
  negatively regulate the heat shock response, as does Hsp90 expression that rebinds
  HSF1.
