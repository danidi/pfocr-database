  GSSG, glutathione disulfide; HAT, histone acetyltransferases; Hcy, homocysteine;

  JMJC, Jumonji domain demethylase; LSD1, lysine-specific demethylase 1; Me, methyl;
  NAD+, nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide; NADH, nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide
