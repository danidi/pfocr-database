caption: The effect of Icariin, Purmorphamine, U0126, and PD98059 on osteogenic signaling
  pathways. Icariin activates PI3K/Akt, promoting downstream RUNX2 expression and
  osteogenic lineage commitment. Purmorphamine stimulates Smoothened (Smo), activating
  the Hedgehog (Hh) signaling pathway, resulting in the upregulation of its downstream
  target gene GLI1, as well as the Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway, leading to bone
  formation and the promotion of osteogenic lineage commitment. Icariin also regulates
  osteogenic commitment via the Wnt/β-catenin pathway. U0126 and PD98059 act as MAPK/ERK
  mitogen activated kinase (MAPK) inhibitors, resulting in inhibition of osteogenic
  lineage commitment.
