  in the mitochondria and acts as an early substrate for the haem biosynthesis pathway.
  The penultimate molecule protoporphyrin IX (PpIX) displays bright red fluorescence
  when excited by blue/violet light. Exogenous ALA preferentially accumulates in neoplastic
  cells leading to an accumulation of PpIX that can be exploited for biophotonic diagnostics.
