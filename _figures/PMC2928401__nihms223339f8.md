  associations between genes for the purpose of defining physiologic pathways regulated

  TFAP2C and green indicates genes induced by TFAP2C. Inset: Retinoic acid signaling
  pathway was also identified as a prominent network involved with TFAP2C target genes,
  RXR, RAR and CRABP2.'
