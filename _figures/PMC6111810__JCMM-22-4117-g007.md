  of NEDD4‐dependent proteasomal degradation. PIP5Kα that produces PIP2 from phosphatidylinositol

  PIP2, a substrate of PI3K for PIP3 formation, which activates its downstream Akt
  (top). The PIP2 levels are reduced by NEDD4‐mediated PIP5Kα degradation via UPS,
  leading to downregulation of the oncogenic PI3K/Akt signalling pathway (bottom)
