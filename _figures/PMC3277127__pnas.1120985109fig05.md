  depicted above the protein and color coded. Solid arrow, protein is a direct substrate
  of the upstream kinase at that site. Dashed arrow, protein interacts directly with
  the upstream kinase/protein. Dotted arrow, protein is found within the pathway of
  the upstream kinase/protein.
