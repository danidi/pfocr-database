caption: Binding of PRL to PRLR induces activation of JAK1/2. CUZD1 forms a complex
  with JAKs and potentiates activation of STAT5 downstream of PRLR. Activated STAT5
  and CUZD1 translocate to the nucleus where STAT5 regulates transcription of target
  genes, such as Ereg and Wap. EREG acts in a paracrine and/or autocrine manner through
  ErbB1 and/or ErbB4 to induce mammary epithelial proliferation. The expression of
  WAP, a milk protein, marks the terminal differentiation of the mammary epithelium.
