  the p53 signaling pathway during viral infection by 1) inhibiting ATM phosphorylation,
  2) inhibiting p53 transcription activation, and 3) inhibiting p300 acetylation of
  p53. Our results suggest that vIRF1 also binds USP7 and decreases the availability
  of USP7 for deubiquitinating and stabilizing p53.
