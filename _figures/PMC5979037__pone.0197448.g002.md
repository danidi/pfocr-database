  reactions and gluconeogenesis. RoxA: Rubber oxygenase A, RoxB: Rubber oxygenase
  B, ODTD: 12-oxo-4,8-dimethyltrideca-4,8-diene-1-al, Mce: Mammalian cell entry protein,
  Mla: An ABC transport system that maintains lipid asymmetry, TBDRs: TonB-dependent
  outer membrane receptors, FACS: fatty acyl coenzyme A (CoA) synthetase: FadL: It
  encodes outer membrane proteins/ transporters involved in long-chain fatty acid,
  TCA: Tricarboxylic acid cycle, PEP: Phosphoenolpyruvate.'
