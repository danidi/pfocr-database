caption: After MI, the expression of TNF-α, IL-6 and TGF-β increased and activated
  several biological pathways, including JAK and PI3K/AKT pathways. These signal transductions
  activated several TFs, such as NFκB, STAT3 and SP1, to promote the transcription
  of miRNAs (e.g. mir-21) and genes (e.g. TIMP3, VEGFA, FASLG and COL4A1). miR-21-5p,
  the miR-29 family and SP1 co-regulated the process of cardiac fibrosis, apoptosis
  and angiogenesis through several cascades.
