  stepwise addition of monosaccharides to ceramide. The sequential action of ST3Gal

  the biosythesis of the precursors of a-, b-, and c-series gangliosides, respectively.
  The 0-series gangliosides are directly synthesized from lactosylceramide. The code
  names of gangliosides are according to Svennerholm [].
