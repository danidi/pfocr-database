caption: Possible schematic model of SNIPER(TACC3)‐induced death in cancer cells.
  SNIPER(TACC3) induces APC/CCDH1‐mediated ubiquitylation and proteasomal degradation
  of TACC3 protein, which results in activation of caspases and apoptosis in some
  cancer cells (right pathway). SNIPER(TACC3) also induces XIAP‐mediated ubiquitylation
  and causes accumulation of ubiquitylated protein aggregates, resulting in cytoplasmic
  vacuolization. Then, a large population of cancer cells appears to undergo PLCD
  (left pathway).
