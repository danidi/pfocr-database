caption: The CART2 domain of LMP1 activates the NF-κB signaling pathway, in turn,
  phosphorylates transcription factor p65. Phosphorylated p65 and p50/p52 complex
  binds and transactivates the DNMT3b promoter. Elevated DNMT3b expression eventually
  leads to higher CpG island methylation intensity of PTEN gene. This LMP1-mediated
  DNMT3b activation can be blocked by NF-κB inhibitor PDTC.
