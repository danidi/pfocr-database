  regulate U937 cell proliferation. LILRA3 might directly/indirectly inhibit class
  O forkhead box transcription factors (Foxo3a) phosphorylation. Whether Foxo3a plays
  a role in LILRA3‐induced cell proliferation needs to be further investigated.
