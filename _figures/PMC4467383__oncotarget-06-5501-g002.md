caption: Through sharing the highly similarity of amino acids sequences and all function
  as protein scaffolds, BRD2, BRD3 and BRD4 recruit different proteins. (A) BRD4 recruits
  proteins in a PTEFb-dependent manner, and it regulates the transcription process
  through coupling with RNA P II. (B) (C) BRD2 and BRD3 both exert function through
  E2F-RB pathway and in a PTEFb-independent manner, while BRD2 distinct from BRD3
  for its ability to binding with SWI/SNF complex and regulating binding of ATP and
  histones. (D) The process how HDAC inhibits transcription through binding with BRD2
  and E2F-Rb complex. (E) The process how BRD4 regulate transcription.
