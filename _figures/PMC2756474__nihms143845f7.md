caption: IL-1β, PGF2α and PGE2 activate CREB-regulated COX2 and MUC5AC gene expression
  through PKA or PKC mediated MAPK/RSK pathway. When synthesized, COX2 catalyzes the
  production of prostanoids, including PGE2 and PGF2α, which further stimulate their
  respective receptors and subsequent production of mucin and COX2 through the same
  pathways. After activation of IL-1β and PGF2α receptors (IL-1R1 and FP), the cytosolic
  signaling molecules are subsequently phosphorylated to the active state in the order
  of PKC, MEK, ERK, RSK, and followed by translocation of RSK into nucleus to activate
  CREB. The stimulation of PGE2 receptor (EP), which activates PKA pathway, also leads
  to CREB activation. The binding of activated CREB to CRE motif on MUC5AC and COX2
  promoters triggers their expression.
