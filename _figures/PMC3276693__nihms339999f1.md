  Abbreviations: MCM, methylmalonyl-CoA mutase; EB, erythronolide B; 6-dEB, 6-deoxyerythronolide

  and transferase enzymes; EryC, dTDP-desosamine biosynthetic and transferase enzymes;
  EryK, C-12 hydroxylase; EryG, S-adenosylmethionine methylase acting on the attached
  mycarose to convert it to cladinose.'
