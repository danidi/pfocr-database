caption: Insulin resistance or hyperinsulinemia-related neurodegenerative pathway.
  Insulin resistance or hyperinsulinemia induces degradation of insulin-degrading
  enzyme (IDE), affects the MAPK signaling pathway, stimulates the amyloidogenic pathway,
  and inhibits the non-amyloidogenic pathway, thereby facilitating the accumulation
  of amyloidβ (Aβ). It also induces Akt downregulation that eventually boosts glycogen
  synthase kinase (GSK) β activation and inhibits phosphatase 2A (PP2A) which leads
  to neurofibrillary tangles (NFTs) formation.
