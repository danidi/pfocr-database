caption: Cytosolic sensing of double stranded DNA (dsDNA) by cyclic GMP-AMP synthase
  (cGAS) leads to the production of cyclic GMP-AMP (cGAMP) as a second messenger,
  which then activates the stimulator of interferon genes (STING) in the endoplasmic
  reticulum (ER) and further upregulates type I interferon (IFN) transcription via
  the recruitment of TANK-binding kinase 1 (TBK1) and interferon regulatory factor
  3 (IRF3). The binding of type I IFN molecules to their receptors initiates the transcription
  of hundreds of IFN-stimulated genes (ISGs) via the activation of the Janus kinase
  (JAK)-signal transducer and activator of transcription (STAT) pathway.
