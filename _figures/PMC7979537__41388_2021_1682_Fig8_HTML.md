  dextromethorphan non-competitively inhibits nicotine-CHRNA7 interaction while metformin
  downregulates CHRNA7 by enhancing its promoter hypomethylation, and thereby they
  synergistically inactivating the JAK2/STAT3/SOX2 signaling pathway.
