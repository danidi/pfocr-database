caption: TLR2 activation induces microglial cell death by inducing autophagy. The
  stimulation of TLR2 with PGN, Pam2CSK4 and Pam3CSK4 induce activation of autophagy
  revealed by increased protein levels of Beclin 1 and LC3-II. Moreover, the co-localization
  of LAMP-1 and LC3-II indicated fusion of autophagosomes with lysosomes. Persistent
  induction of autophagy pathway promotes microglial cell death with a significantly
  reduction of microglial cells in the brain parenchyma. The microglial cell death
  was reverted in the presence of autophagy inhibitors (3-MA and LY294002).
