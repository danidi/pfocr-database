  samples harboring a mutation in a specific gene at a site previously observed in
  the COSMIC database. (B) The significantly mutated genes fall into seven core signaling
  pathways, in which the genes play well-established roles. Red - genes with significant
  mutation frequencies; pink - additional pathway genes with mutations.
