  pathway can induce phosphorylation (P) of TTP, which leads to proteasomal degradation
  of TTP protein. However, when CK2 is activated by TGF-β stimulation, it can activate
  MKP-1, which in turn inhibits the p38 MAPK/MK2 pathway by dephosphorylation of p38
  MAPK, thereby protecting the TTP protein from proteasomal degradation.
