caption: In IPF lungs, TGF-β3, gremlin, and LEF-1 are up-regulated, while BMP-2 is
  down-regulated. Dysregulation of TGF-β/BMP pathway induces a fibrotic phenotype.
  EMT, so important in development, is likely to represent one possible response of
  the alveolar epithelial cell facing sustained injury. Increased expression of BMPs
  may not only antagonize TGF-β-induced EMT, enhancing re-epithelialization, but also
  may cause the opposite process, mesenchymal-to-epithelial transition (MET).
