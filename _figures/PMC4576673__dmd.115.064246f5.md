  of indole, (2) microbial/host indole metabolism to generate AHR ligands, (3) tryptophan

  tryptophan metabolism via the kynurenine pathway, and (5) diet-derived ligand synthesis
  are all pathways of AHR ligand synthesis that mediate (6) AHR activation and transcription
  of target genes interleukin-6 (IL6), cytochrome P450 1A1 (CYP1A1), vascular endothelial
  growth factor A (VEGFA), prostaglandin G/H synthase 2 (PTGS2), interleukin-22 (IL22),
  and cytochrome P450 1B1 (CYP1B1).
