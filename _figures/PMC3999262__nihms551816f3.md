caption: Binding of trimeric TWEAK to Fn14 receptor leads to the recruitment of cIAPs
  and various TRAFs resulting in the activation of multiple downstream signaling cascades.
  TWEAK stimulates the NF-κB pathway which causes translocation of NF-κB into the
  nucleus and expression of several genes such as MuRF1 which activate UPS system.
  TWEAK also activates MAPK signaling cascades resulting in the activation of the
  AP1 transcription factor and gene expression. The activated genes also include cytokines,
  chemokines and MMPs which contribute to collagen remodeling and fibrosis. TWEAK-Fn14
  system inhibits Akt signaling and thus the activation Foxo family transcription
  factors leading to proteolytic degradation through enhanced expression of MAFBx
  and MuRF1. TWEAK also induces the expression of the components of autophagy lysosomal
  system and activates caspases in cultured myotubes to induce atrophy. TWEAK inhibits
  the expression of PGC1α which results in the inhibition of mitochondria biogenesis.
  TRAF, TNF receptor-associated factor; cIAP; cellular inhibitor of apoptosis; AP1,
  activator protein-1; FOXO, Forkhead box O.
