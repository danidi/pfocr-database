caption: A model of temporal regulation of apoptosis in SeV-infected cells by PI3K.
  Virus infection uses the RIG-I pathway to activate cyt c release by IRF-3/Bax translocation
  to mitochondria. XIAP degradation is also induced by the RIG-I pathway but blocked
  by a virus-activated PI3K/Akt pathway in the early phase of virus infection. Prevention
  of XIAP degradation blocked caspase 9 activation in the APAF-1/cyt c apoptosome
  complex.
