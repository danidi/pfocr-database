caption: Quercetin apoptotic mechanism via death ligand and mitochondrial membrane.
  Following the intrinsic pathway, quercetin causes massive release of Cyt-c from
  the mitochondrial intramembranous space and induces apoptosomes. Furthermore, via
  blebbing, DNA fragmentation, and cytoskeleton contraction, it paves the way to apoptosis.
  On the other hand, through the extrinsic pathway, quercetin initiates caspase-8,
  which leads to apoptosis. A substantial increase in JNK and cdc cyclin B while decreasing
  heat shock proteins by quercetin also promotes therapy of prostate cancer. Bcl-xL,
  B-cell lymphoma extra-large; Bcl-2, B-cell lymphoma-2; Cyt-c, cytochrome c; JNK,
  c-jun N terminal kinase; ERK, extracellular signal regulated kinase; PARP, poly
  ADP-ribose polymerase; PI3K, phosphoinositide 3 kinase; Akt, serine/threonine specific
  protein kinase.
