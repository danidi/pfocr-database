  and TLR4 signaling in hepatic stellate cells (HSCs) activation and liver fibrosis.

  which in turn contributes to HSCs activation and then liver fibrosis. Moreover,
  upregulated TGM2 positively strengthened TLR4 signaling by enhancing TLR4 activation
  or possibly by triggering IRAK1 phosphorylation.
