caption: MAP17 function in normal cells. MAP17 interaction with PDZK1 allows the assembly
  of a multimeric and variable complex responsible for cation/anion transport across
  the membrane. This complex potentiates the uptake of glucose by SGLT1/2 and the
  subsequent transport of glucose by GLUT1 from basolateral membrane to the bloodstream.
  Presence of MAP17-PDZK1 complexes in the trans-Golgi network allows cells to adapt
  to a change in ion transport requirements. The Notch pathway remains inactivated
  due to NUMB-NICD interaction, which labels NICD for ubiquitination and degradation
