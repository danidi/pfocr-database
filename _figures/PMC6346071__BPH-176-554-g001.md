  generated from methionine in the transsulfuration pathway. Cysteine is the rate‐limiting

  or H2S to sulfate and taurine or sulfate and thiosulfate respectively. Terminal
  products are underlined. The shapes of the enzymes involved are depicted according
  to the crystal structures available. CBS, 1M54; CDO, 2IC1; CSAD, 2JIS; CSE, 2NMP;
