caption: These pathways putatitvely influence the morphology of radial glia, as well
  as the ability of radial glia to provide a scaffold for migrating neurons and generate
  new neurons. The classic reelin pathway involves VLDL and ApoER2 receptors and Dab1
  as shown on the left in shades of pink; PI3K is also an integral part of reelin
  signaling. Neuregulin (NRG1) signaling progresses primarily through erbB receptors
  and pathways leading to PI3K activation (shades of green) or involvement of the
  Notch signaling pathway (indicated in shades of purple). Activation of Notch results
  in upregulation of BLBP production. In certain radial glial cells, blockade of PI3K
  can be compensated by NRG1 signaling through erbB receptors that coordinate with
  Notch.
