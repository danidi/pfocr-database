caption: As a GAP, RGS19 terminates the action of active GTP-bound Gαi, relieves the
  inhibition of adenylyl cyclase (AC) that produce cAMP. Elevation of intracellular
  cAMP level and the activation of PKA by agonists of Gs-coupled receptors, AC activator
  (forskolin) and cAMP analogues lead to phosphorylation of CREB, thus driving the
  expression of Nm23-H1/2. Endogenous or forskolin-induced Nm23-H1/2 protein expression
  can be suppressed by H-89 which inhibits PKA. Arrow with solid line, activation;
  blunted arrow, inhibition. CRE, cAMP responsive element; CREB, CRE-binding protein;
  P, phosphate; PKA, cAMP-dependent protein kinase.
