caption: Proposed model for H2O2 stimulation of CFTR. In differentiated NHBE cells,
  H2O2 activates the GPCRs EP1 and EP4. EP4, that appears responsible for the majority
  of the H2O2response (Conner et al., ), and albuterol both activate Gαs to stimulate
  tmAC. Increased cAMP activates PKA to stimulate CFTR-mediated Cl− secretion and
  basolateral Kv7.1 K+ secretion. Also, cAMP activation of Epac initiates a Ca2+ signal
  presumably through PLC stimulation. This Epac-dependent Ca2+ signal stimulates both
  tmAC and sAC to amplify the cAMP/PKA pathway.
