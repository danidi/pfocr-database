caption: p73 pathway activation in thyroid cancer. (A) ΔNp73α binding on the p53 promoter
  causes the activation of periostin (1) and a reduction in PTEN expression (blue)
  (2). Transcriptional repression of the PTEN promoter determines an activation of
  the PI3K-Akt pathway resulting in MDM2 phosphorylation that enhances MDM2-dependent
  degradation of p53; (B) TAp73α blocks p53-dependent transcription on the MDM2 promoter.
  In turn, this leads to reduced MDM2 expression and increased p53 stability.
