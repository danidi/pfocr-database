  oncogenic JAK2 signaling pathway regulates arginine methyltransferase PRMT5 activity

  are in bold. Metabolism pathways are marked in italic. P in green dots denotes for
  phosphorylation-mediated regulation. TDH; threonine dehydrogenase, MTAP; methylthioadenosine
