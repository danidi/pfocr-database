caption: BCR, BAFFR and TACI crosstalk. (a) Under normal conditions, BAFFR synergizes
  with the BCR signal to promote pro-survival and metabolic signals via the alternative
  NFκB pathway and by facilitating BCR-mediated activation of Syk in transitional
  cells. (b) In an autoimmune setting of excess serum BAFF, increased levels of BAFF
  60-mers are present. These multimers specifically bind to TACI, driving aberrant
  activation of the classical NFκB pathway and, possibly, directly impact transitional
  B cell activation.
