  mitochondrial function. In diet-induced obesity, increases in ATX-LPA contribute
  to impaired skeletal muscle insulin signaling and glucose transport, a primary driver
  of systemic insulin resistance and type 2 diabetes. Inhibition of the ATX-LPA pathway
  enhances skeletal muscle insulin signaling, glucose transport, and mitochondrial
  glucose oxidation in an obesogenic milieu, thereby ameliorating diet-induced obesity
  and glucose homeostasis. GSV, Glut4 storage vesicles; P, phosphorylation; G, glucose.
