caption: Representation of the transduction pathway involved in Cd signaling. (A)
  Once entered the plant cell Cd induces ROS, responsible for the activation of MAPK
  kinase cascade. This, together with the activation of Ca-calmodulin system and stress-related
  hormones, converge regulating transcription factors in the nucleus. (B) In turn,
  transcription factors induce expression of Cd-detoxification responsible genes.

  protein kinase); TF (transcription factor); PC (phytochelatin); PCS (phytochelatin
  synthetase); MT (metallothionein).
