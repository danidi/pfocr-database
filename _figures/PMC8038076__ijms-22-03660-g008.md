  suppresses PM-induced ROS (hydrogen peroxide, superoxide anions, and hydroxyl radicals).
  The levels of BCL-2 proteins are upregulated by GR-SC65. GR-SC65 also inhibits the
  activation of BAX, caspase-9, caspase-3, and PARP, all downstream signaling molecules.
  The brown arrow indicates the inhibitory activity of GR-SC65 through the PM-induced
  ROS-mediated apoptosis pathway. PM, particulate matter; ROS, reactive oxygen species;
  PAHs, polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons; PARP, poly (ADP-ribose) polymerase; NOX2,
  NADPH oxidase 2.
