  of Src and oncogenic Ras (RasV12/ACT) results in cooperative tumourigenesis in Drosophila
  epithelia, resulting in invasive, overgrown tumours characterised by loss of differentiation
  and F-actin accumulation. Upon Src expression, JNK pathway signalling is activated
  and the Hippo pathway is inhibited, whilst downstream of oncogenic Ras, activation
  of Raf and PI3K is required for cooperative tumourigenesis with Src.
