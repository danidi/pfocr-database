  cycle regulation pathways. VEGF signaling is a growth factor pathway to stimulate
  vasculogenesis and angiogenesis. A, MAPK/ERK signaling regulates cell proliferation.

  pathway (white arrows). C, Damage‐signaling pathway. AKT/PI3K signaling inhibits
  apoptosis, senescence, and cell cycle arrest through inhibition of FOXO, which in
  turn positively regulates p21 and p27 activity.
