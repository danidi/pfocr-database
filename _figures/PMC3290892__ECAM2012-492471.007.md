caption: Proposed schematic diagram for of EA-induced analgesia in normal rats. Peripheral
  stimulation by EA induces glutamate release in the spinal cord dorsal horn this
  neurotransmitter activates NMDAR. Appropriate influx of calcium via activated NMDAR
  leads to the phosphorylation of the PI3K and CREB cascade, especially PI3K, manifesting
  as analgesia. The dotted line denotes where further study is needed to clarify an
  interaction between PI3K and EA analgesia in normal rats.
