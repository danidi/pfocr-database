  8 and cIAP or FLIP. Apoptosis is induced by the activation of caspase 8/caspase
  3 as well as activation of the intrinsic mitochondrial pathway.FADD: Fas-associated
  death domain protein; TRAIL: TNF-related apoptosis-inducing ligand.'
