  insulin resistance, and diabetes mellitus can cause atrial structural, electrical,

  protective effects through various mechanisms. Red arrow shows adverse effects from
  atrial fibrillation, obesity, insulin resistance, and diabetes. Green rectangle
  shows pathway that metformin blocked. Solid green arrow shows protective mechanisms
  of metformin directly demonstrated from the studies in Tables  and . Dotted green
  arrow indicates protective mechanism of metformin from other studies in the text
