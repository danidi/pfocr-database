caption: 'A visual description of the signaling cascade is shown for the signal-receiving
  cell (i.e. the cell expressing the Notch receptor). Pathway inhibitors used include
  antibodies against NOTCH receptors and DLL ligands, γ-secretase complex inhibitors
  (GSI), and small peptides inhibiting formation of the transcriptional complex. Antibody-based
  treatments are shown in purple, GSI compounds in pink, peptide-based drugs in red.
  Potential epigenetic inhibitors (in green) can include BRD inhibitors like JQ1.
  HDAC: histone deacetylase, ICN1: intracellular part of NOTCH1, LSD1: lysine specific
  demethylase 1, SMRT: Silencing-Mediator for Retinoid/Thyroid hormone receptors,
  GSK3β: glycogen synthase kinase 3 beta, DNMAML1: dominant negative MAML1.'
