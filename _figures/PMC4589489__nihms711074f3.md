caption: (A) Schematic of the ERBB(EGFR)/KRAS/BRAF/MAPK signaling axis, and its connection
  with the PI3 Kinase pathway. Common sites for mutation in sporadic CRC are indicated.
  (B) COX and 15-PGDH reciprocal relationship. In sporadic CRCs, Cox-2 is elevated
  and 15-PGDH is diminished, increasing PGE2 levels that can mediate increased proliferation
  and angiogenesis. By blocking Cox-2 pharmacologically, and/or selecting patients
  with high expression of 15-PDGH, levels of PGE2 can be diminished which can help
  prevent both primary and recurrent CRC.
