caption: Signaling pathway involved in THP-1 human monocyte activation by glycomacropeptide.
  Through an unknown (?) receptor, glycomacropeptide (GMP) induces tumor necrosis
  factor (TNF)-α, interleukin (IL)-1β, and IL-8 production. Intracellular molecules
  that participate in the response of monocytes to GMP are mitogen-activated protein
  (MAP) kinases p38, c-Jun N-terminal kinase (JNK), and extracellular-signal-regulated
  protein kinases 1 and 2 (ERK1/2). The nuclear factor-κB (NF-κB) pathway is also
  involved as GMP activates the phosphorylation of inhibitor of κBα (IκBα) and the
  nuclear translocation of the p50 and p65 NF-κB subunits. IKK, kinase IκB; P, phosphate
  group.
