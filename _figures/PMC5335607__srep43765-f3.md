  of Rac signalling by 18/1 and 90/5 patterns of stimulation. Rac signalling pathways
  were oriented to depict extracellular, cytoplasmic, and nuclear sites of action.

  the regulation of target pathway genes. Shaded targets reflect multiple regulated
  components (e.g. MAPK pathway in 18/1 stimulation pattern). Multiple downstream
  pathways interacting through Rac, e.g., actin polymerization, translation and membrane
  ruffling, affect neurite outgrowth and dynamics.
