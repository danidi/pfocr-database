  PI3K catalyzes the phosphorylation of PtdIns(4,5)P2 (phosphatidylinositol 4,5-bisphosphate)

  recruitment of PDK1 and AKT to the plasma membrane and their activation. AKT then
  acts upon multiple downstream effectors, leading to cell growth, proliferation and
  survival.
