caption: Figure 2. GH transmits the signal in two phases. In the first phase (represented
  as dark arrow), GH binds to the GHR and triggers the JAK2-STAT5B signaling axis.
  This leads to the transcription of IGF-1 and IGF-1R. These then mediates the second
  phase (represented by dotted arrows) of signal transduction. Here IGF-1 binds to
  IGF-1R and triggers the MAPK pathway or PI3K/Akt pathway and induces osteoblast
  proliferation and differentiation.
