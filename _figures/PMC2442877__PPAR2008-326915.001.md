  activate PPAR-γ. Selective and nonselective COX-2 inhibitors (COX Inh) block PG
  formation by COX-2 but can also at higher concentrations activate PPAR-γ. Solid
  arrows indicate activation; dashed arrow indicates metabolic pathway; blocked arrow
  indicates inhibition.'
