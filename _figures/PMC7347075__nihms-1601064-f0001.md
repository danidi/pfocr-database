caption: The metabolic kynurenine (Kyn) conversion pathway. In addition to being used
  as building block for protein synthesis, the majority of dietary tryptophan (95%)
  is catabolized via the Trp→Kyn pathway (red arrows). Within the Kyn pathway, underlined
  metabolites can cross the blood brain barrier (BBB). IDO1 (and TDO) are highlighted
  in the black box. 3-HAO, 3-hydroxyanthranilate 3,4-dioxygenase; ACMSD, 2-amino-3-carboxymuconate

  QPRT, quinolinic-acid phosphoribosyl transferase.
