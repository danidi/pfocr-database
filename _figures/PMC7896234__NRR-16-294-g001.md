  Schematic representation of the signaling pathway underlying changes in microglial

  inflammasome. The boxed structure consisting of NLRP3, adaptor protein ASC and Pro-Caspase-1

  IL: interleukin; IL-1R: IL-1β receptor; IP3: inositol 1,4,5-trisphosphate; MT: mitochondrion;

  NF-κB: nuclear factor ‘kappa-light-chain-enhancer’ of activated B-cells; NLRP3:
  NACHT-, LRR- and pyrin (PYD)-domain-containing protein 3; P2X7: ionotropic ATP receptor;
  P2Y1, 2, 4, 6: metabotropic ATP receptors; PLC: phospholipase C; SOCE: store-operated

  potential cation channel, subfamily M, member 2.'
