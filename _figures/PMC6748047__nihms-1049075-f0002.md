caption: Protein pathway analysis using serum proteins present in healthy controls
  and/or AA patients. (A) STRING database was employed for protein pathway analysis
  using proteins higher in HC compared by unpaired t test with AA patients before
  IST. Known (from databases or experimentally determined) or predicted (based on
  gene neighborhood) interactions are shown. Proteins related to immune response,
  coagulation, regulation of apoptotic process, cytokine-cytokine receptor interaction,
  T-cell receptor (TCR), and Wnt, Ras, HIF-1, NF-κB and Jak-STAT signaling cascades
  are highlighted with different colors accordingly. (B) STRING database was employed
  for protein pathway analysis using the 19 selected serum candidate markers and the
  top 10 cellular processes are shown.
