caption: Strategies to inhibit the VEGF signalling pathway. Neuropilins (NRs) can
  function as co-receptors for vascular endothelial growth factor receptor (VEGFR).
  VEGF Trap (decoy receptor), growth factor-binding domains of VEGFR-1 and -2 bound
  to Fc fragment of IgG, and tyrosine kinase inhibitors (TKIs) prevent phosphorylation
  of VEGFR in response to VEGF binding. Delta-like ligand-4 (DLL4) binds to the Notch
  receptor, which leads to the cleavage of the Notch intracellular domain (NCID).
  The cleaved NCID translocates to the nucleus leading to the transcription of notch
  target genes. Protein kinase C (PKC) family of kinases are downstream mediators
  of VEGFR signalling. PlGF=placental-like growth factor.
