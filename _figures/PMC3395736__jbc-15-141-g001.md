  pathway. The solid arrows indicate glycolytic reactions, whereas the dashed arrows
  show the pentose-phosphate pathway. The green arrows indicate further metabolism
  of pyruvate downstream of glycolysis. Pentavalent arsenic compound (H3AsO4) abolishes
  adenosine triphosphate (ATP) generation by causing arsenolysis in the glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate
  dehydrogenase (GAPDH) reaction (From Pelicano H, et al. Oncogene 2006;25:4633-46,
  with permission) []. HK=hexokinase; PGI=phosphoglucose isomerase; PFK=phosphofructokinase;
