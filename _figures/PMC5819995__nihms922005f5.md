  staurosporine and thapsigargin) promote podocyte injury. This results in a reduction
  in MEK/ERK signaling, which leads to cell death. GDC-0879 promotes activation of
  MAPK signaling to rescue podocytes. In a parallel pathway, podocyte injury results
  in a compensatory induction of protein biosynthesis. Forskolin confers a protective
  advantage by attenuating protein biosynthesis.
