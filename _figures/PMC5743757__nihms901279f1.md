caption: TLRs differentially recruit specific sets of TIR domain-containing adaptor
  molecules like MyD88, TRIF, TIRAP/MAL, or TRAM. The MyD88-dependent pathway is utilized
  by all TLRs except TLR3, where TLR1-2 dimers, TLR2-6 dimers, or TLR5 and TLR4 self-dimerization
  lead to the activation of NF-κB and MAPKs for the induction of inflammatory cytokine
  genes. TLR3 utilizes TRIF adapter molecules and leads to the activation of IRF3,
  NF-κB, and MAPKs for induction of type I IFNs and inflammatory cytokine genes.
