caption: Upon entering cells of various types, numerous glucose-derived metabolites
  are sensed by a variety of cellular sensors, including glycogen synthase, ChREBP,
  AMPK, SIRT1–PGC-1α, carnitine palmitoyl transferase 1 (CPT1), and KATP channels.
  Via its diverse metabolites and their sensors, glucose entering the cell changes
  gene transcription, modulates signal transduction networks, and alters substrate
  flux through anabolic and catabolic pathways. ACC, acetyl-CoA carboxylase; FA, fatty
  acid; F6P, fructose-6-phosphate; GLUT, facilitative glucose transporter; GP, glycogen

  PDH, pyruvate dehydrogenase; PPP, pentose phosphate pathway.
