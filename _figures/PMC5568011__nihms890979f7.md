caption: HFD treatment impairs miR-206 biogenesis. Ptpn1 encodes a non-receptor phospho-tyrosine
  protein phosphatase (Ptp1b), which can dephosphorylate Insr, Irs and PP2A (phosphatase
  2A). By targeting Ptpn1, miR-206 prevents dephosphorylation of Insr and Irs1, which
  subsequently improves insulin sensitivity and prevents gluconeogenesis. PP2A is
  able to activate Srebp1c transcription by dephosphorylating Sp1, a transcription
  factor of Srebp1c. Thus, miR-206 is able to inhibit lipogenesis via the Ptpn1-PP2A-Sp1-Srebp1c
  pathway. Thus, miR-206 can simultaneously improve insulin sensitivity through Ptpn1-Insr/Irs1
  and inhibit lipogenesis via Ptpn1-Pp2a-Sp1-Srebp1c axis.
