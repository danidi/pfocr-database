  encoding the indicated shRNAs. At the indicated time point after stimulation with

  reduced metabolite on Pfkl knockdown. (c) RAW264.7 cells were transduced with the
  indicated shRNAs, and NADPH levels were quantified by enzymatic cycling assay. Data
  represent the mean of triplicates±s.d. *P≤0.05 as determined by Student's t-test.
