caption: Overview of the Noncanonical NF-κB signaling pathway. In the absence of a
  stimulus the critical kinase NIK is constitutively targeted for degradation by a
  ubiquitination complex containing TRAF2, TRAF3, cIAP1, and cIAP2. Upon receptor
  activation, NIK is stabilized, leading to IKKα activation and p100 processing to
  p52. RelB-p52 dimers then translocate to the nucleus and activate transcription.
