caption: a, Structures of precolibactin-546 (5), precolibactin-712 (7), precolibactin-795a
  (8), precolibactin-795b (9), precolibactin-886 (10) and precolibactin-969 (11).
  b, Proposed biosynthetic pathway of precolibactins. Extending from ClbJ, the dimodule
  PKS/NRPS ClbK shows diverse functions in the production of clb metabolites. The
  clb pathway utilizes ClbKPKS and ClbL to produce 8 (Route A); or skips ClbKPKS but
  utilizes ClbKNRPS to produce 9 (Route B); or utilizes both of ClbKPKS and ClbKNRPS
  modules to produce 10 which is the precursor for the assembly of 11 (Route C). A,
  adenylation; ACP, acyl carrier protein; Am, amidase; AT, acyltransferase; Cy, cyclization;
  KS, ketosynthase; Ox, oxidase; PCP, peptidyl carrier protein. AT* domains are predicted
  based on structural topology as ancestral inactive relics.
