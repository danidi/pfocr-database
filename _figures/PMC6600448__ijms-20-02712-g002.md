caption: Molecular mechanisms of alcohol-induced apoptosis and autophagy in hepatocytes.
  (A,B) Intracellular ROS is produced through the alcohol metabolism via CYP2E1 and
  NADPH oxidase. These induced ROS level cause hepatocyte apoptosis through AMPK,
  GSH, AKT, JNK/STAT3, and NF-κB pathways. (C) Alcohol consumption activates Kupffer
  cells to release TNF-α AND IL-1β, which then activates the Fas ligand-dependent
  caspase pathway to induce hepatocyte apoptosis.
