caption: 'The schematic diagram illustrates targeting strategies for the NFAT1-MDM2-p53
  pathway. To effectively inhibit NFAT1’s expression and activity, three targeting
  strategies have been proposed: (1) blocking NFAT1-MDM2 DNA binding, (2) promoting
  NFAT1 phosphorylation and/or inhibiting NFAT1 dephosphorylation, and (3) directly
  destabilizing NFAT1 protein. To effectively inhibit MDM2’s expression and activity,
  three targeting strategies have been proposed: (4) blocking MDM2-p53 interaction,
  (5) promoting MDM2 autoubiquitination and proteasomal degradation, and (6) directly
  inhibiting MDM2 expression. Small molecules that can dually inhibit NFAT1-MDM2 may
  be developed using these targeting strategies.'
