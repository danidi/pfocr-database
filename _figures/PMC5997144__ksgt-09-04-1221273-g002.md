caption: P-Rex1 acts as a convergence point for pro-oncogenic/metastatic pathways.
  (A) Activation of P-Rex1 by ligand stimulation of HER/ErbB receptors (for example
  with HRG) and CXCR4 (with SDF-1). PIP3 and Gβγ subunits generated upon receptor
  stimulation activate P-Rex1 GEF-activity toward Rac. (B) HER/ErbB receptors transactivate
  CXCR4 independently of SDF-1, leading to P-Rex1/Rac activation. (C) Sustained stimulation
  of HER3/ErbB3 stabilizes the expression of the HIF-1α, leading to enhanced transcriptional
  activation of the CXCR4 gene. This results in up-regulation of CXCR4 surface expression
  and augmented P-Rex1/Rac1 motility signaling.
