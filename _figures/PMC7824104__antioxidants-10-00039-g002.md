caption: 'Regulation of Nrf2 signaling. Nrf2 pathway is regulated at different levels:
  (A) transcriptional mechanism to induce a positive regulation through binding to
  XRE or ARE in the NFE2L2 gene promoter region. NF-κB, c-Jun, and c-Fos can regulate
  negatively NFE2L2 transcription. (B) NFE2L2 is also regulated in an epigenetic or
  post-transcriptional manner including DNA methylation, histone modification, and
  microRNAs. miRNAs can induce Keap1 mRNA degradation or Nrf2 mRNA inactivation/stabilization.
  (C) Nrf2 is also regulated at the translational level through an internal ribosomal
  entry site (IRES) that can initiate/inhibit Nrf2 mRNA translation. (D) Nrf2 may
  be also regulated by Keap1-, β-TrCP- and Hrd1-mediated Nrf2 proteasomal degradation.'
