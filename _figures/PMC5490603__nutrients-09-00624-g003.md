caption: Anti-inflammatory signaling pathways influenced by free zinc. (a) TGFβ signaling
  is dependent on a dynamic on and off switch in Smad activity. Free zinc is a cofactor
  in Smad proteins and promote Smad 2/3 nuclear translocation and transcriptional
  activity. (b) zinc regulates IL-2 signaling pathway via blocking MAP kinase phosphatase
  (MKP) in extracellular signal-regulated kinases (ERK) 1/2 pathways and Phosphatase
  and tensin homologue (PTEN) which opposes phosphoinositide 3-kinase (PI3K) function
  in PI3k/Akt pathway. (c) free zinc phosphorylates STAT6 and promotes translocation
  of STAT dimers into the nucleus, hence promote the anti-inflammatory effects of
  Il-4.
