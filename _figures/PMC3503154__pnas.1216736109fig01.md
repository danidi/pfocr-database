caption: (A) Characterized pathway for activation of STAT. The Upd3 ligand binds Domeless,

  through an unknown mechanism, activates expression of Vago. Processed and secreted
  Vago binds to an unknown cellular receptor, resulting in Jak-STAT activation and
  expression of vir-1 and yet-uncharacterized antiviral genes.
