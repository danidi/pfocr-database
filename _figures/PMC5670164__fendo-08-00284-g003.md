  (Shh) pathway. Canonical or non-canonical Gli activation induces Snail expression.

  through miRNAs, such as miR-128 or miR-218. Bmi1 is a master regulator that controls
  the expression of several stem cell-related genes, such as Sox2 and Nanog, and CSC
  self-renewal.
