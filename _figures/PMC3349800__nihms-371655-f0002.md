  factors. FoxO phosphorylation by PI3K/Akt facilitates FoxO interaction with 14-3-3
  chaperone proteins and nuclear export; cytoplasmic sequestration inhibits FoxO-dependent
  transcription (). Resveratrol blocks Akt activation and subsequent FoxO phosphorylation
  and nuclear export; on the other hand, resveratrol-induced FoxO expression facilitates
  trans-activation of anti-proliferative/pro-apoptotic forkhead response element (FHRE)-driven
  promoters (see text for references). GF, growth factor; RTK, receptor tyrosine kinase.
