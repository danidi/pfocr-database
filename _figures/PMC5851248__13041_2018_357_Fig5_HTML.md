  highlighted neuronal death signaling pathways associated with excitotoxicity are:
  GluN2B-PSD95-nNOS, GluN2B-DAPK1-p53, GluN2B NMDAR-PTEN, and NMDAR-SFK-Panx1. Further
  downstream death mediators of excitotoxicity are calpain, MAPK:p38 and JNK, and

  previously tested in animal stroke models or clinical trials. *:Peroxynitrite scavengers
  and antioxidants that may act downstream of the GluN2B-PSD95-nNOS pathway to prevent
  neurotoxicity. #: Therapeutic time window of the peptides not yet examined in animal
  ischemic stroke model. Numbers in superscript indicates references in the manuscript'
