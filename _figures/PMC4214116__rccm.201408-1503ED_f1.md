caption: A hypoxia-driven pathway for lung cancer. Under normal conditions, E3 ubiquitin
  ligase von Hippel-Lindau (VHL) labels hypoxia-inducible factor-α (HIF-α) for polyubiquitination
  and proteasomal degradation in the 26S proteasome. During hypoxia, HIF-α is not
  targeted for degradation. Stabilized HIF-α proteins stimulate HOIL-1L gene transcription.
  This E3 ligase component targets the tumor suppressor PKCζ for its degradation by
  the 26S proteasome, thereby promoting tumor growth. HOIL-1L = heme-oxidized IRP2
  ubiquitin ligase 1L; PKCζ = protein kinase Cζ; Ub = ubiquitin.
