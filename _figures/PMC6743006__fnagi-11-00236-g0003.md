caption: The exposure to high fat diets over time, enriched in saturated fatty acids,
  would promote the glial activation and an increased mitochondrial oxidative stress.
  All those stressing factors would activate JNK-1 resulting in an impairment of insulin
  receptor pathway, causing and imbalance in ATP/ADP levels and a failure to control
  mitochondrial biogenesis and to maintain the synaptic connexions. This hypothesis
  would explain how JNK-1 should be a key factor linking diet and insulin signaling
  to synaptogenesis failure.
