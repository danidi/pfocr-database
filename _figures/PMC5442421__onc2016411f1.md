  pathway, from glucose to pyruvate and lactate: red lines. The pathways, from glutamine,
  acetate, and fatty acids to aminoacids, nucleotides and fat: green lines. The Krebs
  cycle: yellow lines. accos, acetylcoA synthase; aldo, aldolase; Atp cl, adenosine

  ldh, lactate dehydrogenase; mct, monocarboxylic acid transporter; me, malic enzyme;
