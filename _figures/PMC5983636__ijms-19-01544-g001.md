caption: Overview of the mammalian target of rapamycin (mTOR) signaling pathway. mTOR
  signaling is activated in several ways, including growth factors, amino acids, and
  increased cellular energy. Growth factors can activate both mTOR complexes or PI3K
  and ERK signaling. mTORC2 phosphorylates Akt at S473 to enhance activation. In turn,
  Akt suppress TSC2, which suppresses Rheb. Additionally, ERK can inhibit TSC2 to
  promote mTORC1 activation. Phosphorylation of 4E-BPs and S6K1/2 by mTORC1 promote
  cap-dependent translation.
