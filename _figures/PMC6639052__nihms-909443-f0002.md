caption: Stimulation of the IGF-1R with IGF-1 ligand results in auto-phosphorylation
  of the receptor, leading to phosphorylation of insulin receptor substrate 1 (IRS1)
  and the activation of the PI3K pathway that ultimately results in cellular growth
  and proliferation. PTEN has recently been shown to be a protein phosphatase for
  IRS-1, and NEDD4 antagonizes this phosphatase activity (). IGF-1 signalling stimulates
  the ubiquitination of pAKT by NEDD4 to promote the peri-nuclear trafficking of pAKT
  (). The adaptor protein Grb10 negatively regulates IGF-1 signalling by binding the
  IGF-1R, and in vivo data indicates that NEDD4 down-regulates Grb10 expression ().
  IGF-1R is also targeted by ubiquitin ligases such as MDM2 and c-Cbl for endocytosis
  and degradation.
