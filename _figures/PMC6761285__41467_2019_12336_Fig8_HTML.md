caption: Damage sensing by Nox-Ask1-MKK3-p38 signaling in the fly intestinal epithelial
  niche. Multiple stresses (pathogenic bacteria, detergents, and wounding) stimulate
  reactive oxygen species (ROS) production by NADPH oxidase (Nox), thus activating
  Ask1-MKK3-p38 signaling in enterocytes (EC). Cell detachment and tumor growth may
  also activate MKK3-p38 signaling either via Nox or Ask1. p38 signaling stimulates
  Upd3 expression in ECs and JAK-STAT activity in intestinal stem cells (ISCs), thus
  boosting ISC proliferation and supporting intestinal regeneration
