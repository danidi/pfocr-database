caption: 'A. The Mechanistic relationship between PI3K pathway activation and cell-cycle
  in tumor cells: PI3K signaling engages AKT, which drives mTORC1 CAP-dependent mRNA
  translation encoding CYCLIN D1/D2/D3. CYCLIN D1/D2/D3 bind CDK4/6 to promote RB
  phosphorylation, which depresses E2F transcription factor to drive expression of
  genes that promote proliferation and suppress apoptosis. CDK6 can phosphorylate
  FOXOM1 to enhance FOXOM1 stability, which suppresses senescence. Blue: FDA approved
  drugs and RED: Active in a clinical trial. B. Intramolecular interactions between
  PIK3CA and PIK3R1: Domain-specific regulation of PIK3CA (a p110α catalytic subunit
  of PI3K) by PIK3R1 (p85α regulatory subunit of PI3K) as scaffolds for regulated
  complex formation. Distribution of some important non-synonymous mutations of PIK3CA
  and PIK3R1 were depicted.'
