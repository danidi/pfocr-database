caption: The proposed scheme for the possible mechanism of EA pretreatment suppresses
  CIR-induced autophagy via the SIRT1-FOXO1 signaling pathway. EA pretreatment at
  GV20, LI11, and ST36 significantly alleviated neurological deficits, reduced infarct
  volume, increased the dendritic spine density of cortical neurons, decreased the
  LC3-II/LC3-I ratio, and up-regulated p62 level. The above beneficial effects may
  be achieved by the activation of SITR1 and the inhibition of acetylation of FOXO1,
  as well as the suppression of Atg7 expression and the interaction of Ac-FOXO1 and
  Atg7.
