caption: (A) Epigenetic regulation of IL-15 transcription by Zeb1 in normal and malignant
  T-cells. Excessive methylation at the Zeb1 binding site promotes the aberrant production
  of IL-15 from CD4+ T cells. (B) Autocrine IL-15 production and consequent signaling
  in CD4+ T cells induces expression of chromatin modifiers HDAC1 and HDAC6. Upregulation
  of HDAC1 positively regulates expression of oncomir miR-21. Collectively, the deregulation
  of these enzymes by aberrant IL-15 signaling contributes to malignant transformation
  of a normal T-cell.
