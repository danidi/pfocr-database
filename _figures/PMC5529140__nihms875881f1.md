  3 is inhibited by the binding of XIAP (gray). SMAC (red), also known as Diablo,

  displaces XIAP-Caspase 3 interaction allowing for induction of apoptosis. In addition
  to Caspase regulation, XIAP is known to interact and regulate RIPK2 (cyan) in the
  NOD pathway of bacterial activation.
