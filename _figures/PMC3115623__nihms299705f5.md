caption: Major intracellular signal transduction pathways for CRF1 and CRF2 receptors.
  While the dominant mode of signaling for both CRF receptors signaling is Gs-coupled
  AC-PKA cascade, they may also signal via the PLC-PKC and ERK-MAPK cascades. CRF
  receptor AC-PKA signaling is stringently regulated by GRK- and arrestin-mediated
  homologous desensitization. Although the mechanisms regulating CRF receptor ERK-MAPK
  signaling are not yet fully understood, this pathway may potentiate BDNF-stimulated
  TrkB receptor function. (In color in Annals online.)
