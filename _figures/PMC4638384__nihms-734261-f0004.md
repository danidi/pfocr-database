caption: Therapeutic targeting of the Hippo pathway. (A) Potential roles of YAP/TAZ
  activity in tissue development and diseases. A confined window of YAP/TAZ activity
  is required for normal tissue development and homeostasis. (B) Strategies for targeting
  YAP/TAZ activity. Inhibitors for MST1/2, MAP4K4, and LATS1/2 can activate YAP/TAZ.
  YAP/TAZ-TEAD interaction may be disrupted by small molecules directly (Verteporfin)
  or AMPK activators (Metformin). Small molecules inhibiting Rho family GTPases or
  ROCK can indirectly activate LATS1/2, leading to YAP/TAZ inhibition.
