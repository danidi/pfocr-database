caption: Association between human TLRs and adaptors determine each TLR-specific signaling
  pathway. Topology of the adaptor proteins in the TIR domains of TLR2, TLR3 and TLR4
  is shown in the schema. The complex consisting of each TIR and adaptors delivers
  TLR signaling to activate NF-κB and the IFN-β promoter (IRF-3). In pDC, activation
  of TLR7 or 9 happens to activate IRF-7 in a MyD88-dependent way followed by induction
  of IFN-α. Representative ligands of TLRs are shown on the top.
