caption: Graphical summary of the anticancer mechanisms of formononetin. Formononetin
  acts on multiple signaling pathways in cancer cells. It induces apoptosis via classical
  caspase-dependent pathway and modulation of Bcl-2 family of proteins. It induces
  cell cycle arrest by modulating the cycle regulatory proteins. It inactivates signaling
  pathways, namely, JAK/STAT pathway, PI3K/AKT pathway, as well as MAPK (ERK1/2) pathways.
  Formononetin also modulates several miRNA expressions as well as supresses cell
  migration, invasion, and angiogenesis (; ; ; ; ; ; ; ; ; ; ). NAG-1, nonsteroidal
