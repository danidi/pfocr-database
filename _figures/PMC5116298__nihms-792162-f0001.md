caption: A) Apoptotic stimulus activates basolateral actomyosin contraction to squeeze
  the dying cell apically out of the epithelial layer and preserve the barrier function.
  B) During normal epithelial turnover, mechanical crowding from cell proliferation
  activates the stretch-activated channel Piezo1 to trigger some live cells to extrude.
  Live extruded cells later die by anoikis, or apoptosis due to loss of matrix-dependent
  survival signaling. The S1P/S1P2signaling pathway is critical for both apoptotic
  and live cell extrusion.
