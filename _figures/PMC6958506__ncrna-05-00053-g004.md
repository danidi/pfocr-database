  and their biological effects. Overexpression of ERBB2 promotes methylation of the
  miR-205-5p promoter via the Ras/Raf/MEK/ERK pathway which upregulates DNMTs, which
  finally results in miR-205-5p downregulation. TP53 and HES also inhibit miR-205-5p
  expression. MiR-205-5p targets different genes directly to regulate cell proliferation,
  tumor metastasis, stemness, and therapeutic resistance.
