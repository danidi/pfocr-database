caption: Schematic of the model. Together with our previous study, we demonstrates
  a model of how PLGF affects metastasis of LC. LC cells not only produce PLGF to
  activate MMP3 through an autologous way via ERK/MAPK signaling pathway, but also
  secretes PLGF to polarize macrophages into TAM in a TGFβ-receptor-signaling-dependent
  manner, which subsequently activates MMP9 in TAM. Both MMP3 from LC cells and MMP9
  from TAM enhance cancer metastasis.
