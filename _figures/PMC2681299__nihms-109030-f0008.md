  sustained ERK activation in response to PDGF (Figs. ,)(). Abl kinases also induce
  phosphorylation of Y63 and Y279 () presumably by activating unknown tyrosine kinases
  (X, Y). Phosphorylation of Y63 potentiates mitogenic signaling in fibroblasts (),
  while phosphorylation of Y279 negatively regulates ERK activation () and proliferation
  (). Abl kinases also activate a Rac/JNK pathway (), which may be dependent or independent
  of SHP-2 ()(dotted lines).
