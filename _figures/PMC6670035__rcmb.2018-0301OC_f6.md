caption: Proposed role of TGF-β1 in HASM cell contractile responses in asthma. TGF-β1
  signaling augments basal and HASM cell shortening through a Smad3, rho-associated
  protein kinase (ROCK)–dependent pathway, as previously described (). In addition
  to modulating HASM cell contractile responses, Smad2/3 activation increases PDE4D
  gene expression, leading to increased cAMP hydrolysis and blunted HASM cell relaxation
  responses. GPCR = G protein–coupled receptor; MLC20 = 20-kD myosin light chain 20;
  MLCP = myosin light-chain phosphatase; RhoA = Ras homolog gene family, member A;
  TβR-I/II = TGF-β receptor I/II.
