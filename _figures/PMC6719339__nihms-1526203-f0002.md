caption: In the canonical pathway mitogenic signals lead to the activation of CDK4/6
  complexes with D-type cyclins. These kinases initiate the phosphorylation and inactivation
  of the retinoblastoma tumor suppressor, thereby leading to the de-repression of
  E2F regulated genes. These proliferative signals can be antagonized by multiple
  anti-proliferative signals which can directly limit the activation of CDK4/6 or
  induce the expression of endogenous inhibitors exemplified by p16ink4a.
