  and Lester () and Schneiter (). Genes are shown in capital italic letters. LCB1
  encodes a subunit of the serine palmitoyl transferase, FEN1 and SUR4 encode the
  elongases and are partially redundant, SUR2 encodes a hydroxylase, SUR1 encodes
  a mannosyl transferase, and IPT1 encodes an inositol phosphotransferase. Abbreviations:
  CoA, coenzyme A; VLCFA, very long chain fatty acid; PI, phosphatidylinositol; DHS
  and PHS, sphingoid bases dihydrosphingosine and phytosphingosine, respectively.
  IPC, inositolphosphorylceramide; MIPC, mannosyl-inositolphosphorylceramide; and
  M(IP)2C, mannosyl-diinositolphosphorylceramide.'
