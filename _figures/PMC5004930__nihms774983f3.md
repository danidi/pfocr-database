caption: Schematic representation of Glut1, Runx2 and osteocalcin pathway by which
  bone formation, osteoblast differentiation and glucose metabolism are coordinated.
  Glucose is transported in osteoblasts through Glut1. Glucose uptake in osteoblasts
  inhibits AMPK activity and therefore on the one hand, favors mTORC1 pathway to promote
  protein synthesis and bone formation, on the other hand, suppresses Smurf1 activity
  to favor accumulation of Runx2. Runx2 promotes osteoblast differentiation and up-regulates
  the expression of Osteocalcin to favor glucose metabolism.
