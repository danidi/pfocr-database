caption: The conflicting metabolic alterations in CSCs. In a hypoxic environment (blue),
  CSCs promote glycolysis through the HIF1a/PDK1-dependent axis, GLUT1 elevation,
  and EMT phenotype and proliferate by activating the Akt pathway. Moreover, CSCs
  upregulate the level of the antioxidant glutathione using PPP. On the other hand,
  in a normoxic environment (red), CSCs enhance mitochondrial OXPHOS by increasing
  IMP2 or PGC-1a. ROS produced by OXPHOS promotes CSC proliferation by activating
  HIF1a or mTORC1. Moreover, the interaction with stromal niche cells enhances OXPHOS
  in CSCs.
