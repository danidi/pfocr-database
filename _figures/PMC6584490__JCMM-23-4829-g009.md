  nitroprusside (SNP) in the HUVECs. DMY treatment activates PI3K/AKT/ FoxO3a pathway

  oxide donor, creates oxidase stress and subsequently down‐regulates PI3K/AKT/FoxO3a
  pathway. Additionally, DMY may modulate others signal pathway such as mTOR, STAT3/NF‐κB
  to antiapoptosis in the HUVECs
