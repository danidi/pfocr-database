caption: The Wnt protein binds to its Frizzled receptors activating Dishevelled followed
  by the inhibition of GSK-3β (glycogen synthase kinase-3β), Axin and APC (adenomatous
  polyposis coli) tumor suppressor protein complex. The GSK-3β, Axin and APC complex
  blocks phosphorylation of ß-catenin. β-catenin translocates to the cell nucleus
  and contributes to the formation of Lef/Tcf (lymphocyte enhancer factor/T cell factor)
  and β-catenin complex that can lead to cellular proliferation, differentiation,
  and survival. The binding of Wnt to Frizzled receptors also can activate Akt that
  prevents the activity of GSK-3β, prevents Mito (mitochondrial) Cyto c (cytochrome
  c) release, caspase activity, and PCD that involves DNA fragmentation and phosphatidylserine
  (PS) exposure.
