  a curated cancer signaling pathway taken from the TCGA's original Mondrian plugin
  to Cytoscape (Cytoscape Consortium; https://cytoscape.org/). The node color represents

  data downloaded from TCGA. The colors represent the fold change for subtype 3 of

  associations. GBM, glioblastoma multiforme; PPI, protein–protein interaction.
