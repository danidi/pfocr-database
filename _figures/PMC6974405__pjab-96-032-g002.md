caption: piRNA biogenesis pathways in the Drosophila ovarian soma (left panel) and
  germ cell (right panel). In the soma, Yb assembles Piwi/Armi/SoYb/Vret in a Yb body.
  Piwi and Armi then translocate onto mitochondria, where Zuc/Gasz/Daed process the
  Piwi-bound piRNA production. In the germ cell, the ping-pong cycle operates in nuage.
  Upon slicing by Aub, piRNA precursors bound to AGO3 are unwound by Vasa and then
  trimmed by Nuclease. Phased piRNA production is induced by the ping-pong cycle.
