  serve to phosphorylate S/T and Y residues on Raf. Some of these phosphorylation
  events serve to enhance Raf activity (shown by a black P in a white circle) whereas
  others serve to inhibit Raf activity (shown by a white P in a black circle. Moreover
  there are phosphatases such as PP2A, which remove phosphates on certain regulatory
  residues. The downstream transcription factors regulated by this pathway are indicated
  in diamond shaped outlines.
