caption: Proposed catabolic model for C. thermarum TA2.A1. The model consists of an
  Embden–Meyenhoff–Parnas type glycolysis (see Figure S1 for details), and a tricarboxylic
  acid (TCA) cycle, including a one-step shunt to connect glutamate to the TCA cycle.
  It also shows fermentative routes towards acetate and lactate. Furthermore, the
  glutamate uptake system is shown, and a pathway to alkalize local environment using
  NH4+. The parallel lines indicate membrane-bound proteins; MQ/MQH2 is the membrane-bound
  electron carrier menaquinone used in the electron transfer chain (see Fig. )
