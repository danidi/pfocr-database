caption: Apoptosis intrinsic (endoplasmic reticulum (ER)) pathway. Schematic representation
  of the intrinsic pathway of apoptosis activation in the the endoplasmic reticulum.
  The main stimulus for this pathway is the misfolding of proteins and their subsequent
  accumulation in the endoplasmic reticulum. When the misfolded proteins reach a critical
  concentration, ER-located sensors (protein kinase RNA-like endoplasmic reticulum
  kinase (PERK), inositol-requiring protein 1 (IRE1a) and activating transcription
  factor 6 (ATF6)) will induce proteolytic degradation and a decrease in protein synthesis.
