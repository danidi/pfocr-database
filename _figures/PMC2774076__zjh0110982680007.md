  Janus tyrosine kinase (JAK), STAT, signal transducer and activator of transcription;
  AMPK, AMP-activated protein kinase. (Drawn from data and concepts presented in Nakamura
  et al, Liu and Simon, van den Beucken et al, Simon et al, Li et al,,  and this study).
  sFLT-1, soluble fms-like tyrosine kinase-1; sTrkB, soluble neurotrophic tyrosine
