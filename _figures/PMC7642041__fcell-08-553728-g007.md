caption: The molecular mechanism of GP treatment mediating oxidative stress in mice
  with depression-like behavior via the Six3os1/Fezf1/AKT. In CUMS-induced mice, miR-511-3p
  targets and inhibits Fezf1, thus stimulating oxidative stress of neurons. After
  GP treatment, Six3os1 expression is upregulated, which increases Fezf1 expression
  by binding to miR-511-3p and then activates AKT signaling pathway, thus alleviating
  oxidative stress in mice with depression-like behavior.
