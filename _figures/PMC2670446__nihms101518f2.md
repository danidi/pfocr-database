  GR, and PPARγ2 have been reported. PKA mediated phosphorylation of CREB leads to
  CREB binding to cyclic AMP response elements (CREs) within the mitochondrial genome
  that promotes neuronal survival. Alongside p53-dependent transcription from mitochondrial
  genome may also result in neuronal cell death. It is well established that p53 and
  Nur-77 complexes with Bcl-xL and Bcl-2 present on mitochondrial membrane which subsequently
  triggers the downstream cascade of apoptosis via mitochondrial transcription independent
  pathway.
