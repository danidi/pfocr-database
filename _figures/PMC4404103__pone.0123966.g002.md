  glycolysis pathway (A), citric acid (TCA) cycle (B) and two mitochondrial shuttles
  (C; the mitochondrial malate, SLC25A11, and aspartate, SLC25A13, transporters were
  not affected). The blue lines indicate the sarcolemma (A) or the mitochondrial membrane
  (C). Metabolites and glycolytic intermediates are shown in black font; gene names
  are italicized. Arrows indicate an increase (↑,⬆) or decrease (↓,⬇) in gene expression
  due to olanzapine infusion; bold arrows indicate a larger magnitude of change. Gray
  font and equal sign (=) indicates no significant change. Colored font indicates
  a change predicted to oppose (red) or promote (green) the pathway. Where the abundance
  of two isoforms is different, the font size is smaller for the isoform where the
  FPKM has a lower value ().
