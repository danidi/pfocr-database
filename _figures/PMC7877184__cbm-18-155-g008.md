caption: Schematic model of morphine-3-glucuronide (M3G) promoting non-small cell
  lung cancer (NSCLC) immune escape via toll-like receptor 4 (TLR4) and programmed
  death ligand 1 (PD-L1). The combination of M3G and TLR4 activates the PI3K pathway
  and induces the upregulation of PD-L1 in NSCLC cells. This will further increase
  the expression level of TIM-3, reduce the release of IFN-γ, and damage the cytotoxicity
  of CD8+ T cells, leading to immune escape of the NSCLC cells.
