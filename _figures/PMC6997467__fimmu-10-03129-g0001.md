caption: Signaling pathway of ncRNAs in RA. NcRNAs (miRNA, lncRNA, circRNA) are involved
  in regulating inflammation and autoimmunity, such as immune cell activation, differentiation,
  and polarization. Some ncRNAs are encapsulated in nanovesicles and exert critical
  effects on inflammatory and immune cells, and some can function as ceRNA by sponging
  miRNAs in RA. NcRNAs participate in RA inflammation and autoimmune disorders primarily
  through TLR4/NF-κB, MAPK/PI3K, and Wnt3a/β-catenin signaling pathways, and so on.
