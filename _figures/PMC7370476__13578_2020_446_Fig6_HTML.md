  its transcriptional activity by recruiting the TIF1β co-repressor and the histone
  deacetylase HDAC1–mSin3 complex. d Inhibition of c-Myc activity by MM-1 can be lost
  due to the missense mutation of amino acid 157 from alanine to arginine (A157R)
