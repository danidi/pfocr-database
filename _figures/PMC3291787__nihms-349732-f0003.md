  with transcription factors E2F1 and STAT3 through importins, leading to up-regulation
  of B-Myb, iNOS and Cyclin D1. HER2 interacts with importin 2 and is internalized,
  leading to the expression of Cox-2, PRPK, MMP-16 and PDX-10. HER4 interacts with
  STAT5a and the C-terminal fragment alone accumulates in the nucleus after undergoing
  gama secreatase mediated cleavage.
