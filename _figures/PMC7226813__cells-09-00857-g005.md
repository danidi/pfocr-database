caption: Differential roles of JNK1 and JNK2 in skin cancers. In BCC, JNK1/2 activates
  Jun/Fos, and enhances their interaction with phosphorylated ATF2, which then enhances
  SHH/Gli induced tumorigenesis. In SCC, MKK4/7 activates JNK1 and JNK2. JNK1 induces
  apoptosis, whereas JNK2 promotes carcinogenesis in an AP1-dependent manner. SCCA1
  promotes SCC via inhibition of JNK1 and CYLD inhibits SCC via suppression of JNK2/AP1
  cascade. In melanoma, the MALT1, MKK4/7, and JNK/AP1 signaling cascade promotes
  melanoma cell proliferation and migration, whereas CYLD inhibits it.
