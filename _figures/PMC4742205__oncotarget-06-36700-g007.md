caption: N-myc amplified NB promotes aggressive tumor growth and correlates with a
  poor prognosis in patients []. Here, we identified that both the Shh signaling pathway
  and N-myc activate a novel downstream target gene, INSM1 that is crucial for the
  stability of N-myc protein accumulation through PI3K/AKT/GSK3β signaling pathways.
  This positive-feedback loop via the INSM1 expression enhances NB cell invasion and
  oncogenesis suggesting that the INSM1 NE factor is important in human NB transformation.
