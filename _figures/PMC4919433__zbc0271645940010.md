  triggers platelet activation via PAR1 and PAR4 and then intracellular signaling
  via Src tyrosine kinases, MEK, MAPK, PLC, and intracellular Ca2+. Activation of
  cPLA2 leads to AA release, which is then oxidized via COX-1 forming DXA3. Free DXA3
  is secreted to interact with neighboring cells, including neutrophils.
