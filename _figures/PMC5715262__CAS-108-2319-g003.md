caption: Molecular pathways around CIC in cancer. (a) Upregulation of downstream genes
  are achieved at the physiological level (top), CIC mutations (middle), or CIC fusion
  (bottom) at various levels. (b) Relationship between inhibitory drugs in the receptor
  tyrosine kinase (RTK)/RAS/MAPK pathway and the ataxin 1 like (ATXN1L)/CIC axis.
  Mutations within the pathway downregulate CIC (i), and tyrosine kinase inhibitors
  (TKI) and trametinib inhibit the downregulation (ii). When expression of ATXN1L
  or CIC is significantly reduced, the effects are cancelled (iii).
