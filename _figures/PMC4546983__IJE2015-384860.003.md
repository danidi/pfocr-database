caption: Pathway in males that leads to growth factor activation of PI3K/AKT of AR
  and GRβ induced growth. Growth factors (e.g., insulin, epidermal growth factor)
  bind to receptor tyrosine kinases (RTK) increasing phosphorylation and activation
  of insulin receptor substrate (IRS), resulting in the induction of the PI3Kinase
  growth pathway. PTEN is a tumor suppressor gene that inhibits PI3K. The PI3K increases
  activity of AKT resulting in enhanced AR signaling and growth. Proinflammatory mediators
  increase expression of GRβ, which has been shown to inhibit the tumor suppressor
  gene, PTEN [], which leads to enhanced AR induced growth [].
