caption: Growth Factor (GF) ligands will induce Growth Factor Receptor (GFR) signaling,
  resulting in phosphorylation of PR at ser294 by the MAPK pathway, leading to both
  ligand-independent receptor activation and induction of proteosome-mediated PR degradation,
  and/or may lead to direct suppression of PR transcription by the Akt/mTor pathway
  (with a resultant decrease in PR protein expression). Such a pathway might explain
  why low PR in lung tumors was associated with worse survival, because it is indicative
  of high levels of GFR siginaling.
