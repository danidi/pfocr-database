caption: The Hippo pathway controls the migration of border cell clusters through
  two mechanisms. In outer border cells, the Hippo pathway regulates polarized distribution
  of F-actin and controls border cell migration. In polar cells, the Hippo pathway
  induces upd expression. Upd from polar cells activates JAK/STAT signaling in outer
  border cells. Activation of JAK/STAT signaling induces slbo expression, which promotes
  the formation and migration of border cell clusters. The Hippo pathway also controls
  polar cell proliferation.
