caption: TLRs signal activation of the MyD88-dependent pathway from the plasma membrane
  (TLR2, TLR4, TLR5) or from endosomes (TLR7, TLR9) to recruit adapter/kinase complexes
  involving MyD88-IRAKs-TRAF6-TAK1 to mediate activation of NF-κB and expression of
  pro-inflammatory cytokines and chemokines. TLR7 and TLR9 also utilize the MyD88-IRAK4/1
  module to phosphorylate and activate IRF7, leading to induction of type I IFNs.
  TLR3 and TLR4 employ TRIF to signal from endosomes and to trigger induction of type
  I IFNs via engagement of the TRAF3/TBK1/IKK-e that phosphorylate and activate IRF3,
  and signal induction of pro-inflammatory cytokines by RIP-1-mediated activation
  of TRAF6-TAK1 module to activate MAPKs and NF-κB.
