caption: ROS are important contributor to renal injury and inflammation. These ROS,
  when produced in excess cause oxidative stress. This leads to dissociation of TXNIP
  (Thioredoxin interacting protein) from Trx1 (Thioredoxin), binding of TXNIP to NLRP3,
  and inflammasome activation. The activated inflammasome leads to production of caspase-1,
  IL-1β, and IL-18 and associated inflammatory response. The NFkB pathway also plays
  an important role in production of cytokines, chemokines and adhesion molecules.
  The pathways are interrelated with cross talks between them.
