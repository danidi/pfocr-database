  negative values were depicted in blue and positive values in red. The box and whisker
  plot on the left summarizes the distribution of all gene perturbations in this pathway,
  the box represents the first quartile, median and third quartile, while circles
  represent outliers. Akt serine/threonine kinase, Akt; phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase,
  PI3K.'
