  on to KEGG Axon guidance pathway (A), Regulation of actin cytoskeleton (B) and Adherens
  junction (C) pathways and visualized manually. Boxes in color represent protein
  coding genes that were present within the enriched GO terms. The gradient of the
  color represents their fold change. Grey boxes represent protein coding genes not
  present in the enriched GO terms. Dotted lines represent individual modules within
  the parent pathway. Pathway components present on double grey lines represent cell
