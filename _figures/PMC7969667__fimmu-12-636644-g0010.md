caption: A schematic model of RipA depicting possible interaction of RipA with surface
  immune receptor TLR4 and emanating downstream cascades. After interaction with TLR4,
  RipA activates a downstream PI3K-AKT-mTORC1 signaling pathway that ultimately culminates
  in the repression of autophagy and apoptosis. RipA recognition by TLR4 activates
  canonical NFκB signaling pathways and induces the production of pro-inflammatory
  cytokines. RipA also inhibits the production of oxidative phosphorylation enzymes
  resulting in metabolic reprogramming.
