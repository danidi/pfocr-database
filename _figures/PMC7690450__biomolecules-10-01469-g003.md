  dysfunction through the upregulation of Bax and downregulation of Bcl-2. While curcumin
  also induces the Fas signaling pathway, doxorubicin does not, since it does not
  upregulate the ligand needed by the signaling pathway to proceed (Fas-L). Ultimately,
  the pathways lead to the activation of caspases and apoptosis.
