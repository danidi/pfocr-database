caption: 'Spectrum of RAS-MAPK pathway disease genes and associated RASopathies. Proteins
  having roles in the RAS-MAPK are shown, and the ones for which pathogenic variation
  in the encoding genes results in one or more RASopathies are indicated. The proteins
  depicted within the boxes largely reside at the cell membrane (indicated with the
  schematic of lipid bilayers, except LZTR1, which is associated with the Golgi apparatus).
  The proteins shown outside of the boxes principally reside in the cytosol. Abbreviations:
  CFCS, cardiofaciocutaneous syndrome; CS, Costello syndrome; NF1, neurofibromatosis
  type 1; NFNS, neurofibromatosis-Noonan syndrome; NS, Noonan syndrome; NSLAH, Noonan
  syndrome with loose anlagen hair; NSML, Noonan syndrome with multiple lentigines;
  NS-like JMML, Noonan syndrome-like disorder with juvenile myelomonocytic leukemia;
  RTK, Receptor tyrosine kinases. Copyright, Ni-ka Ford, Icahn School of Medicine
  at Mount Sinai'
