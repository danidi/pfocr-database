caption: Scheme of sTWEAK-induced activation of the alternative NFκB pathway. In non-stimulated
  cells, cytosolic TRAF2 and the cIAPs are engaged in NIK degradation, thus in suppression
  of the alternative NFκB pathway (left panel). Recruitment of TRAF2 and cIAPs to
  Fn14, irrespective whether triggered by soluble TWEAK or membrane TWEAK, results
  in reduced cytosolic TRAF2 and cIAP levels and thus reduced suppression of NIK degradation
  resulting in the activation of the alternative NFκB pathway by accumulated constitutively
  active NIK.
