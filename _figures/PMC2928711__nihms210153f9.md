caption: Leptin-induced upregulation of VEGF expression in MT mainly involves the
  activation of HIF-1α and NFκB through leptin canonic (PI-3K/AKT1 and MAPK/ERK1)
  and non-canonic (JNK, p38 and to less extent PKC) signalling pathways. However,
  leptin-induced SP1 through leptin canonic signalling pathways (JAK2/STAT3, MAPK
  and PI-3K) signalling pathway is also involved in VEGF gene regulation in 4T1 cells.
  Leptin activation of PKC may be upstream to MAPK/ERK and p38 kinases that in turn
  activate HIF-1α and NFκB for the regulation of VEGF expression in MT.
