caption: Ras signaling pathways. Under physiological conditions, Ras forms nanoclusters
  and activates the Raf/MEK/ERK (MAPK) pathway, leading to cell proliferation. In
  the PI3K/Akt pathway, both Ras and RTK activate PI3K, recruiting it to the plasma
  membrane, leading to cell growth. Under oncogenic conditions, a constitutively active
  Ras (KRas, HRas or NRas) cluster boosts Raf/MEK/ERK signaling. In adenocarcinoma
  of KRAS-driven cancer, CaM specifically recruits oncogenic KRas4B (and possibly
  KRas4A, but not HRas or NRas) from the membrane to PI3Kα and substitutes for the
  missing RTK signal, consequently fully activating the PI3Kα/Akt/mTOR pathway.
