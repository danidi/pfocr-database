caption: 'The signalling pathways initiated downstream of VCAM-1 binding with α4β1
  integrin. The binding of the leucocyte to VCAM-1 and its complex ezrin-moesin (ERM)
  activates Rac-1. Rac-1 induces two pathways: (1) reactive oxygen species (ROSs),
  which activate matrix metalloproteinases (MMPs), protein kinase Cα (PKCα) and protein
  tyrosine phosphatase 1B (PTP1B) and which finally lead to the disruption of the
  endothelial cells’ (ECs) junctions and the (2) p21-activated protein kinase (PAK)-myosin
  light chain (MLC) pathway, which leads to actin remodelling.'
