  increased signal of c-Jun and AP1 in 3T3-L1 after co-culture, representing one of
  the pathways activated by WNT5a up-regulation. The activation of WNT5a receptor
  and downstream signaling is prevented by the binding with its inhibitor SFRP5. Abbreviations:

  5.'
