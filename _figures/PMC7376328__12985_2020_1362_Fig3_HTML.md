caption: Viruses inhibit the antiviral effects of PKR. Viral dsRNA activates PKR,
  and activated PKR inhibits viral protein synthesis by phosphorylating eIF2α (green
  arrow). However, viruses have evolved many escape mechanisms (red arrows), such
  as hiding dsRNA (HSV-1 [, ]), blocking PKR activation (PRRSV [], EMCV [], IAV [,
  ], and MERS-CoV []), competing for PKR phosphorylation substrates (myxoma virus
  []), degrading PKR by the lysosome or proteasome pathway (MAV-1 [] and FMDV [])
  and cleaving PKR via viral proteases (EV71 [])
