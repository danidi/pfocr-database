caption: 'Metallothionein control in FcεRΙ-mediated IL-4 production in basophils.
  FcεRΙ stimulation activates basophils via signaling pathways that increase IL-4
  production. Before stimulation, cytoplasmic free zinc ion inhibits CN. After FcεRΙ
  stimulation, cytoplasmic free zinc ion can induce the expression of metallothionein
  1/2 and metallothioneins can bind to cytoplasmic free zinc ion. As a result, CN/NFAT
  signaling pathway can be activated and leads to increased IL-4 expression. IgE:
  immunoglobulin E; FcεRΙ: high-affinity receptor for IgE; ER: endoplasmic reticulum;
  Zn: zinc; MT-1/2: metallothionein 1/2; Ca: calcium; CN: calcineurin; NFAT: nuclear
  factor of activated T cells; IL-4: interleukin-4.'
