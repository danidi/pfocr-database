caption: miR-26a pathway in the initiation of ASMC hypertrophy. Up-regulation of miR-26a
  in ASMCsDes−/− and reHASMCs, which stably suppress endogenous desmin expression,
  increases global cellular protein synthesis through inhibition of GSK-3β protein
  expression. As a result, ASMC hypertrophic marker proteins (α-actin, SM22, and MHC)
  are up-regulated, which in turn initiates hypertrophy.
