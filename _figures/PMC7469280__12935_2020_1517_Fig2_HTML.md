caption: TRAF6 mediates signaling transduction pathway. TRAF6 participates in multiple
  signaling pathways. In the MyD88-dependent pathway of TLR4 signaling pathway, TRAF6
  interacts with IRAK and forms the IRAK-TRAF6-TAK1-TAB 1TAB 2 complex and subsequently
  activates the MAPKs (ERK1, ERK2, p38, and JNK). Ultimately, NF-κB and AP-1 are activated.
  In the MyD88-independent pathway, TRIF recruits TRAF6 and activates TAK1 to induce
  NF-κB activation. In RANK signaling pathway, activated RANK interacts with TRAF6
  and thereby mediates activation of PI3K-AKT and NF-κB. Akt can phosphorylate Bcl-xL/Bcl-2
  associated death promoter (Bad) and Forkhead family members, thus inhibiting apoptosis.
  In addition, TRAF6 interacts with ECSIT protein results in increasing levels of
  ROS
