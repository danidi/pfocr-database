caption: Activation of the Hypoxia Inducible Factor (HIF) pathway in response to hypoxia.
  HIF-α levels are controlled in normoxia by PHD-mediated hydroxylation (OH) and recognition
  by the E3-ligase complex containing the tumour suppressor pVHL. In hypoxia, Prolyl
  Hydroxylases (PHDs) and FIH are inhibited and HIF-α escapes VHL-dependent degradation.
  T-bars represent repression.
