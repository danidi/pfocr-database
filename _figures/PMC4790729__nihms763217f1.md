caption: 'IGF-1 acts on its receptor to initiate several pathways responsible for
  protein translation, which in turn aids in cell growth, migration and differentiation.
  Activation of the PI3K/Akt/mTOR pathway by IGF-1 leads to activation of S6 kinase
  proteins and phosphorylation of eIF4E, which in turn lead to increased mRNA translation
  (). IGF-1 also activates the MAPK pathway, which increases neuronal proliferation
  via ERK activation and increases gene transcription via ELK1 activation (()Abbreviations:
  IGF-1, insulin-like growth factor 1; IRS-1, insulin receptor substrate 1, PI3K.,
