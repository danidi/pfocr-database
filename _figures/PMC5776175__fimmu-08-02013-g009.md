caption: Model for Brucella Omp25 modulation of tumor necrosis factor (TNF)-α suppression
  in porcine and murine macrophages. Brucella Omp25 inhibits LPS-induced TNF-α at
  the transcriptional levels via miR-146a (in both porcine and murine macrophages),
  or miR-351-5p (in murine macrophages) by targeting TRAF6 or IRAK1 to inhibit the
  activation of NF-κB signaling pathway. At the posttranscriptional levels, Brucella
  Omp25 inhibits LPS-induced TNF-α via upregulating miR-181a and miR-301a-3p (in both
  porcine and murine macrophages), or miR-130a-3p (in porcine macrophages), or miR-351-5p
  (in murine macrophages).
