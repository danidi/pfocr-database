  0.01) were considered. Blue, glycolysis; orange, PPP; pink, TCA. Arrow colors indicate

  FC > 1.0, light red: 1.0 > FC > 0.5); gray: no significant changes; orange: variable
  changes within an enzyme complex. Values shown inside the small boxes indicate the
  fold change in the amount of each metabolite between both cell lines (). Stars depict
  changes in the prevailing isoform. Steps associated with NADP reduction are indicated'
