caption: 1,25(OH)2D3 intracellular signalling pathway. In response to 1,25(OH)2D treatment
  viramin D receptor (VDR) interacts with phosphoinositide 3 (PI3) -kinase, its downstream
  element Akt kinase and with Src kinase. The expression of VDR in the cells increases
  in response to 1,25(OH)2D3. It is unknown if VDR is translocated to the cell nucleus
  in response to 1,25(OH)2D, or if it is continuously shuttled between cytosol and
  nucleus or if 1,25(OH)2D traps it inside the nucleus. In the presence of 1,25(OH)2D3,
  VDR/RXR heterodimers can displace DNA-bound nuclear factor of activated T cells.
