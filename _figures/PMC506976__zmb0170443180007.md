  activated by the p85-associated PI 3-kinase, induces recruitment of Ras to the ERα/Src/p85/PKCζ

  3-kinase- and Ras-dependent kinase cascades leads to increased cyclin D1 transcription
  and decreased p27 nuclear localization, respectively. These events are required
  for the G1/S transition of MCF-7 cells.
