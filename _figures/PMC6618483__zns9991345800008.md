caption: Model describing Etv4 and Etv5 induction following distal NGF stimulation.
  NGF binds TrkA at axon terminals and forms signaling endosomes that are transported
  to the CB. There, TrkA activates MEK/ERK signaling pathway, inducing diverse transcription
  factors, which are involved in survival and axonal extension. Among them are Etv4
  and Etv5, which mediate MMP (i.e., MMP3 and MMP13) expression and axonal growth.
