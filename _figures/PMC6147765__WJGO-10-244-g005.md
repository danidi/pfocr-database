caption: 'TGF/Smad pathway. TGFβ signalling is initiated via the binding of the TGFβ1
  ligand to receptor II, which promotes dimerization between receptor II and receptor
  I and the subsequent transphosphorylation of TGFβRI. Activated TGFβRI therein phosphorylates
  and activates receptor-regulated Smads (Smad2 and Smad3). Phosphorylated Smads,
  along with co-Smads, form a trimeric complex and translocate to the nucleus to induce
  the transcription of target genes and promote cell growth and survival. TGF: Transforming
  growth factor.'
