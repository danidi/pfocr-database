caption: TLR4 Signaling Pathway. LPS-induced activation of TLR4 is mediated by CD14.
  CD14 delivers LPS to TLR4/MD-2 complex and downstream signaling is divided into
  MyD88-dependent and independent signaling. MyD88-dependent signaling results in
  the rapid activation of NF-κB and the induction of pro-inflammatory cytokines, while
  MyD88-independent signaling results in late activation of NF-κB and induction of
  type I interferons.
