caption: 'Immunologic synapse. This schematic depicts T cell activation and how it
  is influenced by expression of common susceptibility genes. Shared susceptibility
  genes for autoimmune thyroid disease and type 1 diabetes are involved in the immunological
  synapse. HLA-DR molecules present autoantigens to T cells, CTLA-4 expression suppresses
  T cell activation, PTPN22 expression negatively influences the T cell receptor (TCR)
  signaling pathway and FOXP3 expression regulates the differentiation of regulatory
  T cells (modified according to ref.[124]). APC: Antigen presenting cell; CTLA-4:
  Cytotoxic T lymphocyte antigen 4; HLA: Human leukocyte antigen; PTPN22: Protein
  tyrosine phosphatase non-receptor type 22; FOXP3: Forkhead box protein P3.'
