caption: Postulated interaction between enzastaurin and erlotinib in the EGFR pathway.
  Upon stimulation several downstream pathways such as the PI3K/AKT and RAS pathway
  are activated. These pathways are involved in the regulation of cell survival, proliferation
  and apoptosis. The EGFR signaling pathway can be inhibited by erlotinib, which blocks
  EGFR autophosphorylation, a critical process for the kinase activation, leading
  to increased apoptosis and decreased cell proliferation.
