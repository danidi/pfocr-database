caption: 'The viruses mainly modulate STAT3 signaling through the IL-6/IL-6R/gp130/JAKs
  cascade. STAT3 signaling, sometimes triggered by other cytokines, growth factor
  and tyrosine kinase, are also modified by several viruses. Furthermore, the downstream
  components of the IL-6/STAT3 cascade can interact with the Ras/Raf/MEK/MAPKs and
  PI3K/Akt/mTOR pathways. The details about how the viruses regulate STAT3 signaling
  can be seen in the main text. Main pathways modified by the viruses are indicated
  with the bold arrow; the broken line indicates that the effects are mainly mediated
  by in an indirect way. The HBV- and HCV-induced effects on STAT3 signaling are specifically
  indicated in the red and orange colors, respectively.ER: Endoplasmic reticulum;
  Mt: Mitochondria; RABV: Rabies virus; ROS: Reactive oxygen species; RSV: Reproductive
  and respiratory syndrome virus; SOCS: Suppressor of cytokine signaling; TF: Transcription
  factor; TMEV: Theiler''s murine encephalomyelitis virus.'
