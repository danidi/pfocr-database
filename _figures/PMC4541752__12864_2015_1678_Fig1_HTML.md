  the central steps of the pathway. Known single enzymatic steps in each cell type

  respectively. DXS, 1-deoxy-D-xylulose-5-phosphate (DXP) synthase; DXR, DXP reductoisomerase;
  CMS, 4-(cytidine 5′-diphospho)-2C-methyl-D-erythritol (CM) synthase; CMK, CM kinase;

  IO, iridoid oxidase; IS, iridoid synthase; 7DLGT, 7-deoxyloganetic acid glucosyltransferase;
