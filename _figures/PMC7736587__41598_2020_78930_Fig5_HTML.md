caption: 'Central metabolic pathway map and comparative visualization of involved
  metabolites after MCAO. Differences in the brain levels of metabolites among the
  control, MCAO-vehicle, MCAO-CD34+ cell, and MCAO-MSC groups are presented in a bar
  graph. MCAO decreased TCA intermediates and neurotransmitter metabolites, while
  MCAO increased metabolites from the amino acid and urea cycles. Data indicate the
  mean ± the S.E.M. of five mice for each group. *p < 0.05 versus the control group.
  #p < 0.05 versus the MCAO-vehicle group. G1P, glucose-1-phosphate; G6P, glucose-6-phosphate;
