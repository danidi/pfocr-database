caption: Schematic representing intrinsic and extrinsic apoptosis initiation. When
  activated, p53 activates Bax and Bak, then Suma and Nova, leading to Apaf1 cleavage
  of pro-caspase 9 into active caspase 9. Bcl-2, along with BclXL and MCL1, can downregulate
  the activation of cytochrome C, halting proapoptotic signals from the intrinsic
  pathway. Tumor necrosis factor (TNF)-related apoptosis-inducing ligand (TRAIL) and
  potentially other activation methods of extrinsic apoptosis remain active in synovial
  sarcoma.
