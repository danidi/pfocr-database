caption: α-IPM synthesis occurs in the mitochondrion, although a small amount of Leu4
  is present in the cytosol. Leucine allosterically inhibits IPM synthase (Leu4/9).
  α-IPM activates the transcription factor Leu3. Enzymes boxed in red contain Fe-S
  clusters. Deletion of Leu2 leads to accumulation of α-IPM. AALD, active acetaldehyde;
  PYR, pyruvate; AL, acetolactate; DHIV, α,β-dihydroxyisovalerate; KIV, α-ketoisovalerate;
