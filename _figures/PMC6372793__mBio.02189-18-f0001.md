  the acs operon, MetF, FolD, FchA, and Fhs; TCA, citric acid cycle; FDH, formate
  dehydrogenase; Hase, hydrogenase; NDH-1, NADH dehydrogenase 1; LDH, lactate dehydrogenase.'
