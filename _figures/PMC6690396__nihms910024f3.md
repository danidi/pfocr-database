caption: Histone methylation-dependent DDR mechanisms. (A) KDM5A-mediated H3K4me3
  demethylation is required for the DDR pathway regulated by the ZMYND8-NuRD complex
  to promote homologous recombination (HR) repair. (B) Trimethylation on H3K36 (H3K36me3)
  catalyzed by SETD2 provides a binding site for LEDGF and MSH6, which promote HR
  and mismatch repair (MMR) respectively. (C) Bivalent recognition of H2AK15Ub and
  H4K20me2 by 53BP1 facilitates non-homologous end-joining (NHEJ) and blocks BRCA1
  to limit HR repair.
