caption: 'Pathway analysis of gene expression profile. Ten differentially expressed
  genes were found to be members of the calcium signaling pathway and are illustrated
  here according to KEGG’s prototype. Rectangular boxes represent genes. Colored boxes
  represent one or more significantly changed genes; red boxes represent increased
  expression, and blue blue boxes represent decreased expression (GluR1 -/- vs. WT).
  Additional outside rectangular boxes indicate that the result was also observed
  in GluR1 +/- mice. Circled numbers are added beside highlighted boxes to index the
  corresponding genes that were significantly changed in the microarray analysis.
  These genes are: 1) calcium channel, voltage-dependent, L type, alpha 1S subunit;
  2) voltage-dependent alpha-1 E calcium channel; 3) calcium channel, voltage-dependent,
  T type, alpha 1G subunit; 4) calcium channel, voltage-dependent, alpha 2/delta subunit
  2; 5) glutamate receptor, ionotropic, NMDA1; 6) neurotensin receptor; 7) sarco/endoplasmic
  reticulum calcium-ATPase, SERCA3c isoform; 8) myosin light chain kinase; 9) calcium/calmodulin-dependent
  protein kinase II alpha; and 10) PTK2 protein tyrosine kinase 2 beta.Figure abbreviations
  (in alphabetical order): ADCY: adenylate cyclase; cADPR: cyclic ADP-ribose; ANT:
  adenine nucleotide translocator; CALM: calmodulin; CAMK: calcium/calmodulin-dependent
  protein kinase; cAMP: cyclic adenosine mono-phosphate; CD38: CD38 antigen; CaN:
  calcineurin; CaV: calcium channel, voltage-dependent; DAG: diglyceride; FAK: focal
  adhesion kinase; GPCR: G-protein coupled receptor; Gq: G-protein, Gq class; Gs:
  guanine nucleotide binding protein, stimulating; IP3: inositol 1,4,5-trisphosphate;
  IP3 3K: inositol 1,4,5-trisphosphate 3-kinase; IP3R: inositol 1,4,5-triphosphate
  receptor; MLCK: myosin light chain kinase; NCX: Na+-calcium+ exchanger; NOS: nitric
  oxide synthase; PDE: phosphodiesterase; PHK: phosphorylase kinase; PKA: protein
  kinase A; PKC: protein kinase C; PLC: phospholipase C; PLN: phospholamban; PMCA:

  kinase; ROC: receptor-operated channel; RYR: ryanodine receptor; SERCA: sarcoplasmic/endoplasmic
