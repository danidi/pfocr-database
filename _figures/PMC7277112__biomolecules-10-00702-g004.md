caption: Representative model of the NRF2 signaling pathway activation in myotonic
  dystrophy 1 (DM1), based on literature evidences. Brain-derived neurotrophic factor
  (BDNF) activation of PI3K/AKT pathway determines the inhibitory phosphorylation
  of GSK3β by blocking NRF2/KEAP-1-indipendent degradation. As BDNF is a NRF2 target,
  this can start a positive feedback contributing to NRF2 activation. At the same
  time, the transcription of NRF2 antioxidant target genes reduces oxidative stress
  in DM1 cells and the pro-inflammatory cytokine IL-1β levels.
