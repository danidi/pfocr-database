caption: Schematic model of microRNA‐27b (miR‐27b)‐mediated regulation of colon cancer
  tumor progression. In normal cells expressing miR‐27b, expression of paxillin and
  ARFGEF1 is limited to avoid unregulated cell growth and/or motility. Once miR‐27b
  expression is downregulated due to activation of the Ras/Src/PI3K pathway, expression
  of paxillin and ARFGEF1 is elevated, resulting in rapid cell growth and stimulation
  of cell adhesion and invasion.
