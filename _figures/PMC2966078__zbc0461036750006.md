caption: A model to demonstrate the inhibitory role of p15RS on the canonical Wnt
  signaling pathway. In the quiescent state (without Wnt), p15RS interacts with TCF4
  on the promoters of Wnt-target genes and inhibits transcription. When Wnt signaling
  is activated, β-catenin translocates into the nucleus to interact with p15RS thereafter
  removing p15RS from its association with TCF4, allowing β-catenin·TCF4 binding to
  the promoter to initiate gene transcription.
