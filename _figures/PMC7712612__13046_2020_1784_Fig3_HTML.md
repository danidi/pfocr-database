caption: MIR22HG is involved in signaling pathways that affect cancer progression.
  a MIR22HG negatively regulates the Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway by downregulating
  the expression of β-catenin, cyclin D1 and c-myc to inhibit cell proliferation and
  migration in CCA. b MIR22HG blocks the SMAD complex, preventing its binding to the
  promoter of SNAI1 and further suppressing the EMT signaling pathway. c MIR22HG inhibits
  the Notch signaling pathway by downregulating nuclear Notch2 and HEY1 expression
  in GC. d MIR22HG activates the STAT3 signaling pathway to promote the proliferation,
  migration and invasion of ESCA
