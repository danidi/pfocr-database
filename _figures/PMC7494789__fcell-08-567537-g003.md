caption: Mechanisms of actin cytoskeleton regulation by GAP-43. GAP-43 positively
  and negatively regulates the actin cytoskeleton through multiple routes of action.
  GAP-43 promotes growth cone motility and axon outgrowth by (1) recruiting actin-regulating
  proteins by forming PI(4,5)P2 clusters, (2) interacting with NCAM and spectrin,
  and (3) stabilizing actin filaments in a PKC-dependent manner. Conversely, m-calpain
  cleavage product of GAP-43 drives growth cone retraction and collapse through the
  Gosignaling pathway.
