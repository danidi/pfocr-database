caption: Major crosstalks between the PI3K and the MAPK cascades. The PI3K and the
  MAPK pathway are highly interconnected and can be activated by the same RTKs, dependent
  on the triggering ligand. Direct Ras-GTP interaction with PI3 Kinases can trigger
  Ras-activated PI3K signaling -. The KSR scaffold is also a potent regulator of key
  PI3K signaling mediators. Several cross-activations and -inhibitions have been assessed
  to date. For instance, Akt can inhibit Ras function , , - and Rsk can interfere
  with TSC2 -. Furthermore, both pathways converge on the same downstream targets,
  such as the FOXO proteins, GSK3, Myc or p70S6K -, and thus promote cancer associated-phenotypes.
