  the activity of key enzymes on different levels in both these metabolic pathways.
  Elliptic shapes represent proteins whereas rectangular shapes represent metabolites
  or metabolic pathways. Solid lines represent metabolic conversion, dotted lines
  represent the regulatory effects from oncogenic signalling transducers (red) on
  metabolic enzymes (blue). Abbreviations: CHK: choline kinase, PCYT1A: choline phosphate
  cytidylyl transferase alpha, PLA: phospholipase A, PLC: phospholipase C, PLD: phospholipase
  D, GDPD: glycerophosphodiester phosphodiesterase, PDK: pyruvate dehydrogenase kinase,
  PDH: pyruvate dehydrogenase, LDH: lactate dehydrogenase, TCA: tricarboxylic acid,
  PTEN: phosphatase and tensin homologue; TIGAR: TP53‐induced glycolysis and apoptosis
  regulator, PI3K: phosphatidylinositol‐3‐kinase, MTOR: mammalian target of rapamycin,
  AKT: v‐akt murine thymoma viral oncogene homologue 1, HIF‐1: hypoxia‐inducible factor
  1. (For interpretation of the references to colour in this figure legend, the reader
