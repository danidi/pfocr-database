caption: Proposed signaling pathway. Cocaine induces neuronal TNF-α expression, leading
  to the phosphorylation of JNK. p-JNK can, in turn, phosphorylate the well-characterized
  c-jun pathway, and potentially RXR-γ. RXR-γ is exported from the nucleus to the
  cytosol in response to phosphorylation and potentially subsequent post-translational
  modification such as ubiquitination. Following export, RXR-γ is degraded in the
  cytosol in a proteasome-dependent manner. In the absence of cellular stressors,
  RXR-γ is free to heterodimerize with nuclear receptor partners including RAR, TR,
  VDR, and PPAR. Ub; ubiquitin, P’some; proteasome.
