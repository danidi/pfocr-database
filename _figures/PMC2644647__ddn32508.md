caption: Working model. TSC1 and TSC2 regulate cilia formation in a TORC1/rapamycin-independent
  manner. Multiple signaling cascades, including MAPK, Akt and Rsk1, converge on TSC2,
  leading to its phosphorylation and inactivation and contributing to mTOR activation.
  Loss of PC1 does not appear sufficient to activate TOR or impact ciliary formation
  via TSC2. However, PC1 may cooperate with the multiple signaling cascades to initiate
  or enhance cystogenesis.
