  curated pathways. Figure adapted from []. Pathway members and interactions in ten
  selected pathways are shown, where gene-level alterations (e.g. mutations, copy
  number alterations, fusions, and epigenetic silencing) across the entire TCGA pan-cancer
  cohort were previously catalogued by the TCGA PanCanAtlas project, with the alteration

  frequencies (shading intensity indicating the average frequency of alteration across
  the TCGA BLCA cohort) by either oncogenic activation (for genes with oncogenic roles,
  alterations including activating mutations, fusions, and gene amplifications) or
  tumor suppressor inactivation (for genes with tumor suppressive roles, alterations
  including inactivating mutations, epigenetic silencing, and gene deletions). Italicized
  genes without boxes denote genes with close to 0% alteration frequencies.
