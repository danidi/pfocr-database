caption: VN/12-1 induces ER stress. This results in inhibition of protein translation,
  downregulation key cell cycle proteins such as cyclin D1 and arrest in G1-S phase
  cell cycle transition. Addition of CHL enhances the ER stress and switches it from
  reversible (protective) pathway to irreversible (apoptotic) pathway by CHOP upregulation.
  VN/12-1 inhibits PI3K-Akt-mTOR pathway and activates autophagy. Inhibition of VN/12-1-mediated
  autophagy by CHL potentiates apoptosis by activation of caspase 9 and PARP cleavage.
