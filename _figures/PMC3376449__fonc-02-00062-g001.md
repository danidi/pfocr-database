caption: HER2 signaling pathway. Receptor homodimerization or heterodimerization is
  the prerequisite step for HER2 activation. Its activation then triggers a broad
  spectrum of downstream cascades to promote numerous effects, including cell growth,
  proliferation, and survival. PI3K/Akt is one of the most well studied pathways activated
  by HER2. Activated PI3K/Akt also triggers mTOR, a master positive regulator of cell
  metabolism. In addition, HER2 activation can activate Ras/Raf and MEK pathways,
  which favors cancer cells’ growth and migration.
