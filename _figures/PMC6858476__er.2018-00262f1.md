caption: 'Schematic overview of steroidogenesis and corresponding urine steroid metabolites.
  Steroids are color-coded according to their bioactivity or commitment to a specific
  pathway: general precursors (yellow), mineralocorticoid precursors (light green),
  active mineralocorticoid (dark green), glucocorticoid precursors and inactive metabolite
  (light orange), active glucocorticoid (dark orange), androgen precursors (light
  blue), and active androgens (dark blue). Corresponding urinary metabolites are shown
  in yellow boxes. Arrows are labeled with the catalyzing enzyme and isoform. Essential
  cofactor proteins are also indicated: ADX, adrenodoxin; b5, cytochrome b5; PAPSS2,
  PAPS synthase 2; PRO, cytochrome P450 oxidoreductase; StAR, steroidogenic acute
  regulatory protein.'
