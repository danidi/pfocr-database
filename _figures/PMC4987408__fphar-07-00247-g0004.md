  this pathway, AKT is the bridge of the extracellular signal and the nuclear gene
  expression, affecting some of its downstream pathways. The regulation of rhein on
  these pathways is probably because of the indirect influence of rhein through hub
  AKT protein mediation or the direct action to the AKT downstream protein. The protein
  marked with star is the target of rhein. Red and green star represent negative and
  positive regulation, respectively.
