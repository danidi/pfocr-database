  AMPK is activated by upstream signaling pathways, including LKB1, CaMKKII, and TAK1.

  of TSC2 via Rheb, and direct phosphorylation of RAPTOR. In addition, mTOR-S6K1 signaling
  pathway inhibits AMPK activation through inactivation of TAK1.
