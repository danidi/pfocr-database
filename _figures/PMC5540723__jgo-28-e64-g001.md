  is ligated with PD-L1 recruitments of SHP-2, which dephosphorylates the downstream
  molecules and blocks T cell activation. The PD-1 and PD-L1 blockade with mAbs can
  enhance T cell antitumor activity [].APC, antigen-presenting cell; CD, cluster of
