caption: Red arrows denote the classical Aβ-tau cascade (Pathway 1). GSK3β affects
  both tau phosphorylation and Aβ production. Blue arrows denote addition roles of
  presenillin (Pathway 2). Purple arrows denote the recently characterized Aβ-PrPC-Fyn
  pathway (Pathway 3). Green and orange arrows denote the dual roles of tau. It may
  bring Fyn to dendritic spines to allow Aβ-induced neurotoxicity (Pathway 4) or directly
  impair synaptic function (Pathway 5). Other cellular events such as ER stress, AMPK
  activation and spastin activation may also be involved. Aβ may also affect neuronal
  circuits by modulating inhibitory synaptic transmission ().
