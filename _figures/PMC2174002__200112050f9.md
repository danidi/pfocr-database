caption: Distinct sets of PtdIns(3)P-binding effectors mediate the signaling effects
  of the Vps34 PtdIns 3-kinase. Etf1 serves as a transmembrane effector of Vps34 in
  the formation of Cvt vesicles through its basic amino acid cluster (KKPAKK). This
  suggests that an unidentified PtdIns(3)P-binding protein carries out the formation
  of autophagosomes in the macroautophagy pathway. The PX protein Vam7, vacuolar t-SNARE
  Vam3, and rab GTPase Ypt7 are key mediators in the vacuolar fusion of autophagic
  transport intermediates of both the Cvt and macroautophagy pathways.
