  the effects of the drug are completely independent of this pathway. b Upregulation
  of the pleiotropic gene NR3C1 could be responsible for proliferative behavior, especially
  when linked to PDGF. Downregulation of SMG9 and a mixture of down- and upregulation
  in this network present a problem determining the exact mechanisms of NR3C1 contributions
