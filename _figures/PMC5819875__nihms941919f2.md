caption: Upon translation and entry into the secretory pathway, Wnt proteins are acylated
  by Porcn and ushered out of the cell via Wls. In the off-state, a destruction complex
  composed of multiple proteins targets β-catenin for degradation by the proteasome.
  In the on-state, Wnt engages a Fzd/Lrp receptor complex leading to the disassembly
  of the destruction complex and re-localization of some of its components to the
  cell membrane. As a result, β-catenin is no longer degraded, accumulates in the
  cytoplasm and enters the nucleus where it associates with the Tcf transcription
  factors to activate expression of target genes.
