  labeled cytosolic proteins organelles and fat. mTOR and Bcl-2 can inhibit the initiation
  of autophagy. The Atg12-Atg5-Atg16 complex, LC3 and the transmembrane Atg9 are recruited
  to the phagophore and are necessary for elongation of the double membrane. Lysosomes
  fuse with the autophagosome, creating the autolysosome. The resulting products of
  the catalytic degradation process are transported to the cytosol and recycled.LC:
  Light chain-3.'
