caption: Lapatinib, a dual EGFR/HER2 kinase inhibitor, inhibits cell proliferation
  and survival by blocking the PI3K/Akt/mTOR (previously demonstrated by Gayle et
  al []) and MEK/ERK pathways. Based on our data, sustained activation of the MEK/ERK
  pathway in the presence of lapatinib is associated with reduced response to lapatinib.
  In this study, we inhibit MEK1/2 genetically using siRNA and pharmacologically using
  PD0325901 and selumetinib. FOXM1 was inhibited genetically using siRNA, pharmacologically
  using thiostrepton, and overexpressed using a FOXM1-CMV plasmid.
