  signaling like insulin and wnt, hypoxia, energy stress and amino acid sufficiency
  to regulate translation initiation. The individual signaling modules survey the
  intra- and extracellular environment for their particular stimuli, and relay their
  signal to key components of the Tor signaling core, Tsc2 and the Tor complex 1 (TORC1).
  Among other processes, Tor regulates the biosynthetic capacity of the cell by controlling
  translational initiation. For species-dependent differences, please see main text.
