  uroplakin 1a molecules found within lipid raft domains of the BEC plasma membranes.

  properties of fusiform vesicles. The cAMP signaling pathway activated when TLR4
  senses LPS abrogates this activity by acting on Rac-1, which is essential for actin
  remodeling.
