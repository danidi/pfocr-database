  The FLCN/FNIP1/FNIP2 complex binds AMPK and FLCN is phosphorylated by a rapamycin-sensitive
  kinase (i.e., mTORC1). B) When FLCN is deficient, AKT, mTORC1 and mTORC2 are activated.
  From Hasumi, et al. with permission ().
