caption: The C. neoformans PKC/Mpk1 pathway. The PKC (Protein kinase C)/Mpk1 pathway
  governs cell wall integrity and stress responses against nitrosative and oxidative
  damaging agents and anti-fungal drugs. This pathway is composed of several components
  including the small GTP-binding protein Rho1, PKC, Ser/Thr MAPKKK Bck1, MAPKK Mkk1/Mkk2,
  MAPK Mpk1, and Ipc1 (inositol-phosphorylceramide synthase-1), an enzyme which produces
  DAG (diacylglycerol) for activating PKC.
