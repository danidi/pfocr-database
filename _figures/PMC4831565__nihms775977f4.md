caption: GAPDH nitrosylation pathway mediates cocaine effects. (A) A recreational
  dose of cocaine induces dopamine-1 receptor (D1R) dependent nNOS-mediated nitrosylation
  of GAPDH. SNO-GAPDH binds Siah1, and both proteins translocate to the nucleus. Degradation
  of SUV39H1 by Siah1 leads to increased CREB dependent gene expression and mediates
  the behavioral effects of cocaine. (B) A toxic dose of cocaine, on the other hand,
  stimulates the NO-GAPDH-Siah1 cell death cascade inducing cell death via activation
  of p300/CBP and p53 mediated gene expression.
