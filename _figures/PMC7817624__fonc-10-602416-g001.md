  of glucose, amino acid lipid by regulating multiple metabolic pathways. Breast tumor
  cells mainly use aerobic glycolysis to produce ATP and utilize the pentose phosphate
  pathway to produce macromolecules such as NADPH. Wild-type and mutant P53 have contrary
  effects in monitoring fatty acid metabolism. Hypoxia, acidosis and ROS are regarded
  as important events which influence multiple metabolic pathways.
