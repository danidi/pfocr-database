  signaling pathways. (A) Generic scheme of the MAPK signaling cascade, whereby an

  response. (B) Schematic of the specific MAPK factors within each of the three major
  MAPK pathways. LeTx targets the MAPKK tier in the cascade, cleaving and inactivating
  all the MAPKK (MEK1-2, MKK3-7) with the exception of MKK5, the pathway for which
  is not depicted.
