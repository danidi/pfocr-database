  include (i) TXA2 pathway inhibitors which block either synthesis or receptors of

  Adhesion antagonists block interaction of collagen and vWF with GpVI and α2β1 receptors
  on platelets. GpIIb/IIIa Inhibitors block binding of fibrinogen to their receptors
  on platelets. ADP, adenosine diphosphate; PAR-1, protease activated receptor 1;
