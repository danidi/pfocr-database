  bind to homodimeric or heterodimeric receptors, after ligand stimulation receptors
  undergo conformational changes and bring JAKs into proximity which each other. JAKs
  trans/auto phosphorylate each other and the receptor, allowing STATS to bind to
  the receptor. Subsequently JAKs phosphorylate STATS, allowing them to dimerise and
  translocate to the nucleus to regulate gene transcription.
