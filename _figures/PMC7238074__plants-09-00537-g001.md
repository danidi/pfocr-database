  reductase; MK: mevalonate kinase; MPK: mevalonate 5-phosphate kinase; MDC: mevalonate
  5-diphosphate decarboxylase; IPPi: isopentenyl diphosphate isomerase; FPS, farnesyl
  diphosphate synthase. Artemisinin biosynthesis genes cloned in p1240 and p1250 vectors
  are shown in red.'
