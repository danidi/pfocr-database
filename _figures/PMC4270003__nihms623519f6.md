caption: 'A part of Gb3synthesis pathway is shown. From lactosylceramide (LacCer)
  to Gb3, alpha 1, 4-galactosyltransferase (EC 2.4.1.228) adds a galactose to LacCer
  to produce Gb3. Likewise, UDP-GalNAc: beta 1,3-galactosaminyltransferase (EC 2.4.1.79)
  works on Gb3 to make Gb4. In the catabolic pathway, beta-hexosaminidase (EC 3.2.1.52)
  degrades Gb4 to Gb3, and alpha-galactosidase (EC 3.2.1.22) makes LacCer from Gb3.'
