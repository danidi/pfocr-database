  binding to transmembrane or soluble IL-6 receptor. IL-6 initiates the IL-6 signaling

  complex then induces homodimerization of gp130, which leads to activation of a signaling
  system. Transcriptional factors including STAT3 activate various gene expressions,
  resulting in cell differentiation or proliferation. JAKs: Janus kinases; STAT3:
  signal transducer and activator of transcription 3; SHP-2: SH2 domain-containing

  protein 2; ERK: extracellular signal-regulated kinase; MAPK: mitogen activated protein

  protein; SAA: serum amyloid A; MMPs: matrix metalloproteinases.'
