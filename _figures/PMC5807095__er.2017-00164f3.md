caption: Schematic of hedgehog signaling pathway components in the ovary. GDF9 made
  by oocytes induces the expression of the ligands IHH and DHH in granulosa cells.
  These ligands bind to and activate cell surface receptors PTCH1/2 in theca cells.
  This activation blocks PTCH inhibition of the target receptor SMO, leading to the
  activation of the transcription factors GLI1, 2, or 3. These transcription factors
  regulate the expression of selected genes, including inhibitory feedback molecules
  (Hhip and Ptch2) and enzymes in the steroidogenic pathway, including Cyp17a1. Adapted
  from Wijgerde et al. ().
