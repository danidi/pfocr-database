  in ACC cells. Sirt1 inhibition reduces ERα expression (1). Reduction of ERα genomic
  action decreases CCND1 expression (2) influencing ACC cell proliferation. Reduction
  of ERα non‐genomic action decreases CREB phosphorylation and consequently IGF1R
  expression (3) influencing ACC cell proliferation. In addition, sirt1 inhibition
  causes ACC cell death by activating the intrinsic apoptotic pathway (4). Moreover
  sirt1 depletion reduces ACC cell motility regulating EMT process (5)
