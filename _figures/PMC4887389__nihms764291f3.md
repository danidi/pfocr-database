caption: A. The SHH ligand patched 1 constitutively represses SMO, allowing the sequential
  phosphorylation of Gli by protein kinase A (PKA), glycogen synthase kinase 3 beta
  (GSK3B), and casein kinase (CK). Phosphorylation of the Gli family zinc finger proteins
  marks them for ubiquitination and limited proteosomal degradation. N-terminal fragments
  of Gli proteins translocate to the nucleus where they repress transcription.B. Binding
  of SHH to patched 1 prevents its inhibition of SMO. This prevents phosphorylation
  and degradation of Gli proteins. Full-length Gli proteins translocate to the nucleus
  and promote transcription.
