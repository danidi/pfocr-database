caption: Signalling interaction of the death receptor pathway and mitochondrial pathway.
  In the death receptor pathway, TNFR can interrupt the interaction of Cypd and ANT
  in a RIP1‐dependent manner, which produces much mitochondrial ROS. The mitochondrial
  ROS can promote the auto‐phosphorylation of RIP1 and assembly of the necrosome while
  the cytoplasm ROS cannot. RIP3 can promote mitochondrial ROS production through
  activation of the metabolism enzymes. The mitochondrial ROS is also the key stimulator
  of the MPTP opening
