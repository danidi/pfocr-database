caption: Sub-network with a size cut-off  of 28 between D-glucose and L-phenylalanine
  in S. cerevisiae iMM904. Dark gray rectangles represent reactions, light gray circles
  represent metabolites. The nomenclature of reaction and metabolite names are consistent
  with iMM904 genome-scale metabolic model. The source and the target metabolite is
  glc-D_c and phe-L_c respectively (shown in boldface and italics). We note that the
  synthesis of L-phenylalanine involves several metabolites from different compartments.
  The reactions in this pathway and the seed metabolites can be found in Supplementary
  Results  5.1 and Supplementary Table  respectively.
