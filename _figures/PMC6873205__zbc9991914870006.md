caption: Biosynthesis by NRPS proteins in insects and vertebrates. A, single-module
  NRPS proteins Ebony and ACSF-U26 contain A and CP domains typical of NRPS biosynthesis.
  However, their C termini encode for AANAT or PQQ domains in place of the usual C
  domain found in NRPS modules. B, nemamides are biosynthesized by a PKS-NRPS hybrid
  in nematodes. PKS-1 is a multimodular PKS-NRPS megaenzyme, whereas NRPS-1 is a two-module
  NRPS. The NRPS modules in this pathway have the common A-CP-C architecture.
