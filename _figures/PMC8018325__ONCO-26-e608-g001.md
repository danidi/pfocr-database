  event, liberating NICD into the intracellular space. NICD can interact with other
  pathways independent of transcriptional activity through noncanonical signaling
  or translocate to the nucleus, associate with CSL and MAML, and promote expression
  of target genes through canonical signaling.Abbreviations: GSI, gamma secretase
  inhibitor; MAML, mastermind‐like family; NICD, Notch intracellular domain.'
