  interaction, NK cell receptors initiate multiple signaling cascades. While the natural
  cytotoxic receptors (NKp30, NKp44, and NKp46) signal through Src family kinase (SFK)
  to activate PI3K/Rac/Erk pathway, DNAM-1 and 2B4 signal SFK to activate Rac/Erk
  pathway, thereby leading to cytotoxicity.
