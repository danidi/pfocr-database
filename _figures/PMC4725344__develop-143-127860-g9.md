caption: Model of Prox1 crosstalk with FGFRs. In normal lenses, FGFs interact with
  the canonical FGFR1-4, or with FGFRL1, to drive intracellular signaling. LCTL facilitates
  the interaction of FGFR1,2,4 with endocrine FGFs. Activation of FGFRs promotes signaling
  via the MAPK pathway, upregulating Prox1 expression in response to FGF stimulation.
  Further, Prox1 upregulates Fgfr3, Fgfrl1 and Lctl expression through direct promoter
  interactions, sensitizing these cells to respond to FGFs, driving LF differentiation.
  LEC, lens epithelial cell; LFC, lens fiber cell.
