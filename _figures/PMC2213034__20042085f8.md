caption: Schematic representation of our model for the mechanism of anti-CD63 inhibition.
  The Lyn–Ca2+-dependent signaling pathway for degranulation is shown in gray. The

  complex is shown in black and yellow. Mechanism 1 (steric hindrance of integrin
  binding to their ECM substrates) is represented in green. Mechanism 2 (general disruption
  of the membrane complexes containing CD63) is represented in blue. Mechanism 3 (sequestration
  of PI4K) is represented in red.
