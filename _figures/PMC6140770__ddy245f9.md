caption: Regulation of AMPK signaling in DM1 muscle. AMPK signaling is repressed in
  DM1 muscle. Pharmacological (AICAR or RSV) or physiological (exercise) activation
  of the AMPK pathway has a beneficial impact on DM1. It promotes the slower, more
  oxidative muscle phenotype, and improves DM1 muscle histology by decreasing muscle
  fiber hypertrophy and central nucleation. It also triggers a decrease in nuclear
  CUGexp RNA aggregation, a rescue in RNA-binding misregulation and a correction of
  alternative splicing towards wild-type conditions.
