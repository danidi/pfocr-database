  to the network and are not found in our microarray data. The solid lines connecting
  molecules here represents a direct relation and dotted lines an indirect relation.

  with p-values ≤0.05 and ≥1.5-fold and was superimposed with the miRNAs (blue colored
  boxes) having their putative targets.
