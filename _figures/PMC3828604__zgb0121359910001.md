caption: Engagement of cell surface TLR1, -2, -4, -5, or -6 initiates the MyD88-dependent
  signaling pathway, which results in activation of the downstream IKK and MKK signaling
  branches. Each of these branches induces expression of proinflammatory cytokines
  such as TNFα, IL-6, and IL-1β by the transcription factors NF-κB and AP-1. The TRIF-dependent
  signaling branch downstream of TLR4 results in expression of type I IFNs mediated
  by IRF3. The regulatory roles of stress proteins important in oxidative stress (yellow),
  heat shock response (green), UPR (blue), and DDR (pink) in these TLR signaling pathways
  are illustrated. →, stimulation; ↔, direct interaction; ⫞, inhibition.
