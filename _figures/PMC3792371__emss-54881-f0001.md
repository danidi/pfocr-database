caption: Following TCR engagement protein tyrosine kinases (PTKs) such as Lck, phosphorylate
  the tyrosine residues in the ITAMs in the cytoplasmic tails of CD3ζζ, which serve
  as docking sites for the Src homology 2 (SH2) domain containing tyrosine kinase
  ZAP-70. ZAP-70 phosphorylates adaptor proteins, including LAT and SLP-76, activating
  PI3-kinase pathway, Ras-MAPK pathways and Ca2+-mediated signalling. In the appropriate
  cytokine environment (red cytokines), CD4+ T cells differentiate into reversible
  TH1, TH2, TH9, TH17 and Treg cells and secrete a suite of characteristic cytokines
  (blue, bold cytokines).
