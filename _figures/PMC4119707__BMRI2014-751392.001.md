caption: The signaling pathway of chemoattractant GPCRs. Chemoattractant GPCRs activated
  by ligands elicit a cascade of signal transduction pathways involving G proteins,
  phospholipase C (PLC), phosphoinositide (PI) 3 kinases, protein kinase C (PKC),
  Ca2+, RAS, and MAPKs to mediate leukocyte migration and activation. Chemoattractant
  GPCRs also play a crucial role in tumor progression upon activation by their ligands
  culminating in cell chemotaxis, invasion, production of mediators promoting angiogenesis,
  and transactivation of EGFR.
