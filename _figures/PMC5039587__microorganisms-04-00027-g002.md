caption: Modulation of Toll-like receptor 4 (TLR4) signaling pathway in porcine intestinal
  epithelial (PIE) cells by acidic (APS) and neutral (NPS) exopolysaccharides produced
  by Lactobacillus plantarum N14 and Lactobacillus delbrueckii TUA4408L. Treatment
  of PIE cells with L. plantarum N14, L. delbrueckii TUA4408L or their APS and NPS
  induce the expression of TLR negative regulators TOLLIP, IRAK-M, SIGIRR, MKP-1,
  A20, and/or Bcl3 via TLR2, TLR4, and/or RP105. Treated-PIE cells showed a reduced
  inflammatory response in terms of chemokines and cytokines production after the
  subsequent challenge of with TLR4 agonists.
