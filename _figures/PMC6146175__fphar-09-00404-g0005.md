caption: STAT 3 signaling pathway and site of action of napabucasin. Attachment of
  cytokines to its receptors activates Janus kinases which in turn phosphorylates
  STAT 3. The phosphorylation of STAT 3 leads to dimerization and activation of STAT
  3. STAT 3 enters the nuclei to bind at STAT 3 binding sites on DNA which triggers
  transcription of several proteins which regulate cell proliferation, survival, angiogenesis,
  invasion and migration. Through Beta-catenin pathway STAT 3 induces genes responsible
  to trigger the formation and maintenance of stem cells.
