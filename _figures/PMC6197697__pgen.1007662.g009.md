  directly activates the transcription of OsGST4 and ABA–dependent OsP5CR to increase
  antioxidant responses and proline accumulation to fulfill ROS-scavenging, in combination
  with ABA–dependent abiotic stress–responsive regulatory pathway; besides, OsMADS25
  might regulate the root growth via auxin signaling, which also enhances ABA signaling
  in oxidative stress, thereby accounting for the positive role of OsMADS25 in the
  growth and salinity tolerance in rice.
