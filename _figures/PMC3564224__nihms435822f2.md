  modulates signaling mediated by Ca2+, cGMP, and cAMP in vascular cells. CD47 ligation
  also controls cell survival via mitochondrial dependent pathways mediated by translocation
  of BNIP3 and suppression of a protective autophagy pathway that blocks apoptosis.
  CD47 also serves as the counter-receptor for SIRPα. Engagement of SIRPα on phagocytic
  cells suppresses phagocytic killing of target cells.
