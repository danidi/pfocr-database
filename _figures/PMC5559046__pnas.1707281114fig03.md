caption: 'Cooperation of 20HE, IS, and TOR signaling pathways in the regulation of
  fly larva growth and development. (A) The elements of signaling pathways recognized
  in D. melanogaster larvae and their putative homologs (highlighted in colored rectangles)
  represented on our C. costata custom microarray. The 20HE signaling cascade is in
  red, forkhead box O (FOXO) pathway components are in green, TOR pathway components
  are in yellow, and the serine-threonine kinase LKB1-activated pathway is shown in
  brown. White rectangles with gray font indicate elements not represented on our
  custom microarray. (B) Results of microarray transcriptional analysis in C. costata.
  Only results in which statistically significant up- or down-regulations of signaling
  pathway components were observed are shown (the numbers in each box are log2-transformed
  average FCs in expression). For more details, see  and , Excel sheet: 20HE, IS,
  TOR.'
