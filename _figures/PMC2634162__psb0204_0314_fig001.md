caption: (A) Localization of pMtIRE-gusA expression in wild-type nodulated roots.

  activity. The arrow points to the X-Gluc staining in the proximal side of zone II
  in a 15 dpi nodule. The arrowhead points to root hairs in which no staining was
  observed. Bar = 100 µM. (B) Phospholipid signaling pathway that may activate MtIRE
  protein's presumed kinase activity.
