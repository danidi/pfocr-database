caption: NF-κB in lymphoid organogenesis. NF-κB regulates key components of a positive
  feedback loop between hematopoietic and stromal cells. LTα1β2-expressing hematopoietic
  cells induce production of VCAM-1 through the canonical NF-κB pathway and chemokines
  through the non-canonical pathway in LTβR-expressing stromal cells. Stromal expression
  of chemokines induces the upregulation of integrins (α4β1) on hematopoietic cells
  resulting in increased recruitment of LTα1β2-expressing cells, and increased signaling
  through stromal LTβR. RANKL signaling through RANK on the hematopoietic cell leads
  to activation of NF-κB, and further upregulation of LTα1β2.
