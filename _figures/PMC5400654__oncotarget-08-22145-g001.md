caption: TGF-β signaling pathway is initiated by binding of TGF-β ligands to TβRII
  and TβRI. The Smad pathway is mediated by phosphorylation of TβRI by TβRII and subsequent
  activation of Smad2/3. Activated Smad2/3 form complexes with Smad4 and translocate
  into the nucleus. The Non-Smad pathway takes place through multiple intracellular
  signaling cascades such as Par6-Smurf1-RhoA, RAS-RAF-MEK-ERK and PI3K/Akt pathway.
  Other signaling pathways, such as Wnt, Notch and HIF-1α, are also involved in EMT.
  Wnt signaling promotes EMT by inhibiting GSK3β to stabilize β-catenin, which translocates
  to the nucleus with LEF/TCF. The interaction between Delta/Jagged and its receptor
  Notch induces the release of Notch ICD. Hypoxia in the tumor microenvironment promotes
  EMT through HIF-1α and crosstalks with Wnt and Notch pathways. Activation of above
  pathways induces the expression of master regulators of EMT including Snail1/2,
  Twist and ZEB1/2 families, which can initiate a coordinated transcriptional network
  leading to suppression of epithelial marker and up-regulation of mesenchymal marker
  expressions.
