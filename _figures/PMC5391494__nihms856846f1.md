  CNG) cation channels, cGMP-dependent protein kinases (cGKs) and cGMP-regulated phosphodiesterases

  CYB5R3, NADH-cytochrome b5 reductase 3; DNA, desoxyribose nucleic acid; eNOS, endothelial

  Hb2+/Hb3+, hemoglobin α (reduced/oxidized); PDE, phosphodiesterase.
