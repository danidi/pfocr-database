  de novo pathway (pink background), the salvage pathway (light blue background) and
  the sphingomyelin hydrolysis pathway (green background). Green arrows indicate an

  ceramidase; ACER3, endoplasmic reticulum and golgi ceramidase; CERS1, 2, 5, 6, ceramide
  synthases; DEGS1, dihydroceramide desaturase; CERK, ceramide kinase; SGMS1, sphingomyelin
  synthase 1; SMPD1, lysosomal sphingomyelin phosphodiesterase 1; SMPD2, neutral sphingomyelin

  phosphatase 1.'
