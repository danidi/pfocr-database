caption: DNA damage recruits and activates PARP-1 and ATM, with PARP-1 producing poly(ADP-ribose)
  leading to assembly of IKKγ/NEMO and PIASy and ATM. PIASy and ATM SUMOylate and
  phosphorylate IKKγ/NEMO respectively which is then translocated to the cytoplasm
  to form the IKK complex. ATM independently translocates to the cytoplasm to induce
  TRAF6-Ubc13 polyubiquitination, which promotes cIAP dependent IKKγ/NEMO ubiquitination
  which then promotes TAB2-TAK1 dependent phosphorylation and activation of IKKβ.
