caption: 'The EGFR signaling pathway exerts its biological effects via two major modes
  of actions, namely, the cytoplasmic/ traditional (a) and the nuclear (b) signaling
  modes.a. The cytoplasmic/traditional EGFR pathway is consisted of five major modules:
  PLC-γ-CaMK/PKC, Ras-Raf-MAPK, PI-3K-Akt-mTOR, JAK2/STAT3 and STAT3. Activation of
  these signaling modules often leads to tumorigenesis, tumor proliferation, metastasis,
  chemoresistance, and radio-resistance.b. Nuclear EGFR has three key functions: (i)
  gene transactivation, (ii) tyrosine kinase, and (iii) protein-protein interaction.'
