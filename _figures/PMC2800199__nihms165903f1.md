  of ERK leads to induction of CDK4/cyclin D activity and Rb phosphorylation. Raf-1

  of genes involved in cell cycle progression, angiogenesis and tumor growth. Treatment
  with RRD-251 disrupts RB-Raf-1 interaction and prevents Rb phosphorylation and inhibits
  E2F-mediated transcription of S-phase genes.
