caption: Surfactant therapy and antioxidants work through the same pathway to enhance
  efferocytosis. Oxidant stress activates RhoA, an inhibitor of Rac-dependent actin
  rearrangement that is required for engulfment of ACs; antioxidants suppress RhoA,
  allowing Rac activation and permitting apoptotic cell engulfment (shown on the left).
  Surfactant therapy decreases oxidant stress and may favor antioxidant production,
  shifting the balance toward RhoA inhibition and apoptotic cell ingestion (shown
  on the right). See  legend for expansion of abbreviation. (Illustration by Haderer
  & Muller Biomedical Art, LLC.)
