caption: 'Regulation of SCFCOI1 by Nedd8/RUB. The activity of SCFCOI1 is modulated
  by cyclic attachment and removal of the ubiquitin-like modifier (UBL) Nedd8/RUB
  to the CUL1 subunit of the SCF complex. Accordingly, plants carrying mutations in
  the genes encoding components of the neddylation machinery (E1: ECR1/AXR1/AXL; E2:
  RCE1/2) or in the COP9 deneddylase signalosome complex are also affected in JA signaling.
  Defects in the RUB-conjugation pathway can also be mimicked using the E1-specific
  inhibitor MLN4924 [].'
