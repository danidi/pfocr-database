  lineages, the TE and PE. Inhibitors of both the Smad-dependent (Dorsomorphin) and

  have different effects on the PE. Blocking the Smad-dependent pathway results in
  the elimination of PE precursor cells, while blocking the Smad-independent pathway
  compromises the differentiation of PE cells.
