caption: The hypothetic mechanism of LEF1 in the Notch signaling pathway. In the nucleus,
  PML/RARα fusion gene may induce plakoglobin (γ-catenin) expression, leading to transcriptional
  activation of LEF1. LEF1 itself is a coactivator of NICD and may crosstalk with
  the Notch signaling pathway by regulating the expression of JAG1 on the cytomembrane.
  Furthermore, JAG1 is overexpressed in APL, and upon receiving ATRA therapy, it is
  downregulated in the NB4 cell line which indicates that JAG1 may be a therapeutic
  target of ATRA. PML, promyelocytic leukemia; RARα retinoic acid receptor α; LEF1,
  lymphoid enhancer binding factor-1; NICD, Notch intracellular domain; APL, acute
  promyelocytic leukemia; ATRA, all-trans retinoic acid; JAG1, Jagged 1.
