  mechanisms, which include the inhibition of β-catenin-dependent signaling, EGFR

  apoptosis, JNK1 signaling, ERS signaling and ERK1/2 signaling. PI3K/Akt signaling
  can also be activated to inactivate JAK/STAT pathway.
