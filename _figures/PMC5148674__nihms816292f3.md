  (A) suppressing glucose uptake; (B) inhibiting aerobic glycolysis; and (C) promoting

  of glucose uptake, respectively. In (B), the genes colored in orange and blue represent
  positive and negative regulators of glycolysis. In (C), the genes colored in blue
  represents positive regulators of gluconeogenesis. PPP, pentose phosphatase pathway;
