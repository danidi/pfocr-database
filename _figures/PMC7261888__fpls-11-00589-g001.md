  the products are generated naturally in cyanobacteria whilst the arrows with dashed
  lines indicate that the products could be potentially produced from the corresponding
  substrates through metabolic engineering. MEP, methylerythritol-4-phosphate; GPPS,
  geranyl pyrophosphate synthase; FPPS, farnesyl pyrophosphate synthase; GGPPS, geranylgeranyl
  pyrophosphate synthase.
