caption: Ligation of TCR and PD-1 leads to tyrosine phosphorylation (P) of the ITIM
  and ITSM of PD-1. Binding of the ITSM by SHP-1 or SHP-2 results in the dephosphorylation
  of proximal signaling molecules and augmentation of PTEN expression. This effectively
  attenuates the activation of the PI3K and Akt pathways. PD-1 signaling may result
  in decreased T-cell proliferation, survival, protein synthesis, and IL-2 production.
  (Red arrows and text indicate consequence of PD-1-mediated signaling)
