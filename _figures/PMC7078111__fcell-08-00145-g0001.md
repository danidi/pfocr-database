  downstream signaling pathways; (B) miRNAs are generated from the nucleus and can
  be decoyed by specific lncRNAs in cytoplasm, and antioncogenic miRNAs may suppress
  tumor progression by repressing the translation of c-Met mRNA; (C), illustration
  of antioncogenic lncRNAs (MEG3 and MIR22HG) in the regulation of c-Met; (D) the
  graphical representation of p53/miR-34 and lncRNA/miR-34/c-Met pathway; (E) oncogenic
  lncRNAs/miRNAs/c-Met in drug resistance; (F) oncogenic miRNAs in c-Met related carcinogenesis.
