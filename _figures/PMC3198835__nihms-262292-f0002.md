  released in the cytosol in response to cell death stimuli. Mitochondrially released
  survivin forms a complex with XIAP that is negatively regulated by protein kinase
  A (PKA) phosphorylation of survivin on Ser20, and results in increased XIAP stability
  against proteasomal degradation, enhanced gene expression, i.e. NF-κB, and synergistic
  inhibition of effector and initiator caspases (a schematic diagram of caspase 9
  is shown).
