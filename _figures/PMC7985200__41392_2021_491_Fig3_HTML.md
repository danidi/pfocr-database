caption: The adaptive transition within GSCs classifications. Therapeutic sensitive
  GSCs like PN GSCs can transform into MES GSCs which show resistance to cancer treatment.
  PN GSCs mostly dependent on glycolysis, and MES GSCs switch its metabolism between
  glycolysis and OXPHOS. PN GSCs metabolize glucose while MES GSCs can switch between
  glucose and glutamine. The Notch and Wnt pathway are preferentially activated in
  PN GSCs; the activation of TGF-β pathway and NF-κB pathway are mostly observed in
  MES GSCs
