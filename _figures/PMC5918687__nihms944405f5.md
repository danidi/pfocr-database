caption: In the presence of Wnt ligands derived from the nail epithelium, osteoblasts
  that form the bone and precursors of osteoclasts that break down the bone both display
  Wnt pathway activation. Wnt activation in osteoblasts results in osteoprotegerin
  (Opg) expression, which is known to inhibit osteoclast activation and differentiation.
  Wnt activation in osteoclast precursors, on the other hand, inhibits their differentiation
  into mature osteoclasts, together ensuring the bone maintenance.
