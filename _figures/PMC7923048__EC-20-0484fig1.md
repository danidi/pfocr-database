caption: Classic vitamin D signalling pathway. Serum 25(OH)D3 is converted to 1,25(OH)2D3
  by mitochondrial 1α-hydroxylase, initiating the signalling cascade by binding to
  VDR, which dimers with RXR, and binds to specific DNA sequences, VDREs, regulating
  the transcription of several genes. 1,25(OH)2D3-VDR-RXR complex then synthesizes
  cathelicidin as an inactive pro-peptide (hCAP18) which is cleaved upon release of
  the active LL-37 by proteases.
