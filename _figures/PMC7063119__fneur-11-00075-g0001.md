caption: Overview of apelin/APJ system–mediated signaling pathways. Canonical ligand-dependent
  APJ signaling via Gαi and Gαq leads to the activation of protein kinase C (PKC),
  phosphatidylinositide 3-kinase (PI3K), and nitrous oxide synthase (NOS) pathways
  and the inhibition of adenylyl cyclase (AC) (). In endothelial cells, ligand activates
  a Gα13-dependent pathway that allows the transcription of myocyte enhancer factor-2
  (MEF2). Moreover, the G protein–independent pathway of the apelin/APJ system is
  mediated by G protein–coupled receptor kinase (GRK) and β-arrestin. Arrowheads indicate
  activation, and blunted arrows indicate inhibition.
