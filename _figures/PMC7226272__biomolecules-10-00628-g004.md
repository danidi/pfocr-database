caption: Viral manipulation of polyamines in infected cells. The herpesvirus Epstein–Barr
  virus (EBV), herpes simplex virus (HSV), bovine herpesvirus (BoHV), and human cytomegalovirus
  (HCMV) induce polyamine levels through ODC1 and SAMDC. Hepatitis C virus (HCV) proteins
  induce both SAT1 and ODC1, and cells harboring HCV replicons exhibit reduced levels
  of polyamines. Paramecium busaria chlorella virus (PBCV-1) encodes a polyamine biosynthetic
  pathway. Mechanisms that are not fully understood are represented by dashed lines.
