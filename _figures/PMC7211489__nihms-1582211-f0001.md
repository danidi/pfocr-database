  and signaling in the mitogen-activated protein kinase (MAPK) pathway (Raf/MEK/ERK).

  domain interaction releases the autoinhibition. Raf is activated through side-by-side
  dimerization. Rap1 dilutes oncogenic Ras nanoclusters, suppressing Raf’s activation.
  Abbreviations: CRD, cysteine-rich domain; KD, kinase domain.'
