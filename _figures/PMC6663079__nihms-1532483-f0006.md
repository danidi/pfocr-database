caption: GAS5 binds p53 and its activator p300 to enhance p53 protein stability and
  its activity, causing increased expression of cell cycle regulators p21, which inhibits
  CDK1 activity, leading to cell cycle arrest. Increased p53 expression/activity also
  enhances NOXA expression, which promotes Caspase 3 cleavage and thus VSMC apoptosis.
  The combined effects of cell cycle arrest and apoptosis cause the reduction of injury-induced
  neointima formation.
