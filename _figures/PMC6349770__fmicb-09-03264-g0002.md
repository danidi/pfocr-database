  xylose utilization via ameliorating cofactor imbalance, including (I) discovery

  enhancing NAD+ regeneration through overexpressing noxE; (V) improving NAD+ regeneration
  through the integration of acetate-consuming pathway. Abbreviation of metabolites−1,3-BPG,

  G3P, glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate. Abbreviation of enzymes—XR, xylose reductase; XDH,

  dehydrogenase; noxE, NADH oxidase; AADH, acetylating acetaldehyde dehydrogenase;
