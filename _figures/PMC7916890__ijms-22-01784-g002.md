caption: Amino acids and growth factors-mediated activation of the mTORC1 signaling.
  Amino acids bind to their specific sensors and transduce the signal through GATOR2,
  GATOR1, KICSTOR, and Ragulator complexes, leading to activation of Rag small GTPases
  and lysosomal recruitment of mTORC1. Growth factors activate other lysosomal localized
  small GTPases Rheb through Akt/TSC signaling axis. The small GTPases Rags and Rheb
  form two arms to fully activate mTORC1 pathway in response to amino acids and growth
  factors.
