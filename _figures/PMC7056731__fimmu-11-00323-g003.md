caption: The mutual activation of Hippo-Yorkie pathway and Toll/IMD mediated anti-bacterial
  response in Drosophila. Yorkie-Scalloped complex induce the expression of Cactus,
  which abolishes the activity of Drosophila the NF-κB transcription factors dorsal/DIF
  and AMPs production. Yorkie also impairs the IMD pathway mediated AMPs production
  via suppressing the expression of Relish, the NF-κB protein in IMD pathway. Activation
  of Toll receptor activates Hippo via Toll-Myd88-Pelle cascade mediated degradation
  of Cka. The interplays between Hippo-Yorkie and Toll/IMD pathway are highlighted
  with red.
