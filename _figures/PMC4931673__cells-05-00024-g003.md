caption: Circuitry of amino acid signalling to MTORC1. Extracellular amino acids are
  first transported into the cell. Regulatory amino acids such as Leu and Arg are
  further transported into the lysosome. Lysosomal Arg is sensed via SLC38A9.1, leading
  to activation of the vATPase-Ragulator complex and MTORC1. Leu is sensed via Sesn2
  to regulate the GATOR1 pathway. Gln activates MTORC1 via an Arf1-mediated pathway.
  Amino acids also activate MTORC1 at the Golgi via Rab1A. Growth factor signalling
  leads to activation of PI3K and AKT, which controls the TSC1/2 complex and Rheb.
