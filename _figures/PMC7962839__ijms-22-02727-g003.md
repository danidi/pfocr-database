  signaling pathway. Exposure of renal tubular cells leads to the externalization
  of phosphatidylserine (PS) with a release of transforming growth factor-β (TGF-β)
  and the formation of caveolae with sequestration of key signaling proteins such
  as TGF-β receptors, extracellular regulated kinase (ERK), sphingosine kinase 1 (SK-1)
  and sphingosine-1-phosphate (S1P). SMAD-3: mothers against decapentaplegic homolog
  3; CD: cluster of differentiation; HIF-α: hypoxic inducible factor-α; A1AR: A1 adenosine
  receptor; IL-11: interleukin 11; IL-11R: IL-11 receptor. Adapted from: Fukazawa
  K, Lee HT. Volatile anesthetics and AKI: risks, mechanisms, and a potential therapeutic
  window. J. Am. Soc. Nephrol. 2014 May; 25(5):884–892. doi:10.1681/ASN.2013111215.'
