caption: AVP stimulation of V1AR on CM leads to enhanced protein synthesis and cellular
  hypertrophy in phospholipase C (PLC)-protein kinase C (PKC)-dependent increases
  in calcium and c-fos. AVP-induced hypertrophy also increases atrial myosin light
  chain-1 (ALC-1) activation via a Ca2+-calmodulin-calcineurin-NFAT pathway. G protein-coupled
  receptor kinase (GRK)-dependent V1AR signaling decreases beta-adrenergic receptor
  (βAR) responsiveness and increases cell survival during hypoxia. V1AR signaling
  also increases ventricular differentiation and maturation via GATA-4 regulation.
  AVP also alters cellular metabolism, increasing glycolysis and decreasing fatty
  acid oxidation.
