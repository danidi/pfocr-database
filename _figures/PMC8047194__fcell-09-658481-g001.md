caption: Hippo signaling pathways in Drosophila and mammals. Multiple upstream signals
  act through the Hpo/MST1/2-Wts/Lats1/2 kinase cascade to control the subcellular
  localization and stability (for mammalian pathway only) of the pathway effectors
  Yki/Yap/Taz. When phosphorylation of Yki/Yap/Taz by the Hpo kinase cascade is compromised,
  these pathway effectors enter the nucleus and binds the transcription factors Sd/TEAD1-4
  to regulate genes involved in cell growth, proliferation, survival, plasticity,
  and metabolism.
