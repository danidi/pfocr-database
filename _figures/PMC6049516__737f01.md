  of its transcriptional target, c-Myc. RhoGEF (guanine nucleotide exchange factor)
  LARG, RhoA, and ROCK (Rho-associated protein kinase) act downstream from Pkd1 to
  regulate the Hippo pathway and YAP/TAZ.
