caption: 'For clarity, we show the effect of LNX2 on NOTCH pathway with the WNT pathway
  unaffected. However, NOTCH and WNT pathways have been reported to work cooperatively
  in tumorigenesis and hence a change in one will affect the other []. Based on our
  studies, LNX2 can bind numb and induce its ubiquitination and subsequent proteasomal
  degradation []. Numb inhibits the NOTCH pathway by degrading NICD []. Aberrant changes
  in the WNT and NOTCH pathways have been associated with cancer [, ]. This leads
  us to speculate a role for LNX2 in tumorigenesis by affecting the WNT and NOTCH
  pathways through the ubiquitination of Numb. Our study provides the structure of
  the functional machinery of LNX2 and thus a potential target for therapeutic intervention.
  Abbreviations: NICD, Notch intracellular domain; Ub, Ubiquitin.'
