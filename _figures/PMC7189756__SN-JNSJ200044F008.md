caption: Schematic model of downstream signaling events associated with type I interferon
  actions in nociceptors. Our work demonstrates that type I IFN production rapidly
  induced nociceptor hyperexcitability and mechanical pain sensitization via IFNR
  activation, likely on sensory neurons. The signaling mechanisms are dependent on
  MNK-eIF4E with no apparent ISR induction. Therefore, MNK-eIF4E signaling is a key
  translation regulation pathway that modulates viral infection-induced pain.
