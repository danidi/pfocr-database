caption: Proposed scheme for the antiproliferative mechanism of metformin in estrogen‐dependent
  endometrial cancer (EC) cells. Metformin inhibits EC cell proliferation by activating
  the AMP‐activated protein kinase/human Forkhead box O1 (AMPK–FOXO1) pathway. Metformin
  may exert its antiproliferative effect by activating AMPK and thus decreasing phosphorylation
  (p) of FOXO1 protein, thereby triggering the relocalization of FOXO1 protein from
  the cytoplasm to the nucleus and resulting in increased FOXO1 activity.
