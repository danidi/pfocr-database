  of the transcription factor CREB, which translocates to the nucleus and promotes
  the expression of zif 268. Acting as a transcription factor, zif 268 regulates the
  expression of late response genes, such as glutamic acid decarboxylase (GAD), synapsins
  and neurofilament 68.
