caption: 'Hypothetical pathway (solid lines) for glucose dissimilation by triose phosphate
  isomerase-negative (tpi1Δ) S. cerevisiae. DHAP is detoxified by its reduction to
  G3P and subsequent dephosphorylation to glycerol. This requires that all NADH generated
  in the glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate dehydrogenase reaction be available for DHAP reduction.
  Oxidation of either cytosolic G3P or cytosolic NADH by other processes (dotted lines)
  will lead to accumulation of DHAP. Abbreviations: GAP, glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate;
  FBA, fructose-1,6-biphosphate aldolase; GPD, cytosolic G3P dehydrogenase; GPP, glycerol
  phosphatase; GUT, mitochondrial flavin adenine dinucleotide-dependent G3P dehydrogenase;
  NDE, external mitochondrial NADH dehydrogenase; PDC, pyruvate decarboxylase; ADH,
  alcohol dehydrogenase.'
