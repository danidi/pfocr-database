caption: FSH acts through the downstream mediators PKC δ and θ, which recruit the
  cytosolic components p47phox and p67phox to the plasma membrane, allowing their
  combination with membrane components of NOX to form an active enzyme system to generate
  ROS. Then, ROS activate TACE by oxidizing the inhibitory prodomain to expose the
  catalytic domain. Next, the activated TACE via ROS cleaves pro-EGF-like factors
  into soluble EGFR ligands, which bind to and activate EGFR in an autocrine mode,
  thereby resulting in oocyte maturation.
