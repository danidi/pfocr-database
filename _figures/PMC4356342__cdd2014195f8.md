caption: Schematics show the promoting role of MT2 in axonogenesis and synaptic potentiation.
  Activation of MT2 by melatonin (MEL), IIK7 or MT2 overexpression can stimulate a
  direct association of MT2 with Akt, which in turn initiate Akt/GSK-3β/CRMP-2 signaling
  pathway, promote formation of multiple functional axons and potentiate synaptic
  transmission. On the contrary, inhibition of MT2 signaling by specific inhibitors
  such as 4P-PDOT and K185, antibody blockage, genetic knockout, or membranous permeable
  peptide suppresses the axonogenesis and synaptic transmission
