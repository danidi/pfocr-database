  involvement of HCV in the development of hepatitis, fibrosis, and cirrhosis. HCV

  inflammation, metabolic pathway deregulation leading to steatosis, oxidative stress,

  hepatic stellate cells, and attenuate immune cell response leading to immune evasion.

  evaluated (not limited to liver diseases) are highlighted in bold. HCV: hepatitis
  C virus, HCC: hepatocellular carcinoma, ECM: extracellular matrix.'
