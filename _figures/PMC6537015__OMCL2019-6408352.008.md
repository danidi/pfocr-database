  and cardiac fibrosis after MI injury. Signaling model for CMF transdifferentiation
  whereby MI injury upregulates TRPA1 expression, enhancing Ca2+ entry and leading
  to CaN activation, resulting in the translocation of NFAT to the nucleus to participate

  mediates NFAT transcription factors as likely regulators of CMF transdifferentiation-related
  gene expression.
