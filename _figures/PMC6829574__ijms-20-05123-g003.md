caption: The relative expression levels of the unigenes related to anthocyanin biosynthesis.
  (A) The biosynthesis pathway. Arrows show the metabolic stream, left or upward arrows
  represent the genes catalyzing the progress, the blue abbreviations express the
  genes found in the assembly unigenes, and the number under the genes represents
  the relative expression level in transgenic lines versus the WT. The numbers under
  the chemical compounds represent the relative contents of the compounds accumulated
  in the transgenic lines versus the WT. These structural anthocyanin biosynthesis
  genes were more significantly upregulated in transgenic lines than WT. (B) The relative
  transcript level of the genes relative to anthocyanin biosynthesis based on qPCR
  experiments.
