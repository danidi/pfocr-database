caption: Schematic representation of β-amyloid precursor protein (APP) processing.
  The vast majority of BACE1 cleavage of APP occurs into BACE1-containing acidic organelles
  via clathrin-dependent endocytosis. The subsequent γ-secretase activity determines
  the release of Aβ. Alternatively, APP can undergo a non-amyloidogenic pathway that
  involves the α-secretase ADAM10 and occurs largely on the plasma membrane and in
  the trans-Golgi network. Red arrow, exocytosis/endocytosis pathways; black arrows,
  enzymatic cleavage of APP.
