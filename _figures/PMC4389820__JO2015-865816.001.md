  for TGF-β signaling acts through the formation of a complex between Smad 2/3 and
  Smad 4. The complex then moves to the nucleus and stimulates the transcription of
  target genes. Sharp arrows denote activation/upregulation and blunt arrows denote
  inhibition/downregulation. Fz: frizzled receptors; Gli: glioma-associated oncogene
  family of transcription factors; GSK-3b: glycogen synthase kinase; Hh: hedgehog;
  PI3K: phosphatidylinositol-3-kinase; ILK: integrin-linked kinase; LRP: low-density
  lipoprotein receptor-related protein; p38 MAPK: mitogen-activated protein kinase;
  Ptc: patched receptor for Hh signaling; SMO: smoothened; TGF-β: transforming growth
  factor β; uPAR: urokinase plasminogen activator receptor.'
