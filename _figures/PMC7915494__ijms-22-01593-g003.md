caption: 'Proposed pathways of ABCA1 involvement in cancer. Downregulation of ABCA1
  promotes cell growth and proliferation. a: ABCA1 downregulation avoids raft domain
  disruption and Akt pathway activation; b: ABCA1 downregulation causes cholesterol
  accumulation in the mitochondrial membrane inhibiting cytochrome c release and apoptosome
  formation; and c: ABCA1 downregulation activates the IL3-receptor, activating Janus
  kinase 2 (JAK2) and mitogen-activated protein kinase (MAPK) pathways. Upregulation
  of ABCA1 stabilizes caveolin-1 promoting epithelial-mesenchymal transition, and
  thus cell migration an invasion.'
