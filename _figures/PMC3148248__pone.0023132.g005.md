caption: Both receptors partially share ligands and adaptor proteins. TLR4 transduces
  a signal to NFκB via TIRAP, MyD88 and IRAK4 and to IRF3 via TRAM and TRIF. RAGE
  phosphorylated by PKCζ upon ligand binding also uses TIRAP, MyD88 and IRAK4 for
  signal transduction to NFκB. Presence of yet unknown signaling pathway from RAGE
  is still possible. Simultaneous activation of both receptors resulted in enhanced
  activation of NFκB. The signaling machinery is also linked to Akt and caspase-8,
  and possibly Rac1.
