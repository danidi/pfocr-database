  breast cancer cells. Ivermectin treatment promotes ubiquitination-mediated degradation
  of PAK1, leading to inactivation of the AKT-MTOR signaling pathway. The inhibition
  of AKT-MTOR signaling subsequently induces the formation of the BECN1-containing
  PtdIns3 K complex and ultimately results in elevated autophagic flux, which represses
  the growth of breast cancer cells independent of apoptosis. Ub, ubiquitin.
