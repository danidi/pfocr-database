  pathway based upon the mammalian cAMP signalling cascade. (Redrawn from Matsuda
  & Kroth []). This model describes the signalling route under high CO2 conditions.

  protein phosphatase; CBP, CREB (cAMP binding protein) binding protein; DARPP, dopamine-
  and cAMP-regulated phosphoprotein. Parentheses indicate the number of candidate
  genes in the P. tricornutum genome. No functional analogue of DARPP has been identified
  so far.
