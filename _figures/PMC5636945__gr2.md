  modules for the pentose phosphate pathway (A), Non-Oxidative Glycolysis (NOG) (B),

  reactions of transketolase/transaldolase in the non-oxidative pentose phosphate
  pathway. Glu, glucose; G6P, glucose 6-phosphate; F6P, fructose 6-phosphate; FBP,
