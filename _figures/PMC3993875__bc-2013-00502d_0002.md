caption: Monitoring the kinetics of sialic acid biosynthesis in mammalian cell lines.
  (A) Sialic acid biosynthetic pathway. Metabolic conversion of Ac4ManNAl into a cell
  surface sialoside proceeds by the sequential action of nonspecific esterases (a),
  ManNAc 6-kinase (b), sialic acid 9-phosphate synthase (c), sialic acid 9-phosphatase
  (d), CMP-sialic acid synthetase (e), CMP-sialic acid Golgi transporter (f), and
  sialyltransferases (g). Monitoring the kinetics of sialic acid biosynthesis in Jurkat
  cells using (B) Ac4ManNAl (50 μM) and (C) SiaNAl (1.5 mM) as the metabolic precursor.
  (D) Monitoring the kinetics of sialic acid biosynthesis in CHO cells using SiaNAl
  (2 mM) as the metabolic precursor. Data shown in (D) were obtained using biotin
  azide 7.
