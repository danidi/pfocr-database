  in A498 cells. This scheme is described in the Discussion section.Notes: BK stimulates
  ERK through the B2 receptor-Gq/11pathway, which includes the activation of phospholipase

  the ERK-dependent proliferation of A498 cells. The dashed line indicates a putative
  pathway used by BK to activate NHE upstream of ERK.Abbreviations: BK, bradykinin;
  ERK, extracellular acidification rate; NHE, Na+/H+ exchange.'
