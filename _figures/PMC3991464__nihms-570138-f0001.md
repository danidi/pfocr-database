caption: Four pathways of androgen metabolism. Pathway 1 is the direct conversion
  of T into DHT through 5α-reduction (large dashed and dark large dashed lines). Pathway
  2 involves the formation of T using DHEA, which is converted into either androstenediol
  or androstenedione (faint gray long dashes). Pathway 3 involves T synthesis through
  the cholesterol pathway (small gray dashes). Pathway 4 is the backdoor pathway involving
  the generation of DHT using androstane3α-17β-diol instead of T as an intermediate
  (outlined in bold).
