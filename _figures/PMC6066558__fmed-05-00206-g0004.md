caption: 'Schematic representation of soluble klotho pleiotropic functions and FGF23
  – sKlotho interactions. Soluble klotho can modulate several signaling pathways by
  receptor or soluble factor biding. In relation to circulating FGF23, soluble klotho
  can bind it and act in three different ways: First, as decoy receptor leading to
  FGF23 inactivation or excretion; second, as a bona fide co-receptor, favoring FGF23
  biding to FGFR1c, and third, soluble klotho can also serve as a on demand co-receptor
  to FGFR1c on cardiomyocyte membrane, changing the signaling pathway through FGF23
  can act on myocardium. FGF23, Fibroblast growth factor 23; FGFR4, Type 4 fibroblast
  growth factor receptor; NFAT, Nuclear factor of activated T-cells; FGFR1c, Type
  1c fibroblast growth factor receptor; IGF1R, Insulin growth factor 1 receptor; TGFβRII,
  Type II transforming growth factor β.'
