caption: The left side of the model indicates components of the classic Wnt/Frizzled
  mediated activation of ß-catenin. In this pathway the disruption complex (Axin,
  APC) that enables GSK3ß- and Caseine kinase 1 (CK1)-mediated phosphorylation of
  ß-catenin leading to its degradation, is disrupted in a Dishevelled-mediated way
  upon Wnt binding to Frizzled/LRP. US28 activates ß-catenin signaling in a ligand-dependent
  and independent manner, involving respectively Gα12/13 and Gαq proteins and respective
  RhoGEFs, converging at RhoA/ROCK, resulting in increased Tcf-Lef transcriptional
  activation.
