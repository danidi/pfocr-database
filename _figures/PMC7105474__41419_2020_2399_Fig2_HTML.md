  IKKs or IκBα. If IKK is degraded, pro-tumoral NF-κB signaling is inhibited, while
  the autophagic degradation of IκBα causes NF-κB activation and tumor promotion.
  The autophagic targeting of core elements of NF-κB pathway involves specific cargo
  receptors, such as KEAP1, Ro52, Hsp90, SKP2, p62, and NRF2.
